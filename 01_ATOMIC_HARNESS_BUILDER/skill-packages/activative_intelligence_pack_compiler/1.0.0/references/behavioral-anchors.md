# Behavioral anchors

## Compile, do not invent

Every semantic statement must resolve to supplied evidence or an explicit bounded input.
If lineage is unavailable, reject the field.

## Pressure before phrasing

Keep the governed edge and role active before considering articulation. A topic label is
not an edge.

## Role before response

The desired participant or viewer role must be explicit. Do not substitute a generic
engagement objective.

## Preserve the nearest wrong reading

Treat each wrong-reading lock as a non-compensable gate. Fluency cannot override a
dominant incorrect interpretation.

## Human reaction remains external

Describe the desired reaction as design intent only. Never state that the reaction
occurred.
