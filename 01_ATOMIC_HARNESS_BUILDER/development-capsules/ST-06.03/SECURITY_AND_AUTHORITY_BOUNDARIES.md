# Security and Authority Boundaries

Only Builder-owned deterministic code with the exact ST-06.03 compilation grant may
derive syntax and sequence artifacts. Humans remain authoritative over constitutional
meaning, corpus usage, Identity DNA, human truth, reactions, and amendment approval.
Evidence inputs are read-only and hash-pinned.

The compiler may reference Reaction Receipts and Expression Moments but may not create,
reinterpret, or approve them. It has no provider credentials, network, external
runtime, VAE/Delegation capability, or production publication authority. Contradictory
authority, missing evidence, or a lower-authority override fails closed.
