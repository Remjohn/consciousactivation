# Security and Authority Boundaries

- Only deterministic Builder code with `TRANSITION_RUN` authority may generate the capsule.
- Human authority is limited to the standing synthetic demonstration campaign and does not confer production authorization.
- Constitution and Builder V1.2 references are immutable inputs; the Builder may reference but not fork or weaken them.
- The generated capsule may describe only the repository-owned synthetic Harness.
- No agent may widen scope, authorize production, claim certification, add external behavior, or substitute unresolved decisions.
- No network, database, external runtime, provider, VAE, Delegation runtime, Format 02, conversational product, API, UI, Control Tower, signing or publication behavior is permitted.
- Missing or unverifiable authority fails closed.
