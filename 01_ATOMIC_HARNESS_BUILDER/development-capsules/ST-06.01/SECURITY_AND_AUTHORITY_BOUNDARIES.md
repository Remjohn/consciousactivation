# Security and Authority Boundaries

- Constitution V1.1 and Builder PRD V1.2 precede local configuration.
- The canonical registry is referenced and hash-validated; code does not become an
  independent category authority.
- Human-owned Identity DNA, reaction, and amendment authority are immutable references.
- Builder owns compile/validate/receipt behavior only.
- VAE, Delegation, Interview, ReelCast, provider, and publication behavior remain external.
- No secrets, network calls, absolute paths, production credentials, or certification
  authority may enter this Story.
