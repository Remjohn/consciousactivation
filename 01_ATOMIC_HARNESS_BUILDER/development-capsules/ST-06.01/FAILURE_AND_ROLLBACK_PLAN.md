# Failure and Rollback Plan

All validation occurs before commit. Registry drift, invalid authority, incomplete
semantic lineage, unsupported/multiple categories, missing locks, conflicting commands,
and false maturity claims leave no binding, receipt, observation of success, or changed
portable artifact. Injected pre-commit failure proves zero partial state. Rollback is the
non-destructive removal of the additive ST-06.01 sources and restoration of the prior
manifest/portable-definition seam; historical receipts remain unchanged.
