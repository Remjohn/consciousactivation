# Security and authority boundaries

- The prior human atomic-boundary ratification is the only semantic authority accepted by this mode.
- Deterministic Builder code may validate, map, canonicalize, hash, persist, replay, and invalidate exact governed values. It may not invent, promote, broaden, merge, or split meaning.
- Agents, evaluators, external actors, and direct callers cannot write authoritative IR values or bypass upstream authority.
- The compiler receives immutable in-memory snapshots only. It has no network, secrets, model, external runtime, unrestricted filesystem, or source-write capability.
- `HarnessIR` owns product meaning. Workflow IR, workers, queues, retries, sandboxes, and deployment state are explicitly excluded.
- The five canonical categories remain unchanged. The synthetic proof has `category_binding: none_test_only` and is not a sixth category.
- Missing Activative/category fields remain individually governed `NOT_APPLICABLE`; they are never compressed into notes or inferred.
- Delegation and VAE schemas/runtime behavior remain externally owned. No RC4 dependency is required for this synthetic mode.
- Full Release 1, full-product, certification, production, publication, and external execution authorization remain false.
