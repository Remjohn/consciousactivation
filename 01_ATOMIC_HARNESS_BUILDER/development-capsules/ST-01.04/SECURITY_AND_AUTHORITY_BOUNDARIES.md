# Security and Authority Boundaries

Only registered deterministic Builder code may evaluate the synthetic contract.
Code may issue `PASS`, blocked results, or insufficient evidence; it may not waive
critical gaps, promote evidence authority, invent claims, or issue
`PASS_WITH_LIMITATIONS`. That outcome requires an explicit governed human waiver and
is contract-tested without implementing a waiver workflow.

Evidence is read-only. Missing or unverifiable authority, provenance, hashes or
current lineage fail closed. The Constitution and PRD are referenced, never copied,
forked or edited. Format 02, Human Reaction data, VAE, Delegation runtime, provider,
GPU, production and certification authority remain external or later-phase scopes.
