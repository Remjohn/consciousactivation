# Relevant Accepted ADRs

- `ADR-003` governs atomic state, command receipt, outbox, optimistic concurrency,
  immutable artifacts, replay and rollback.
- `ADR-007` governs versioned Source Profiles, exact evidence identity, gaps,
  authority conflicts and Saturation Contracts.
- `ADR-012` governs deny-by-default authority and read-only evidence boundaries.

The Story is additive. It does not revise an ADR, introduce a shared schema, select
a production database, or close `BD-004`, `HD-006`, or any external-product gate.
