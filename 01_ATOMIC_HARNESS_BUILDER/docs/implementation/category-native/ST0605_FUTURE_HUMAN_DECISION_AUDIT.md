# ST-06.05 Future Human-Decision Audit

Status: `READ_ONLY_AUDIT_COMPLETE_STORY_NOT_AUTHORIZED`

This audit does not close HD-006 or HD-007 and does not authorize ST-06.05.

## Existing authority

- HD-006 is `CATEGORY_CONDITIONAL`. It activates when a conversational branch
  collects, retains, redacts, exposes, withdraws, or stores Human Reaction material
  or Identity DNA amendment proposals. It does not apply to a purely structural,
  non-personal fixture, but it directly blocks executable ST-06.05 behavior.
- HD-007 is `PRODUCTION_CERTIFICATION_ONLY`. It activates for protected
  conversational evaluation and certification. Structural contracts may be prepared
  without inheriting certification, but ST-06.05 cannot claim evaluation maturity or
  certification while HD-007 is open.
- The Constitution defines Human Reaction as the human answer, correction, memory,
  example, stance, emotion, or story produced by an Activative Call. The Builder may
  preserve and validate evidence; it may not manufacture that reaction.
- An Expression Moment is a source-backed segment. It requires attributable source
  spans and, for conversational compilation, a governed Reaction Receipt reference.
- Identity DNA amendment proposals remain proposals until the human identity authority
  approves them. The Builder cannot merge them autonomously.

## Ownership boundary requiring confirmation

The future decision must confirm all of the following without transferring ownership
to the Builder:

1. The source human owns their original truth and reaction.
2. An authorized operator or capture service may record the reaction only under the
   ratified consent and withdrawal policy.
3. The Builder may compile immutable references, validate provenance, and route typed
   outcomes; it may not synthesize a Reaction Receipt or human landing.
4. A Reaction Receipt is valid only when it identifies the Activative Call, exact
   evidence spans, capture authority, consent-policy version, withdrawal state, and
   recompile disposition.
5. An Expression Moment is valid only when it retains its Reaction Receipt reference,
   source spans, observed or explicitly human-ratified landing status, routeability,
   and withdrawal state.
6. Identity DNA amendment proposals require human review; merge authority remains
   `human_identity_authority`.
7. Interview Expression and ReelCast live behavior remain externally owned pending
   their own product authority.

## Exact future HD-006 decision

The `product_lead_evidence_steward_privacy_authority` must issue one versioned,
hash-pinned Human Reaction policy that truthfully declares:

- lawful or otherwise authorized purpose and usage scope;
- consent capture and proof requirements;
- retention periods and deletion triggers;
- redaction and minimization rules;
- access roles and audit evidence;
- withdrawal, restriction, descendant invalidation, and reprocessing behavior;
- provenance and source-span requirements;
- storage and pre-approval handling of Identity DNA amendment proposals;
- profile applicability for `public_comment`, `reply_dm`, `reelcast_expression`, and
  `interview_expression`;
- the authority identities allowed to capture, inspect, restrict, withdraw, and
  approve each governed object.

Closure evidence must be the approved policy bytes, version, SHA-256, approval receipt,
and tests proving fail-closed behavior when consent, provenance, access, retention, or
withdrawal evidence is absent or stale.

## Exact future HD-007 decision

The `evaluation_governance_and_category_steward` must issue a separate versioned,
hash-pinned conversational evaluation policy that declares:

- protected conversational cases and custody model;
- non-compensable dimensions, including human-truth non-invention and wrong-reading
  resistance;
- development, shadow, and production thresholds without status inheritance;
- repeated-run and fresh-context policy;
- evaluator authority, disagreement, escalation, and amendment rules;
- certification scope independently for each conversational profile.

Closure evidence must include the policy and approval receipt, immutable protected-case
manifest, repeated-run results, evaluator provenance, and a certification receipt.
Development evidence alone is insufficient.
