## Artifact verdicts

- UNCHANGED: valid as-is or intentionally preserved as historical evidence.
- PATCH_REQUIRED: later bounded patch needed.
- NEW_ARTIFACT: later authority or contract artifact must be added.
- BLOCKED: patch cannot be finalized without a named decision or input.
- DEFERRED: intentionally outside the current release or batch.
