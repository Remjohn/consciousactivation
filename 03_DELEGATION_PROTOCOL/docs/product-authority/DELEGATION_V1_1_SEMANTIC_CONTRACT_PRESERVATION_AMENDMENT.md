# Delegation V1.1 Semantic Contract Preservation Amendment

**Amends:** `CMF_CONTENT_HARNESS_VISUAL_ASSET_EDITOR_DELEGATION_SHARDED_PRD_V1_1`  
**Aligned package:** `CMF_CONTENT_HARNESS_VISUAL_ASSET_EDITOR_DELEGATION_SHARDED_PRD_V1_1_1`  
**Authority:** CCP Activative Intelligence & Visual Narrative Constitution v1.1.0  
**Status:** Binding amendment for shared schemas, authority, compatibility, and conformance

## Purpose

The original protocol correctly protects ownership, immutability, lifecycle, compatibility, and audit. This amendment expands the semantic payload it must preserve so the protocol does not provide perfect governance around an incomplete contract.

## Constitution-complete authority domains

The field-level authority matrix now includes:

```text
activative_semantic_lineage
activation_contract
visual_semantic_pack
visual_narrative_program
feature_contracts
somatic_route_request
expression_moment_lineage
wrong_reading_locks
```

These fields are owned by the requesting Content Harness or its authoritative upstream compiled contracts. The protocol validates and preserves them. The VAE interprets and enforces them but may not rewrite them.

## Compatibility law

A compatibility verdict cannot be `COMPATIBLE` when a receiver parses a field but cannot enforce its behavior. Adapters and migrations may rename, nest, or normalize fields; they may not drop, weaken, synthesize, or flatten constitutional semantic objects.

Lossy handling of the following is prohibited:

```text
Identity DNA and Context Premise references
Matrix of Edging and Activative Intelligence Pack references
activation directions and roles
stance and identity urge
participation design and micro-commitment
recognition carrier
Visual Narrative attention states
feature contracts
wrong-reading locks
Expression Moment lineage
```

## Demand schema preservation

The canonical shared Visual Asset Demand schema now matches the VAE-aligned contract and requires non-empty wrong-reading locks plus the complete Activative visual context.

## Conformance requirements

The conformance suite must include:

1. rejection when an adapter drops an Expression Moment reference;
2. rejection when a receiver ignores required wrong-reading locks;
3. migration preserving the hash and version lineage of the Activative Intelligence Pack;
4. authority rejection when the VAE changes recognition intent, role, stance, or Visual Narrative Program;
5. compatibility failure when a declared evaluation profile cannot evaluate the requested activation or feature contracts.

## Affected base requirements

This release strengthens `FR-011` and the authority, compatibility, schema, and readiness registries without changing stable FR identifiers.

## Non-goals

- Moving creative selection into the protocol.
- Making the protocol compile Activative Intelligence.
- Giving the protocol authority to rank recognition carriers or visual narratives.
