# F28 — Activative Psychological Role, Archetype Coalition, and Final Script Authority

## 1. Product claim and user outcome

**User outcome:** Every derivative gives the viewer a clear psychological role inside a source-backed tension and reaches composition through an approved archetype-coalition Final Script.

This feature restores archetypes as execution-bearing psychological geometries and makes the Final Script the mandatory bridge between expression and composition.

The controlling Activative law is: **Content activates when it gives the viewer a psychological role inside a tension.** This feature must preserve that law from source interpretation through script, composition, render, evaluation, and repair.

## 2. Product boundary and ownership

The Atomic Harness Pipeline compiles and executes this behavior. Interview Expression supplies authenticated expression material. Builder supplies the category-native Harness. Studio supplies operator commands and approval. VAE supplies visual ingredients. None of these products may silently replace the approved archetype coalition, Primitive Coalition Contract, Final Script, or Activative Intelligence contracts.

## 3. Canonical objects and state

Core invariants:
- Every derivative declares a psychological role inside a tension.
- Matrix of Edging precedes archetype routing.
- Guest Voice DNA governs non-verbatim text.
- Final Script approval precedes every composition.

The feature emits immutable, versioned objects linked to source package, archetype route, Primitive Coalition Contract, Brand Context Version where applicable, Final Script, composition program, rendered result, and evaluation receipts.

## 6. Functional Requirements

### FR-163 — Declare the viewer psychological role inside a named tension

**Lifecycle:** `NEW_V1_2`  
**Normative requirement:** Every derivative job shall declare the psychological role offered to the viewer, the meaningful tension that makes the role available, the intended movement through that tension, and the evidence connecting the tension to the source.

**Trigger and preconditions:** An Expression Moment or source-backed premise is proposed for derivative routing.

**Required output or state transition:** A PsychologicalRoleInTension contract attached to the archetype-routing decision.

**Authority and invariants:**
- The role is specific enough to evaluate.
- The tension comes from source and Matrix of Edging evidence.
- A topic or emotion label alone is insufficient.

**Acceptance scenarios**

- **Primary success:** Given an Expression Moment about inherited silence, when routed, then the contract names the viewer role, such as witness, recognizer, challenger, or participant, and the tension the viewer enters.
- **Adversarial or denial case:** Given a generic “inspire the audience” intention, when validation runs, then routing is blocked because no psychological role or live tension is declared.
- **Evidence required:** Source spans, Matrix of Edging trace, role/tension evaluation, and operator approval receipt.

**Governing decisions:** D055, D056  
**Exact source references:** SRC-DOCT-001, SRC-INT-002, SRC-INT-003

### FR-164 — Compile broad signal, coalition signature, and Edge Product through Matrix of Edging

**Lifecycle:** `NEW_V1_2`  
**Normative requirement:** The system shall distinguish pre-expression broad primary signal from post-expression Edge Product formation and shall compile surviving primitive candidates into one measurable Coalition Signature.

**Trigger and preconditions:** Source expression is accepted and an archetype route is requested.

**Required output or state transition:** A MatrixOfEdgingResult containing broad signal lineage, candidate survival, coalition signature, Edge Product, and routeability.

**Authority and invariants:**
- Primitives are not edges.
- Unsupported cleverness does not survive.
- Coalition fatality remains visible.

**Acceptance scenarios**

- **Primary success:** Given a truth-rich source answer, when the Matrix pass runs, then broad pressure, surviving primitive candidates, coalition geometry, and the emergent Edge Product remain separately inspectable.
- **Adversarial or denial case:** Given an Edge Product invented directly from a topic label without primitive survival, when validation runs, then it is rejected.
- **Evidence required:** Candidate survival scores, coalition geometry, fatality cases, and routeability receipts.

**Governing decisions:** D055, D058, D066  
**Exact source references:** SRC-DOCT-001, SRC-DOCT-006, SRC-DOCT-009

### FR-165 — Compile an archetype coalition for every derivative

**Lifecycle:** `NEW_V1_2`  
**Normative requirement:** Each derivative shall bind a primary archetype, supporting archetype functions, asset-derivative route, category grammar, psychological geometry, and tension progression as one versioned ArchetypeCoalitionContract.

**Trigger and preconditions:** An Edge Product and content-batch job are accepted.

**Required output or state transition:** An ArchetypeCoalitionContract eligible for script compilation.

**Authority and invariants:**
- Archetype is a psychological geometry, not a theme label.
- Supporting archetypes cannot blur the primary role.
- Category and substrate remain explicit.

**Acceptance scenarios**

- **Primary success:** Given one Edge Product, when a Carousel and short-video route are created, then each receives a category-native archetype coalition sharing source force without sharing a flattened template.
- **Adversarial or denial case:** Given three attractive archetype labels with no primary geometry or sequence law, when validation runs, then the coalition is rejected.
- **Evidence required:** Archetype registry trace, coalition compatibility, category crosswalk, and CBAR audit.

**Governing decisions:** D055, D057, D066  
**Exact source references:** SRC-DOCT-002, SRC-DOCT-005, SRC-INT-003

### FR-166 — Compile text-bearing content through Guest Voice DNA

**Lifecycle:** `NEW_V1_2`  
**Normative requirement:** JIT Skill script writers and Composers shall write or rewrite non-verbatim text through the active Guest Voice DNA, source evidence, Edge Product, archetype coalition, and Distillation Layers; verbatim quotations shall remain marked as verbatim.

**Trigger and preconditions:** A derivative needs a headline, claim, slide copy, caption, overlay, voiceover, or animation line.

**Required output or state transition:** A VoiceDNAConstrainedScriptDraft with source and transformation lineage.

**Authority and invariants:**
- No anonymous generic brand voice.
- Rewriting preserves source truth and guest cadence.
- Verbatim and rewritten language are distinguishable.

**Acceptance scenarios**

- **Primary success:** Given a dense interview answer, when Carousel copy is compiled, then the slides sound like the guest while using category-native compression rather than copying raw transcript mechanically.
- **Adversarial or denial case:** Given polished copy that could belong to any creator, when Voice DNA evaluation runs, then the draft is rejected or repaired.
- **Evidence required:** Voice DNA version, source-span crosswalk, distillation trace, contrastive examples, and VoiceContinuity evaluation.

**Governing decisions:** D060, D061, D066  
**Exact source references:** SRC-DOCT-002, SRC-DOCT-003, SRC-DOCT-006

### FR-167 — Approve one archetype-coalition Final Script before composition

**Lifecycle:** `NEW_V1_2`  
**Normative requirement:** No Composition IR, VideoEditProgram, animation scene program, visual generation demand, or renderer workspace may be compiled until one versioned Final Script passes source fidelity, Voice DNA, archetype, primitive, Activative, and operator approval gates.

**Trigger and preconditions:** A script draft is proposed as the content substrate for a derivative.

**Required output or state transition:** An approved FinalScript record or a typed repair request.

**Authority and invariants:**
- Composition cannot conceal an unresolved script.
- Approval pins the exact source and coalition versions.
- Later revisions create a new script version.

**Acceptance scenarios**

- **Primary success:** Given a source-led short, when the Final Script is approved, then selected source words, overlay copy, scene roles, animation opportunities, and primitive obligations are fixed before timeline composition.
- **Adversarial or denial case:** Given a renderer-ready layout with no approved Final Script, when execution is requested, then composition is blocked.
- **Evidence required:** Final Script review, source-fidelity evaluation, primitive receipt, archetype receipt, and operator decision.

**Governing decisions:** D057, D060, D062  
**Exact source references:** SRC-DOCT-002, SRC-DOCT-004, SRC-INT-001

### FR-168 — Preserve role, tension, coalition, and Final Script lineage through every embodiment

**Lifecycle:** `NEW_V1_2`  
**Normative requirement:** Every content-bearing object and rendered artifact shall link to the PsychologicalRoleInTension contract, Matrix result, Primitive Coalition Contract, Archetype Coalition Contract, Final Script, source spans, and transformation history.

**Trigger and preconditions:** Any downstream composition, generation, edit, repair, or export is created.

**Required output or state transition:** Complete doctrine-to-artifact lineage and invalidation edges.

**Authority and invariants:**
- A renderer cannot erase upstream reason.
- A repair cannot change the role or tension silently.
- Cross-format reuse retains the originating coalition.

**Acceptance scenarios**

- **Primary success:** Given one animation scene reused as video B-roll and on a Carousel slide, when audited, then both uses resolve to the same source, Edge Product, primitive coalition, archetype coalition, and Final Script version.
- **Adversarial or denial case:** Given an asset copied into another derivative without coalition lineage, when validation runs, then the use is rejected.
- **Evidence required:** Lineage graph, selective invalidation tests, cross-format reuse receipts, and replay proof.

**Governing decisions:** D044, D057, D067  
**Exact source references:** SRC-DOCT-001, SRC-DOCT-002, SRC-DOCT-009


## 7. Execution, model, tool, and human responsibilities

JIT Skill writers and Composers may draft and rewrite only inside the active Voice DNA, archetype, primitive, and source contracts. Programmed Models are bounded compilers or evaluators, not sources of doctrine. The operator approves the Final Script and any new creative boundary. Renderers execute composition programs and cannot invent the recipe.

## 8. Validation and quality gates

Validation must prove source pressure, role clarity, Voice DNA, archetype geometry, primitive coalition, and Final Script integrity. Primitive coverage, misuse, coalition fatality, source fidelity, psychological-role clarity, edge integrity, negative-space integrity, Voice DNA, Visual DNA, category grammar, and the active Activative Intelligence contracts are separate evaluation dimensions.

## 9. Risks, mitigations, and stop conditions

| Risk | Concrete consequence | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Centroid flattening | The result is polished but carries no live tension or psychological role. | Enforce Matrix of Edging, coalition signature, Edge Product, CCV variation, and anti-centroid evaluation. |
| Primitive tokenism | Primitive IDs are attached after the creative decision rather than building it. | Require Primitive Bindings and Coalition Contract before Final Script and composition. |
| Archetype decoration | An archetype label is applied without its psychological geometry or sequence obligations. | Require an archetype coalition contract and approved Final Script. |
| Renderer-led meaning | The runtime chooses layout, motion, or imagery without the coalition recipe. | Renderer adapters consume only typed composition programs. |

**Stop conditions**

- No approved psychological role inside a named tension.
- No current Primitive Coalition Contract or archetype coalition.
- No approved Final Script before composition.
- Primitive misuse or coalition fatality exceeds the declared threshold.
- The output collapses source specificity, edge integrity, Voice DNA, Visual DNA, or category grammar.
