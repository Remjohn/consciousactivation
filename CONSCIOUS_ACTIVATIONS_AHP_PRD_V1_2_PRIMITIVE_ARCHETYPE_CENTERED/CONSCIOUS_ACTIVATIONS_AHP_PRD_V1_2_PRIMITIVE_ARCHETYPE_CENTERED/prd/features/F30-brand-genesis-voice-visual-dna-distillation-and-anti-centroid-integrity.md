# F30 — Brand Genesis, Voice/Visual DNA, Distillation Layers, and Anti-Centroid Integrity

## 1. Product claim and user outcome

**User outcome:** Brand Context, Voice DNA, Visual DNA, RSCS, CCV, SDA, SFL, Source Fidelity, Negative Space, and Edge Integrity remain active through the entire source-to-artifact chain.

This feature prevents the source-first system from becoming a generic repurposing pipeline by preserving the guest’s linguistic and visual organism while expanding it through controlled variation.

The controlling Activative law is: **Content activates when it gives the viewer a psychological role inside a tension.** This feature must preserve that law from source interpretation through script, composition, render, evaluation, and repair.

## 2. Product boundary and ownership

The Atomic Harness Pipeline compiles and executes this behavior. Interview Expression supplies authenticated expression material. Builder supplies the category-native Harness. Studio supplies operator commands and approval. VAE supplies visual ingredients. None of these products may silently replace the approved archetype coalition, Primitive Coalition Contract, Final Script, or Activative Intelligence contracts.

## 3. Canonical objects and state

Core invariants:
- Brand Context Version is explicit and versioned.
- Voice DNA and Visual DNA are separate contracts.
- RSCS, CCV, and CBAR remain orthogonal engines.
- Source Fidelity, Negative Space, and Anti-Centroid integrity are measured at every handoff.

The feature emits immutable, versioned objects linked to source package, archetype route, Primitive Coalition Contract, Brand Context Version where applicable, Final Script, composition program, rendered result, and evaluation receipts.

## 6. Functional Requirements

### FR-175 — Load Brand Genesis Session and active Brand Context Version

**Lifecycle:** `NEW_V1_2`  
**Normative requirement:** A brand-bound campaign shall resolve the accepted Brand Genesis Session outputs and one active Brand Context Version before script, visual research, primitive selection, or composition.

**Trigger and preconditions:** A campaign or derivative is associated with a brand workspace.

**Required output or state transition:** A pinned BrandContextBinding available to JIT Capsules.

**Authority and invariants:**
- Brand context is versioned.
- Historical versions remain replayable.
- Brand context cannot override source truth or Activative Intelligence.

**Acceptance scenarios**

- **Primary success:** Given a campaign for one guest brand, when composition begins, then the active Brand Context Version is loaded with its identity, visual, voice, micro-semiotic, and negative-space context.
- **Adversarial or denial case:** Given two active Brand Context Versions with unresolved precedence, when a job launches, then it is blocked pending operator selection.
- **Evidence required:** Brand version resolution, JIT inclusion receipt, replay, and supersession tests.

**Governing decisions:** D061  
**Exact source references:** SRC-DOCT-003, SRC-INT-001

### FR-176 — Apply Voice DNA and Visual DNA as separate composition contracts

**Lifecycle:** `NEW_V1_2`  
**Normative requirement:** Voice DNA shall constrain linguistic cadence, syntax, pressure, intimacy, and anti-draft behavior; Visual DNA shall constrain identity geometry, visual constitution, material language, color, typography, texture, motion, and visual Negative Space.

**Trigger and preconditions:** A text-bearing or visual derivative is drafted.

**Required output or state transition:** VoiceDNAContract and VisualDNAContract bindings with evaluations.

**Authority and invariants:**
- Voice DNA is not a generic tone adjective.
- Visual DNA is not a moodboard label.
- Source fidelity remains upstream of both.

**Acceptance scenarios**

- **Primary success:** Given a guest with a reflective but confrontational voice pattern and tactile visual language, when content is composed, then text and visuals preserve those separate constraints.
- **Adversarial or denial case:** Given a polished output using generic motivational copy or generic premium visual style, when DNA evaluation runs, then it is rejected or repaired.
- **Evidence required:** Contrastive Voice/Visual DNA fixtures, version pins, composition checks, and operator labels.

**Governing decisions:** D060, D061, D065  
**Exact source references:** SRC-DOCT-002, SRC-DOCT-003, SRC-DOCT-004

### FR-177 — Apply RSCS Distillation Layers before script approval

**Lifecycle:** `NEW_V1_2`  
**Normative requirement:** The JIT script pipeline shall apply explicit Distillation Layers implementing RSCS saturation, collision detection, compression, evaluation, and recursion so that source density survives reduction into category-native copy and scene programs.

**Trigger and preconditions:** Source expression is condensed or rewritten into a derivative script.

**Required output or state transition:** A DistillationTrace linking source saturation, collisions, compressed claims, rejected generic drafts, and recursive revisions.

**Authority and invariants:**
- Saturation precedes compression.
- Meaning emerges through source-backed collision.
- Compression cannot invent missing signal.

**Acceptance scenarios**

- **Primary success:** Given a long interview answer, when distilled into six Carousel slides, then each compression step preserves irreducible signal and identifies the tension or recognition collision it carries.
- **Adversarial or denial case:** Given a clean summary that removes all specificity and costly exposure, when RSCS evaluation runs, then it fails signal-density and reality-contact gates.
- **Evidence required:** Distillation traces, density benchmark, source-contact evaluation, and rejected centroid drafts.

**Governing decisions:** D056, D060, D066  
**Exact source references:** SRC-DOCT-006, SRC-DOCT-001, SRC-INT-001

### FR-178 — Use CCV for controlled archetype and composition variation

**Lifecycle:** `NEW_V1_2`  
**Normative requirement:** Candidate generation shall vary explicit archetype, emotional register, Voice DNA register, intensity, audience maturity, representation geometry, and category-compatible composition axes while remaining anchored to RSCS-compressed truth and CBAR constraints.

**Trigger and preconditions:** The Harness requests multiple valid candidates or a novel category-native expression.

**Required output or state transition:** A CCVVariationPlan and diverse candidate portfolio.

**Authority and invariants:**
- Variation axes are explicit and separable.
- Novelty does not erase source truth.
- Candidates differ meaningfully, not only by seed.

**Acceptance scenarios**

- **Primary success:** Given one Edge Product, when three SuperVisual candidates are requested, then they occupy distinct controlled coordinates while preserving the same source and coalition.
- **Adversarial or denial case:** Given three near-identical candidates that drift toward the same generic social aesthetic, when diversity evaluation runs, then the portfolio fails.
- **Evidence required:** Axis coordinates, diversity measures, source-fidelity checks, and comparative evaluation.

**Governing decisions:** D065, D066  
**Exact source references:** SRC-DOCT-005, SRC-DOCT-006, SRC-SPEC-001

### FR-179 — Apply SDA and SFL crosswalks to semantic geometry and delivery function

**Lifecycle:** `NEW_V1_2`  
**Normative requirement:** The Pipeline shall resolve current SDA invariants, representation and archetypal geometries, plus SFL function, compression, surface, and failure-corpus crosswalks for the active archetype, primitive coalition, category, and output surface.

**Trigger and preconditions:** A Final Script or composition program is compiled.

**Required output or state transition:** An SDA/SFLContextPacket and evaluation obligations.

**Authority and invariants:**
- Semantic direction and delivery function are distinct.
- Surface adaptation cannot corrupt invariant geometry.
- Known SFL failure patterns become hard negatives.

**Acceptance scenarios**

- **Primary success:** Given a source-backed claim moving from video to Carousel, when crosswalks compile, then the invariant meaning remains stable while delivery function and compression adapt to the surface.
- **Adversarial or denial case:** Given a surface adaptation that preserves words but reverses the archetypal geometry or overresolves meaning, when evaluated, then it fails.
- **Evidence required:** SDA ontology crosswalk, SFL profile, failure-corpus tests, and surface comparison receipts.

**Governing decisions:** D056, D059, D066  
**Exact source references:** SRC-DOCT-007, SRC-DOCT-008, SRC-DOCT-003

### FR-180 — Enforce Source Fidelity, Negative Space, and Edge Integrity across all handoffs

**Lifecycle:** `NEW_V1_2`  
**Normative requirement:** Every script, composition, edit, generation, render, and repair shall measure source fidelity, linguistic and visual Negative Space, focal and peripheral Edge Integrity, anti-centroid charge preservation, and the survival of the intended viewer role inside tension.

**Trigger and preconditions:** Any content-bearing state crosses a workflow handoff or is proposed for acceptance.

**Required output or state transition:** A DoctrineIntegrityReceipt with per-layer verdicts and repair ownership.

**Authority and invariants:**
- Centroid polish cannot compensate for lost force.
- Negative Space is active structure, not unused area.
- Edge Integrity survives source-to-script-to-composition-to-render.

**Acceptance scenarios**

- **Primary success:** Given a short-video edit, when evaluated, then the system proves that source specificity, reaction tension, visual asymmetry, peripheral cues, and the viewer’s role survived the final render.
- **Adversarial or denial case:** Given a technically correct and visually polished artifact that neutralizes the Edge Product, when doctrine evaluation runs, then acceptance is denied and the responsible handoff is identified.
- **Evidence required:** Cross-handoff doctrine receipts, anti-centroid benchmarks, negative-space metrics, Visual Syntax reparse, and operator calibration.

**Governing decisions:** D055, D056, D065, D066  
**Exact source references:** SRC-DOCT-001, SRC-DOCT-003, SRC-DOCT-004, SRC-DOCT-006


## 7. Execution, model, tool, and human responsibilities

JIT Skill writers and Composers may draft and rewrite only inside the active Voice DNA, archetype, primitive, and source contracts. Programmed Models are bounded compilers or evaluators, not sources of doctrine. The operator approves the Final Script and any new creative boundary. Renderers execute composition programs and cannot invent the recipe.

## 8. Validation and quality gates

Validation must compare source, script, composition, and render states rather than evaluating only the final surface. Primitive coverage, misuse, coalition fatality, source fidelity, psychological-role clarity, edge integrity, negative-space integrity, Voice DNA, Visual DNA, category grammar, and the active Activative Intelligence contracts are separate evaluation dimensions.

## 9. Risks, mitigations, and stop conditions

| Risk | Concrete consequence | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Centroid flattening | The result is polished but carries no live tension or psychological role. | Enforce Matrix of Edging, coalition signature, Edge Product, CCV variation, and anti-centroid evaluation. |
| Primitive tokenism | Primitive IDs are attached after the creative decision rather than building it. | Require Primitive Bindings and Coalition Contract before Final Script and composition. |
| Archetype decoration | An archetype label is applied without its psychological geometry or sequence obligations. | Require an archetype coalition contract and approved Final Script. |
| Renderer-led meaning | The runtime chooses layout, motion, or imagery without the coalition recipe. | Renderer adapters consume only typed composition programs. |

**Stop conditions**

- No approved psychological role inside a named tension.
- No current Primitive Coalition Contract or archetype coalition.
- No approved Final Script before composition.
- Primitive misuse or coalition fatality exceeds the declared threshold.
- The output collapses source specificity, edge integrity, Voice DNA, Visual DNA, or category grammar.
