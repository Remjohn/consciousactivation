# F29 — Primitive-First Coalition Composition and Evaluation

## 1. Product claim and user outcome

**User outcome:** Primitive Families and Bindings build the real creative recipe, coalitions organize force, and evaluations measure primitive coverage, misuse, fatality, and Edge Product survival.

This feature makes primitives upstream recipe builders rather than decorative tags and positions Steering Recipes as evidence-backed execution strategies derived from the coalition.

The controlling Activative law is: **Content activates when it gives the viewer a psychological role inside a tension.** This feature must preserve that law from source interpretation through script, composition, render, evaluation, and repair.

## 2. Product boundary and ownership

The Atomic Harness Pipeline compiles and executes this behavior. Interview Expression supplies authenticated expression material. Builder supplies the category-native Harness. Studio supplies operator commands and approval. VAE supplies visual ingredients. None of these products may silently replace the approved archetype coalition, Primitive Coalition Contract, Final Script, or Activative Intelligence contracts.

## 3. Canonical objects and state

Core invariants:
- Meaning and Experience Primitive planes remain distinct.
- Primitive Bindings precede coalition and recipe selection.
- Coalition Signature and Edge Product are measurable objects.
- Primitive Evaluation Receipts are mandatory for content-bearing outputs.

The feature emits immutable, versioned objects linked to source package, archetype route, Primitive Coalition Contract, Brand Context Version where applicable, Final Script, composition program, rendered result, and evaluation receipts.

## 6. Functional Requirements

### FR-169 — Query current Meaning and Experience Primitive Families

**Lifecycle:** `NEW_V1_2`  
**Normative requirement:** Before script or composition strategy is selected, the Pipeline shall query eligible Meaning and Experience Primitive Families using source evidence, psychological role, tension, archetype, category, Brand Context Version, and execution stage.

**Trigger and preconditions:** An accepted Edge Product enters derivative planning.

**Required output or state transition:** A bounded primitive candidate field with activation, suppression, synergy, conflict, and misuse metadata.

**Authority and invariants:**
- Primitive retrieval precedes recipe selection.
- Meaning and Experience planes remain distinct.
- Primitive IDs are current and versioned.

**Acceptance scenarios**

- **Primary success:** Given a SuperVisual concept, when primitives are queried, then meaning-transform, delivery-shape, and format-material candidates are returned with reasons and conflicts.
- **Adversarial or denial case:** Given a preferred layout template, when it attempts to backfill primitive labels after selection, then the route is rejected.
- **Evidence required:** Primitive registry queries, eligibility results, exclusions, and source-to-primitive rationale.

**Governing decisions:** D056, D058, D059  
**Exact source references:** SRC-DOCT-007, SRC-DOCT-008, SRC-DOCT-009

### FR-170 — Create explicit Primitive Bindings

**Lifecycle:** `NEW_V1_2`  
**Normative requirement:** Every selected primitive shall be bound with plane, family, role, activation reason, source evidence, suppression conditions, misuse risks, and evaluation targets.

**Trigger and preconditions:** Primitive candidates survive selection for one derivative.

**Required output or state transition:** Versioned PrimitiveBinding records.

**Authority and invariants:**
- A primitive label without activation reason is invalid.
- Misuse and conflict metadata travel with the binding.
- Required bindings reference evidence.

**Acceptance scenarios**

- **Primary success:** Given an Irony Inversion primitive, when bound, then its source contradiction, intended role, suppression conditions, and misuse evaluation are explicit.
- **Adversarial or denial case:** Given a primitive chosen because it is fashionable or frequently used, when no source or coalition reason exists, then binding fails.
- **Evidence required:** Binding validation, source evidence, suppression tests, and primitive-role coverage.

**Governing decisions:** D058, D059  
**Exact source references:** SRC-DOCT-007, SRC-DOCT-008, SRC-DOCT-009

### FR-171 — Compile the Primitive Coalition Contract before recipe selection

**Lifecycle:** `NEW_V1_2`  
**Normative requirement:** Primary, supporting, suppression, and conflict bindings shall compile into one PrimitiveCoalitionContract with coalition intent, signature, coverage threshold, misuse threshold, source context, and content hash before Steering Recipes or composition methods are selected.

**Trigger and preconditions:** Primitive Bindings are accepted for a derivative.

**Required output or state transition:** An immutable PrimitiveCoalitionContract.

**Authority and invariants:**
- Coalition precedes Steering Recipe.
- Coalition intent preserves psychological role and Edge Product.
- Conflicts have explicit resolution.

**Acceptance scenarios**

- **Primary success:** Given a Carousel route, when the coalition compiles, then the exact meaning transform, delivery force, progression function, format material, and suppression bindings are fixed before slide composition.
- **Adversarial or denial case:** Given a Steering Recipe that has no compatible Primitive Coalition Contract, when retrieval returns it, then it is excluded.
- **Evidence required:** Coalition hash, coverage checks, conflict tests, recipe-compatibility tests, and operator inspection.

**Governing decisions:** D058, D059, D062  
**Exact source references:** SRC-DOCT-001, SRC-DOCT-009, SRC-CUR-020

### FR-172 — Emit Coalition Signature and Edge Product as measurable execution objects

**Lifecycle:** `NEW_V1_2`  
**Normative requirement:** The coalition compiler shall emit the weighted family/candidate geometry, synergy, redundancy, routeability, and resulting Edge Product as separate measurable objects available to scripts, composition, evaluation, and learning.

**Trigger and preconditions:** A PrimitiveCoalitionContract is finalized.

**Required output or state transition:** CoalitionSignature and EdgeProduct records.

**Authority and invariants:**
- Edge Product is emergent, not a renamed primitive.
- Redundancy and fatality remain measurable.
- Routeability is category-specific.

**Acceptance scenarios**

- **Primary success:** Given multiple surviving primitives, when the signature forms, then the system can explain which geometry produces recognition, humor exposure, mechanism reveal, or another Edge Product.
- **Adversarial or denial case:** Given a coalition with high local charge but no routeable category expression, when evaluated, then coalition fatality is recorded rather than hidden.
- **Evidence required:** Signature vectors, routeability scores, fatality fixtures, and category crosswalks.

**Governing decisions:** D055, D058, D066  
**Exact source references:** SRC-DOCT-001, SRC-DOCT-005, SRC-DOCT-009

### FR-173 — Build Steering Recipes from primitive coalitions

**Lifecycle:** `NEW_V1_2`  
**Normative requirement:** Steering Recipes shall be compiled or retrieved as execution strategies for a specific Primitive Coalition Contract, archetype coalition, composition state, embodiment, and known failure; they shall never act as the upstream source of the creative recipe.

**Trigger and preconditions:** A coalition and Final Script are approved and composition planning begins.

**Required output or state transition:** An eligible Steering Recipe set linked to coalition and embodiment.

**Authority and invariants:**
- Primitives build the recipe.
- Steering Recipes operationalize and repair it.
- Applicability and contradictory precedents are explicit.

**Acceptance scenarios**

- **Primary success:** Given a weak identity-anchor composition, when recipes are retrieved, then only strategies compatible with its primitive coalition, archetype, Visual DNA, and current geometry are considered.
- **Adversarial or denial case:** Given a visually similar recipe that would suppress the coalition’s recognition edge, when ranking runs, then it is excluded or returned as a contradiction.
- **Evidence required:** Coalition-to-recipe crosswalk, hard negatives, retrieval receipt, and downstream decision lift.

**Governing decisions:** D058, D059  
**Exact source references:** SRC-CUR-020, SRC-DOCT-009, SRC-EXT-027

### FR-174 — Emit Primitive Evaluation Receipts and misuse/fatality diagnosis

**Lifecycle:** `NEW_V1_2`  
**Normative requirement:** Each script, composition, provider plan, render, sequence, and repair shall receive a Primitive Evaluation Receipt measuring required primitive coverage, misuse risk, coalition integrity, Edge Product survival, and responsible repair layer.

**Trigger and preconditions:** A content-bearing candidate or final artifact is evaluated.

**Required output or state transition:** PrimitiveEvaluationReceipt plus pass, repair-required, or blocked decision.

**Authority and invariants:**
- Producer cannot approve itself.
- Coverage does not hide misuse.
- Repair preserves already-valid primitive relationships.

**Acceptance scenarios**

- **Primary success:** Given a polished SuperVisual with weak delivery-shape coverage, when evaluated, then the receipt identifies the missing role and a bounded repair target.
- **Adversarial or denial case:** Given high aesthetic scores but collapsed edge integrity or excess primitive misuse risk, when final evaluation runs, then the artifact cannot pass.
- **Evidence required:** Primitive coverage benchmark, misuse corpus, coalition fatality cases, independent evaluation, and repair replay.

**Governing decisions:** D059, D065, D066  
**Exact source references:** SRC-DOCT-009, SRC-DOCT-007, SRC-DOCT-008


## 7. Execution, model, tool, and human responsibilities

JIT Skill writers and Composers may draft and rewrite only inside the active Voice DNA, archetype, primitive, and source contracts. Programmed Models are bounded compilers or evaluators, not sources of doctrine. The operator approves the Final Script and any new creative boundary. Renderers execute composition programs and cannot invent the recipe.

## 8. Validation and quality gates

Validation must begin from primitive-specific activation, suppression, misuse, conflict, coverage, and coalition-fatality evidence. Primitive coverage, misuse, coalition fatality, source fidelity, psychological-role clarity, edge integrity, negative-space integrity, Voice DNA, Visual DNA, category grammar, and the active Activative Intelligence contracts are separate evaluation dimensions.

## 9. Risks, mitigations, and stop conditions

| Risk | Concrete consequence | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Centroid flattening | The result is polished but carries no live tension or psychological role. | Enforce Matrix of Edging, coalition signature, Edge Product, CCV variation, and anti-centroid evaluation. |
| Primitive tokenism | Primitive IDs are attached after the creative decision rather than building it. | Require Primitive Bindings and Coalition Contract before Final Script and composition. |
| Archetype decoration | An archetype label is applied without its psychological geometry or sequence obligations. | Require an archetype coalition contract and approved Final Script. |
| Renderer-led meaning | The runtime chooses layout, motion, or imagery without the coalition recipe. | Renderer adapters consume only typed composition programs. |

**Stop conditions**

- No approved psychological role inside a named tension.
- No current Primitive Coalition Contract or archetype coalition.
- No approved Final Script before composition.
- Primitive misuse or coalition fatality exceeds the declared threshold.
- The output collapses source specificity, edge integrity, Voice DNA, Visual DNA, or category grammar.
