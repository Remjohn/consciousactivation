# PRD V1.0 → V1.1 Change Summary

## Executive change

V1.0 described a capable brownfield Pipeline, static/temporal runtimes, retrieval, Programmed Models, evaluation, and Studio workbenches, but it did not yet make the interview source package the root of the whole production system. V1.1 corrects that.

## Major product changes

1. **Canonical source root**  
   The interview video, transcript, speakers, timestamps, tags, shots, keyframes, Expression Moments, source authority, and provenance become one Canonical Interview Source Package.

2. **Dual source admission**  
   Activative Interview Brief-led sessions and imported interview plus transcript are equal production paths after truthful validation.

3. **Tag and expression governance**  
   Planned, observed, inferred, operator-confirmed, rejected, and superseded states are distinct. Hunters propose; Analysts verify; approval is explicit.

4. **Content-batch orchestration**  
   One source package compiles into eligible archetype-routed Atomic Harness jobs for short video, Carousel, SuperVisual, and separately authorized animation derivatives.

5. **Talking-head A-roll rule**  
   Interview-derived source-led shorts preserve original footage and source audio as the base timeline.

6. **Canonical VideoEditProgram**  
   The predecessor edit-decision and timeline contracts are adopted and extended. The UI is a projection and typed command surface rather than canonical state.

7. **External-project capability extraction**  
   OpenMontage, video-use, Remotion, HyperFrames, OmniShotCut, OpenVideo, lineCode, AI YouTube Shorts Generator, CutClaw, Stretchy Studio, OKF, embeddings, and GNM contribute bounded patterns under review.

8. **Human resolution and Revision Compiler**  
   Natural-language and direct UI changes compile into minimal typed commands. Every meaningful decision becomes a HumanResolutionEpisode.

9. **VAE/GNM ownership correction**  
   SAM3, Lucida, layered providers, ComfyUI, and GNM remain VAE-owned. GNM provides geometry controls, not human identity or emotion truth.

10. **Format 02 deferral**  
    Historical registries and prototypes remain evidence, but there is no active runtime, Studio surface, model claim, benchmark, or release path until the current Harness gate passes.

11. **Release 1 correction**  
    Release 1 now proves one real interview source package, one source-led short, one source-grounded Carousel or SuperVisual, evaluation/repair, one human resolution, replay, and truthful evidence.
