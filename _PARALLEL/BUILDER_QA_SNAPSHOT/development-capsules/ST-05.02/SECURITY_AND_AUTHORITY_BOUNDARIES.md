# Security and Authority Boundaries

Deterministic Builder code may evaluate the exact synthetic necessity input, validate existing ownership, and emit the no-skill decision and receipt. It may not modify the registry, policy, snapshot, context, Constitution, PRD, Story inventory, or external contracts.

Humans retain authority for a new canonical capability, material adaptation, and acceptance of any Skill Design Brief. Independent evaluators retain maturity authority. Neither authority is invoked by the no-gap synthetic outcome.

Dynamic discovery, undeclared tools, secrets, network grants, prompt-based authority escalation, hidden orchestration, and implicit Builder-code ownership fail closed. A model or agent may not select dependencies or convert workflow responsibility into a skill.

VAE and Delegation behavior remain externally owned. The decision is non-production, non-certified, and cannot authorize execution, publication, or later Stories.
