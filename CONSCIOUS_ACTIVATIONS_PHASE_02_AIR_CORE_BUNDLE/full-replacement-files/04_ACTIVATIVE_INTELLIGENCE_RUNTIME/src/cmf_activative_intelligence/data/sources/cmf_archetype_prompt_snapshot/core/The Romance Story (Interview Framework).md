### <a id="_nh1vupj2in0a"></a>__✨ The Romance Story \(Interview Framework\)__

__Storage Table__: archetype\_prompts\_library __Prompt ID__: the\_romance\_story\_interview __Purpose__: To generate a completely customized interview guide\. This engine synthesizes research to create unique questioning sequences designed to extract a coach's most powerful story about the emotional journey of finding authentic romantic connection\.

__PRIMING & REFERENCE LIBRARY__ Stolen glances, hearts flutter, love blossoms, the first kiss, the spark ignites, the passion burns, the love story unfolds, the journey of two souls, the dance of intimacy, the power of connection, the magic of love, the fairytale ending, the happily ever after, authentic love, raw vulnerability\.

__RESEARCH SYNTHESIS PROTOCOL__ You will analyze the variables to create completely customized interview questions:

- __Content Idea Analysis__: Break down the specific type of romance in \{content\_idea\} \(e\.g\., finding a partner, deepening a connection, healing from heartbreak\)\.
- __Audience Psychology Mapping__: Use \{context\_premise\} to understand the audience's emotional landscape regarding love, vulnerability, and intimacy\.
- __Reference Point Mining__: Extract 2\-3 compelling examples from \{deep\_research\} of powerful stories about authentic love and connection\.
- __Current Relevance__: Find timely angles from \{fresh\_research\} that make this story of romance feel urgent and relevant in the modern dating world\.

__VARIABLES & DYNAMIC ELEMENTS__

\{content\_idea\}: The specific romance theme to explore

\{context\_premise\}: The audience's psychological profile \(fears, dreams, frustrations\)

\{deep\_research\}: Strategic intelligence and compelling examples

\{fresh\_research\}: Current trends and urgent context

\{coach\_background\}: Coach's expertise and journey

\{stream\_of\_consciousness\}: Your authentic voice and perspective

__DYNAMIC QUESTION GENERATION INSTRUCTIONS__

### <a id="_9twkuuwf87we"></a>__STEP 1: CONTENT IDEA ANALYSIS__

First, analyze \{content\_idea\} to identify:

- __The Core Romance Type__: What is the nature of the story? \(New love, long\-term partnership, self\-love, navigating conflict\)\.
- __The Emotional Stakes__: What was the cost of the old approach to love? What did authentic connection make possible?
- __The Common Misconceptions__: What "fairytale" lies or societal scripts about romance needed to be unlearned?
- __The Central Tension__: What was the primary internal or external obstacle to intimacy?

### <a id="_mujnsnefd1qi"></a>__STEP 2: CONTEXT PREMISE INTEGRATION__

Using \{context\_premise\}, identify:

- __Audience's Romance Anxiety__: What specific fears do they have about rejection, vulnerability, or being truly seen?
- __Their "Love" Distortions__: What unhealthy or unrealistic beliefs do they hold about what a perfect partner or relationship should be?
- __Their Intimacy Barriers__: What is preventing them from forming the deep, authentic connections they desire?
- __Their Connection Vision__: What does the feeling of true, soulful love they are dreaming of look and feel like?

### <a id="_x7wl1oy2eyt4"></a>__STEP 3: RESEARCH\-POWERED REFERENCE POINTS__

From \{deep\_research\}, select 2\-3 examples that:

- Mirror the specific romance theme in \{content\_idea\}\.
- Connect to the audience's psychological profile from \{context\_premise\}\.
- Provide surprising or counter\-intuitive insights about the nature of love and vulnerability\.

### <a id="_xabgioawil7w"></a>__STEP 4: GENERATE CUSTOM INTERVIEW SEQUENCE__

Now create a completely customized interview framework:

#### <a id="_h148kscnntx3"></a>__PHASE 1: DYNAMIC CONTEXT SETTING__

Create an opening that weaves together the theme, the audience's feelings, and a compelling reference\. __Template__: "I want to explore the reality of authentic love around \[specific aspect of content\_idea\] with you\. Your audience often feels \[specific struggle from context\_premise\] when it comes to relationships\. It's so relevant because \[current trend from fresh\_research\] is changing how we connect\. I was just reading about \[specific example from deep\_research\], and it made me think\.\.\." Then generate 2\-3 __customized permission questions__ that feel natural and create a safe space for discussing the heart\.

#### <a id="_ee629dfqvok4"></a>__PHASE 2: THE "BEFORE" \- DISCONNECTION ARCHAEOLOGY__

Generate 4\-5 questions focused on the state *before* the romantic breakthrough:

- Questions that uncover their personal experience with the specific feeling of disconnection or flawed romance related to \{content\_idea\}\.
- Questions that reveal the deep emotional cost of that inauthentic approach\.
- Questions that connect their "before" state to the audience's current fears from \{context\_premise\}\. Include 1\-2 __pattern\-matching probes__ using your selected references from \{deep\_research\}\.

#### <a id="_yyjsbr54ck4b"></a>__PHASE 3: THE STRUGGLE \- "FLAWED SCRIPT" ARCHAEOLOGY__

Create 5\-6 questions that:

- Explore the specific, conventional "scripts" they followed for romance, which ultimately failed\.
- Mirror the audience's own "noble failure" patterns of trying to be the "perfect" partner from \{context\_premise\}\.
- Reference why these romantic scripts are so prevalent now \(using \{fresh\_research\}\)\.
- Uncover the moment they realized that following the rules was preventing true intimacy\.

#### <a id="_phbxe794rdv9"></a>__PHASE 4: THE REVELATION \- "VULNERABILITY" ARCHAEOLOGY__

Design 4\-5 questions that:

- Seek to capture the sensory and emotional details of the moment they chose authentic vulnerability over performance\.
- Challenge common misconceptions about what a true romantic breakthrough feels like\.
- Connect to the audience's breakthrough barriers from \{context\_premise\}\.

#### <a id="_lyfsp454tpch"></a>__PHASE 5: THE "AFTER" \- CONNECTION ARCHAEOLOGY__

Create 4\-5 questions that:

- Explore the specific, tangible ways their relationship and internal state are different now\.
- Connect to what the audience truly desires from a feeling of love, based on \{context\_premise\}\.
- Uncover the unexpected emotional reality of what it feels like to be in a truly authentic partnership\.

#### <a id="_88v0sdf52ne0"></a>__PHASE 6: WISDOM EXTRACTION \- ACTIONABLE INSIGHTS__

Generate 3\-4 questions that:

- Distill the core lesson learned about the nature of real, sustainable love\.
- Address the audience's immediate next steps for cultivating more authenticity in their own relationships\.
- Provide hope for their own journey to find connection\.

__OUTPUT REQUIREMENTS__

### <a id="_pk56di8qaz0h"></a>__1\. CUSTOMIZED INTERVIEW GUIDE__

- __Content\-Specific Opening Frame__ \(unique to \{content\_idea\}\)
- __Dynamic Permission Questions__ \(2\-3 options tailored to the theme\)
- __Phase\-by\-Phase Question Sets__ \(completely customized to the content and audience\)
- __Strategic Reference Integration__ \(natural weaving of \{deep\_research\} examples\)

### <a id="_95tzo4jl0scx"></a>__2\. INTERVIEW INTELLIGENCE BRIEF__

- __Content Theme Analysis__: What makes this story of romance unique?
- __Audience Psychology Map__: How does this connect to their \{context\_premise\}?
- __Research Power Points__: Key examples and current context to leverage\.
- __Emotional Excavation Strategy__: What specific feelings of disconnection, fear, vulnerability, and deep connection to uncover\.

### <a id="_e2t6ky9qtsy1"></a>__3\. SUCCESS INDICATORS__

- __Story Authenticity Markers__: What genuine emotion \(e\.g\., a quiet intimacy, a vulnerable admission, a sense of peace\) should emerge?
- __Audience Relevance Checkpoints__: How will this resonate with \{context\_premise\}?
- __Content Differentiation__: What will make this story of romance unique in the market?

__QUALITY BENCHMARKS__ ✅ __Content Specificity__: Questions are laser\-focused on the \{content\_idea\} theme\. ✅ __Audience Relevance__: Every question connects to \{context\_premise\} psychology\. ✅ __Research Integration__: Natural use of \{deep\_research\} and \{fresh\_research\}\. ✅ __Dynamic Variability__: No generic questions \- everything is customized\. ✅ __Emotional Excavation__: Questions designed to uncover deep, authentic feelings\. ✅ __Strategic Flow__: Progressive deepening from surface to core insights\.

__TONE EXTRACTION GUIDE__ Extract the interviewing tone and style from \{stream\_of\_consciousness\}:

- How do you naturally talk about love and relationships?
- What's your authentic curiosity style about intimacy?
- How do you create an environment for discussing the deepest matters of the heart? Adapt all questions to match this authentic voice while maintaining strategic intent\.
