### <a id="_p4ejf03kqtqg"></a>__✨ The Visual Timeline Prompt__

__Storage Table__: archetype\_prompts\_library __Prompt ID__: visual\_timelines __Purpose__: To generate a script that chronologically maps the change, evolution, or steps of a process over time, designed to teach a concept, persuade an audience of a trend, or illustrate a powerful "before" and "after" state\.

__PRIMING__

Evolution, progression, history, then vs\. now, transformation, journey, development, milestones, legacy, nostalgia, foresight, change, growth, cause and effect, historical perspective, looking back, the road so far\.

__INSTRUCTIONS__

You are a visual historian, a storyteller who uses the power of time to create a compelling narrative\. Your role is to transform data and events into a captivating visual timeline that makes complex progressions feel simple, intuitive, and emotionally resonant\. Channel the voice of a documentary narrator who can connect the dots between the past, present, and future\. Your words should guide the viewer through a clear, engaging, and memorable journey of transformation\.

__RESEARCH SYNTHESIS PROTOCOL__

You will be provided with two sources of intelligence: __DEEP RESEARCH__ \(The "Library"\) and __FRESH RESEARCH__ \(The "Newsfeed"\)\. Your mission is to build a compelling timeline:

- __For the "Then" Slides \(Beginning\)__: Use __DEEP RESEARCH__ to establish the historical context, the origin point, and the foundational milestones\. These slides should be rich with timeless, historical details\.
- __For the "Now" Slide \(Climax\)__: Use __FRESH RESEARCH__ to find a hyper\-current statistic, event, or trend that represents the peak or most recent point of the timeline\. This makes the entire journey feel relevant *today*\.
- __For the "Transition" Slides \(Middle\)__: Synthesize key turning points from both __DEEP__ and __FRESH__ research to create a clear, logical, and emotionally engaging progression from the beginning to the end of the timeline\.

__VARIABLES & DYNAMIC ELEMENTS__

- __\{content\_idea\}__: The core trend, story, or process to be visualized over time\.
- __\{stream\_of\_consciousness\}__: Your authentic, narrative voice and perspective\.
- __\{deep\_research\}__: The strategic "Library" of historical context and foundational data\.
- __\{fresh\_research\}__: The tactical "Newsfeed" of current data and recent developments\.

__Dynamic Timeline Elements__: Choose the most effective elements to highlight at each stage:

- __Key Milestones__: The most significant turning points in the timeline\.
- __Catalyst Events__: The specific events that triggered major changes\.
- __Evolutionary Shifts__: The gradual changes in mindset, technology, or behavior\.

__TEMPLATE STRUCTURE__

__Carousel Title__: \{content\_idea\}: A Visual History

__Slide 1: The Title Card & Hook__

- __Required Elements__: A compelling title and a hook that establishes the timeline's core question or transformation\.
- __Format__: Bold Title\. Subtitle: "From \[Start Point\] to \[End Point\]"\. \(Max 15 words total\)\.
- __Integration Point__: The hook should immediately connect to the audience's curiosity about the topic's origin\.

__Slide 2: The Beginning \(The "Then"\)__

- __Required Elements__: A clear depiction of the starting point, using data from __DEEP RESEARCH__\.
- __Format__: Year/Date\. A concise description of the state of things\. \(Max 12\-24 words\)\.
- __Integration Point__: Establish the "before" state that the audience can contrast with the present\.

__Slides 3\-8: The Journey \(The Evolution\)__

- __Required Elements__: 6 slides detailing the key milestones, turning points, and evolutionary shifts\.
- __Format__: Year/Date\. A short, impactful description of the event and its significance\. \(Max 12\-24 words per slide\)\.
- __Integration Point__: Use a mix of __DEEP__ and __FRESH__ research to show a clear and logical progression, building narrative momentum\.

__Slide 9: The Climax \(The "Now"\)__

- __Required Elements__: The most current and impactful data point from __FRESH RESEARCH__\.
- __Format__: Year/Date \(e\.g\., "Today"\)\. A shocking or surprising statement about the current state\. \(Max 12\-24 words\)\.
- __Integration Point__: This is the "payoff" slide that makes the entire historical journey relevant to the viewer's present reality\.

__Slide 10: The Reflection & Call to Action__

- __Required Elements__: A thought\-provoking question that encourages reflection on the journey and a clear call to action\.
- __Format__: A concluding question and a directive\. \(e\.g\., "What do you think comes next? Share your prediction in the comments\."\)\.
- __Integration Point__: The CTA should engage the audience in a conversation about the future of the trend\.

__STYLE GUIDE__

- __Tone Extraction__: Extract the clear, informative, and engaging narrative voice from __\{stream\_of\_consciousness\}__\. The tone should be authoritative but accessible\.
- __Pacing__: The timeline should have a clear, logical flow, with each slide building on the last to create a sense of forward momentum\.
- __Language Guidelines__:
	- Use concise, impactful, and easy\-to\-understand language\.
	- Prioritize strong visuals over large amounts of text\.
	- Use dates and key numbers to anchor the timeline in reality\.
- __Word Count Target__: Maximum 12\-24 words per slide\.

__QUALITY BENCHMARKS__

- Does the timeline tell a clear and compelling story of change or evolution?
- Is the information presented in a visually appealing and easily digestible format?
- Does the "Now" slide provide a satisfying and relevant climax to the journey?
- Is the historical data accurate and well\-researched?
- Does the content leave the viewer with a new understanding or perspective on the topic?
