### <a id="_a3x0ltsycrj3"></a>__✨ The Transformation Story \(Interview Framework\)__

__Storage Table__: archetype\_prompts\_library __Prompt ID__: the\_transformation\_story\_interview __Purpose__: To generate a completely customized interview guide\. This engine synthesizes research to create unique questioning sequences designed to extract a coach's most powerful story about the emotional journey of profound personal change and metamorphosis\.

__PRIMING & REFERENCE LIBRARY__ Metamorphosis, the shedding of old skin, the journey of change, the power of reinvention, the beauty of evolution, the rise from ashes, the phoenix moment, the courage to change, the embrace of the new, the breaking of chains, the liberation of self, the path to growth, the evolution of consciousness, the blossoming of potential\.

__RESEARCH SYNTHESIS PROTOCOL__ You will analyze the variables to create completely customized interview questions:

- __Content Idea Analysis__: Break down the specific type of transformation in \{content\_idea\} \(e\.g\., mindset, lifestyle, career\)\.
- __Audience Psychology Mapping__: Use \{context\_premise\} to understand the audience's emotional landscape regarding being "stuck" and the fear of change\.
- __Reference Point Mining__: Extract 2\-3 compelling examples from \{deep\_research\} of powerful stories about personal evolution\.
- __Current Relevance__: Find timely angles from \{fresh\_research\} that make this story of transformation feel urgent and necessary\.

__VARIABLES & DYNAMIC ELEMENTS__

\{content\_idea\}: The specific transformation theme to explore

\{context\_premise\}: The audience's psychological profile \(fears, dreams, frustrations\)

\{deep\_research\}: Strategic intelligence and compelling examples

\{fresh\_research\}: Current trends and urgent context

\{coach\_background\}: Coach's expertise and journey

\{stream\_of\_consciousness\}: Your authentic voice and perspective

__DYNAMIC QUESTION GENERATION INSTRUCTIONS__

### <a id="_na6cdo3icmy8"></a>__STEP 1: CONTENT IDEA ANALYSIS__

First, analyze \{content\_idea\} to identify:

- __The Core Transformation Type__: What is changing? \(A belief, a habit, an identity, a circumstance\)\.
- __The Emotional Stakes__: What was the cost of staying the same? What was the reward of changing?
- __The Common Misconceptions__: What do people get wrong about what it takes to truly transform?
- __The Central Tension__: What was the primary force resisting the change? \(e\.g\., fear, comfort, identity\)\.

### <a id="_dqgiztf1tpw1"></a>__STEP 2: CONTEXT PREMISE INTEGRATION__

Using \{context\_premise\}, identify:

- __Audience's Transformation Anxiety__: What specific fears do they have about letting go of their old self?
- __Their "Change" Distortions__: What unrealistic expectations do they have about how personal growth happens?
- __Their Stagnation Barriers__: What is keeping them stuck in their "before" state?
- __Their "After" Vision__: What does the transformed life they are dreaming of look and feel like?

### <a id="_jd143twk6odg"></a>__STEP 3: RESEARCH\-POWERED REFERENCE POINTS__

From \{deep\_research\}, select 2\-3 examples that:

- Mirror the specific transformation theme in \{content\_idea\}\.
- Connect to the audience's psychological profile from \{context\_premise\}\.
- Provide surprising or counter\-intuitive insights about the process of change\.

### <a id="_f6uxsi8r98zb"></a>__STEP 4: GENERATE CUSTOM INTERVIEW SEQUENCE__

Now create a completely customized interview framework:

#### <a id="_5yyua6u1mmxn"></a>__PHASE 1: DYNAMIC CONTEXT SETTING__

Create an opening that weaves together the theme, the audience's feelings, and a compelling reference\. __Template__: "I want to explore the deep process of transformation around \[specific aspect of content\_idea\] with you\. Your audience often feels \[specific struggle from context\_premise\] when they think about making a big change\. It's so relevant because \[current trend from fresh\_research\] is forcing so many people to reinvent themselves\. I was just reading about \[specific example from deep\_research\], and it made me think\.\.\." Then generate 2\-3 __customized permission questions__ that feel natural and create a safe space for discussing personal evolution\.

#### <a id="_80v8fx426lr8"></a>__PHASE 2: THE "BEFORE" \- STAGNATION ARCHAEOLOGY__

Generate 4\-5 questions focused on the state before the transformation began:

- Questions that uncover their personal experience with the feeling of being "stuck" in relation to \{content\_idea\}\.
- Questions that reveal the deep emotional cost of not changing\.
- Questions that connect their "before" state to the audience's current fears from \{context\_premise\}\. Include 1\-2 __pattern\-matching probes__ using your selected references from \{deep\_research\}\.

#### <a id="_99s4u84yshot"></a>__PHASE 3: THE CATALYST \- "POINT OF NO RETURN" ARCHAEOLOGY__

Create 5\-6 questions that:

- Explore the specific event, realization, or moment of hitting "rock bottom" that made change a necessity\.
- Mirror the audience's desire for a "wake\-up call" from \{context\_premise\}\.
- Reference why this type of catalyst is so relevant now \(using \{fresh\_research\}\)\.
- Uncover the moment they decided the pain of staying the same was greater than the fear of changing\.

#### <a id="_wlmc6gohdfed"></a>__PHASE 4: THE PROCESS \- "THE MESSY MIDDLE" ARCHAEOLOGY__

Design 4\-5 questions that:

- Seek to capture the sensory and emotional details of the actual process of changing\.
- Challenge common misconceptions about transformation being a linear or easy path\.
- Connect to the audience's breakthrough barriers from \{context\_premise\}\.

#### <a id="_vipme3ewo3ku"></a>__PHASE 5: THE "AFTER" \- NEW REALITY ARCHAEOLOGY__

Create 4\-5 questions that:

- Explore the specific, tangible ways their daily life and internal state are different now\.
- Connect to what the audience truly desires from a feeling of transformation, based on \{context\_premise\}\.
- Uncover the unexpected emotional reality of what their "after" state actually feels like\.

#### <a id="_4ya574x0a9tf"></a>__PHASE 6: WISDOM EXTRACTION \- ACTIONABLE INSIGHTS__

Generate 3\-4 questions that:

- Distill the core lesson learned about the nature of true, sustainable change\.
- Address the audience's immediate next steps for beginning their own transformation\.
- Provide hope for their own journey of evolution\.

__OUTPUT REQUIREMENTS__

### <a id="_4qdqmgn0z996"></a>__1\. CUSTOMIZED INTERVIEW GUIDE__

- __Content\-Specific Opening Frame__ \(unique to \{content\_idea\}\)
- __Dynamic Permission Questions__ \(2\-3 options tailored to the theme\)
- __Phase\-by\-Phase Question Sets__ \(completely customized to the content and audience\)
- __Strategic Reference Integration__ \(natural weaving of \{deep\_research\} examples\)

### <a id="_hqjygjpmnvd8"></a>__2\. INTERVIEW INTELLIGENCE BRIEF__

- __Content Theme Analysis__: What makes this story of transformation unique?
- __Audience Psychology Map__: How does this connect to their \{context\_premise\}?
- __Research Power Points__: Key examples and current context to leverage\.
- __Emotional Excavation Strategy__: What specific feelings of being stuck, the fear of change, and the peace of alignment to uncover\.

### <a id="_ssjt7vbe87u1"></a>__3\. SUCCESS INDICATORS__

- __Story Authenticity Markers__: What genuine emotion \(e\.g\., vulnerability, relief, quiet confidence\) should emerge?
- __Audience Relevance Checkpoints__: How will this resonate with \{context\_premise\}?
- __Content Differentiation__: What will make this story of transformation unique in the market?

__QUALITY BENCHMARKS__ ✅ __Content Specificity__: Questions are laser\-focused on the \{content\_idea\} theme\. ✅ __Audience Relevance__: Every question connects to \{context\_premise\} psychology\. ✅ __Research Integration__: Natural use of \{deep\_research\} and \{fresh\_research\}\. ✅ __Dynamic Variability__: No generic questions \- everything is customized\. ✅ __Emotional Excavation__: Questions designed to uncover deep, authentic feelings\. ✅ __Strategic Flow__: Progressive deepening from surface to core insights\.

__TONE EXTRACTION GUIDE__ Extract the interviewing tone and style from \{stream\_of\_consciousness\}:

- How do you naturally talk about growth and change?
- What's your authentic curiosity style about personal evolution?
- How do you create emotional safety for discussing past selves? Adapt all questions to match this authentic voice while maintaining strategic intent\.
