### <a id="_vbptkc6n3a91"></a>__✨ The Cuteness Story \(Interview Framework\)__

__Storage Table__: archetype\_prompts\_library __Prompt ID__: the\_cuteness\_story\_interview __Purpose__: To generate a completely customized interview guide\. This engine synthesizes research to create unique questioning sequences designed to extract a coach's most powerful story about a heartwarming and adorable moment\.

__PRIMING & REFERENCE LIBRARY__ Button noses, fluffy tails, clumsy paws, big eyes, tiny giggles, heartwarming moments, adorable creatures, playful antics, innocent charm, the aww factor, the irresistible urge to cuddle, the melting heart, the joy of innocence, the power of cute, the happiness overload, the cuteness explosion\.

__RESEARCH SYNTHESIS PROTOCOL__ You will analyze the variables to create completely customized interview questions:

- __Content Idea Analysis__: Break down the specific source of cuteness in \{content\_idea\} \(e\.g\., a pet, a child, an act of innocence\)\.
- __Audience Psychology Mapping__: Use \{context\_premise\} to understand the audience's emotional landscape regarding their need for warmth, affection, and lightheartedness\.
- __Reference Point Mining__: Extract 2\-3 compelling examples from \{deep\_research\} of universally heartwarming or adorable stories\.
- __Current Relevance__: Find timely angles from \{fresh\_research\} that make this story of cuteness feel like a needed dose of positivity\.

__VARIABLES & DYNAMIC ELEMENTS__

\{content\_idea\}: The specific cuteness theme to explore

\{context\_premise\}: The audience's psychological profile \(fears, dreams, frustrations\)

\{deep\_research\}: Strategic intelligence and compelling examples

\{fresh\_research\}: Current trends and urgent context

\{coach\_background\}: Coach's expertise and journey

\{stream\_of\_consciousness\}: Your authentic voice and perspective

__DYNAMIC QUESTION GENERATION INSTRUCTIONS__

### <a id="_lh7w90gf95he"></a>__STEP 1: CONTENT IDEA ANALYSIS__

First, analyze \{content\_idea\} to identify:

- __The Core Cuteness Type__: What is the source? \(An animal, a child, an unexpected moment of innocence\)\.
- __The Emotional Stakes__: What feeling did this moment provide? \(Comfort, joy, a break from stress\)\.
- __The Common Misconceptions__: What do people get wrong about the power of simple, charming moments?
- __The Central Tension__: What was the contrast between the "normal" state and this sudden moment of cuteness?

### <a id="_8d6fttvu06yv"></a>__STEP 2: CONTEXT PREMISE INTEGRATION__

Using \{context\_premise\}, identify:

- __Audience's Cuteness Need__: What specific stress or cynicism in their life makes a story like this so appealing?
- __Their "Joy" Distortions__: What complex things do they believe they need to be happy, which this simple story can contrast?
- __Their Affection Barriers__: What prevents them from noticing or appreciating these small, heartwarming moments?
- __Their "Aww" Vision__: What does the feeling of pure, uncomplicated warmth they are dreaming of look and feel like?

### <a id="_pl5lnsbmoy8"></a>__STEP 3: RESEARCH\-POWERED REFERENCE POINTS__

From \{deep\_research\}, select 2\-3 examples that:

- Mirror the specific cuteness theme in \{content\_idea\}\.
- Connect to the audience's psychological profile from \{context\_premise\}\.
- Provide insights into the psychology of why humans are drawn to cuteness\.

### <a id="_c4s60mfx6rd5"></a>__STEP 4: GENERATE CUSTOM INTERVIEW SEQUENCE__

Now create a completely customized interview framework:

#### <a id="_2cat8ia10df7"></a>__PHASE 1: DYNAMIC CONTEXT SETTING__

Create an opening that weaves together the theme, the audience's feelings, and a compelling reference\. __Template__: "I want to explore the simple, powerful emotion of cuteness around \[specific aspect of content\_idea\] with you\. Your audience is often dealing with \[specific struggle from context\_premise\], and a moment of pure charm can be a real antidote\. It's so relevant because \[current trend from fresh\_research\] shows everyone is searching for more lighthearted content\. I was just reading about \[specific example from deep\_research\], and it made me think\.\.\." Then generate 2\-3 __customized permission questions__ that feel natural and create a safe space for sharing heartwarming stories\.

#### <a id="_vhggstcs5ycs"></a>__PHASE 2: THE "BEFORE" \- THE NORMAL MOMENT ARCHAEOLOGY__

Generate 4\-5 questions focused on the state *before* the cute moment happened:

- Questions that uncover the ordinary, everyday context in which the adorable event occurred\.
- Questions that reveal the emotional state right before the cute interruption \(e\.g\., stressed, busy, tired\)\.
- Questions that connect this "normal" state to the audience's own daily grind from \{context\_premise\}\. Include 1\-2 __pattern\-matching probes__ using your selected references from \{deep\_research\}\.

#### <a id="_uwcllvmj8bbl"></a>__PHASE 3: THE INCIDENT \- "MISCHIEF" ARCHAEOLOGY__

Create 5\-6 questions that:

- Explore the specific, endearing "mischief" or problem the cute subject caused\.
- Mirror the audience's own "noble failure" experiences with pets or children from \{context\_premise\}\.
- Reference why this type of adorable chaos is so relatable now \(using \{fresh\_research\}\)\.
- Uncover the moment of mild frustration just before the heart\-melting resolution\.

#### <a id="_tumcklo8e74s"></a>__PHASE 4: THE REVEAL \- "HEART\-MELTING" ARCHAEOLOGY__

Design 4\-5 questions that:

- Seek to capture the sensory and emotional details of the moment the mischief turned into something even more adorable\.
- Challenge common misconceptions about why these simple moments are so emotionally powerful\.
- Connect to the audience's breakthrough barriers from \{context\_premise\}\.

#### <a id="_w5kle2oa6uxs"></a>__PHASE 5: THE "AFTER" \- THE WARMTH ARCHAEOLOGY__

Create 4\-5 questions that:

- Explore the specific, tangible shift in their emotional state after the event\.
- Connect to what the audience truly desires from a feeling of warmth and affection, based on \{context\_premise\}\.
- Uncover the unexpected emotional reality of how a tiny, cute moment can change an entire day\.

#### <a id="_x2p658bkzbpy"></a>__PHASE 6: WISDOM EXTRACTION \- ACTIONABLE INSIGHTS__

Generate 3\-4 questions that:

- Distill the core lesson learned about the importance of noticing and appreciating small, charming moments\.
- Address the audience's immediate next steps for finding more delight in their own lives\.
- Provide hope and a sense of lightheartedness\.

__OUTPUT REQUIREMENTS__

### <a id="_smbixll4gf1a"></a>__1\. CUSTOMIZED INTERVIEW GUIDE__

- __Content\-Specific Opening Frame__ \(unique to \{content\_idea\}\)
- __Dynamic Permission Questions__ \(2\-3 options tailored to the theme\)
- __Phase\-by\-Phase Question Sets__ \(completely customized to the content and audience\)
- __Strategic Reference Integration__ \(natural weaving of \{deep\_research\} examples\)

### <a id="_3mcvapvemhi1"></a>__2\. INTERVIEW INTELLIGENCE BRIEF__

- __Content Theme Analysis__: What makes this story of cuteness unique?
- __Audience Psychology Map__: How does this connect to their \{context\_premise\}?
- __Research Power Points__: Key examples and current context to leverage\.
- __Emotional Excavation Strategy__: What specific feelings of warmth, affection, mild frustration, and overwhelming delight to uncover\.

### <a id="_9xfw3kqaw1lf"></a>__3\. SUCCESS INDICATORS__

- __Story Authenticity Markers__: What genuine emotion \(e\.g\., a heartfelt laugh, a warm smile, a sense of pure delight\) should emerge?
- __Audience Relevance Checkpoints__: How will this resonate with \{context\_premise\}?
- __Content Differentiation__: What will make this story of cuteness unique in the market?

__QUALITY BENCHMARKS__ ✅ __Content Specificity__: Questions are laser\-focused on the \{content\_idea\} theme\. ✅ __Audience Relevance__: Every question connects to \{context\_premise\} psychology\. ✅ __Research Integration__: Natural use of \{deep\_research\} and \{fresh\_research\}\. ✅ __Dynamic Variability__: No generic questions \- everything is customized\. ✅ __Emotional Excavation__: Questions designed to uncover deep, authentic feelings\. ✅ __Strategic Flow__: Progressive deepening from surface to core insights\.

__TONE EXTRACTION GUIDE__ Extract the interviewing tone and style from \{stream\_of\_consciousness\}:

- How do you naturally talk about lighthearted and charming moments?
- What's your authentic curiosity style about innocence and playfulness?
- How do you create an environment for sharing joyful and affectionate stories? Adapt all questions to match this authentic voice while maintaining strategic intent\.
