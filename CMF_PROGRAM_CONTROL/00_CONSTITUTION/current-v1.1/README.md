- Do not ask AI to invent human truth.
- Do not treat a topic as an edge.
- Do not generate interview questions from a biography or generic persona alone.
- Do not flatten Activative Intelligence into `semantic_intent` plus a prompt.
- Do not let Delegation adapters drop constitutional fields.
