# VAE V1.1 Activative Visual Contract Amendment

**Amends:** `CMF_VISUAL_ASSET_EDITOR_SHARDED_PRD_V1`  
**Aligned package:** `CMF_VISUAL_ASSET_EDITOR_SHARDED_PRD_V1_1`  
**Authority:** CCP Activative Intelligence & Visual Narrative Constitution v1.1.0  
**Status:** Binding amendment for Visual Asset Demand, evaluation, and readiness

## Purpose

The original VAE product boundary remains valid. This amendment prevents the editor from receiving a semantically flattened request and then guessing the missing Activative and visual-narrative decisions.

## Expanded Visual Asset Demand

The authoritative demand must now carry or reference:

```text
Activative semantic lineage
Activation contract
Visual Semantic Pack
Visual Narrative Program
Feature contracts
T/V somatic route request
non-empty wrong-reading locks
Composition Intent
```

### Activative semantic lineage

Required references include the Activative Intelligence Pack and the authoritative Identity DNA, Context Premise, Resonance, and Matrix of Edging objects used to compile it. Interview-derived assets also preserve Reaction Receipt and Expression Moment references.

### Activation contract

The demand declares edge pressure, activation directions, viewer roles, stance, identity urges, intended reaction, participation design, and smallest useful commitment.

### Visual Semantic Pack

The demand declares recognition intent, selected recognition carrier, audience visual-world references, visual dog-whistles or behavior traces where relevant, real-life reference classes, emotional load carrier, and the Semiotic MCDA decision receipt.

### Visual Narrative Program

The demand declares pattern-matching entry, pattern interrupt, attention-state sequence, viewer-role progression, prediction gap, payoff, affinity field, and anticipation residue.

### Feature contracts

Gaze, hands, posture, witness relationships, object punctum, negative space, contact, distance, crop, and motion are explicitly contracted when they can change meaning.

## Wrong-reading locks

`wrong_reading_locks` is mandatory and non-empty for generation, compositing, restyling, inpainting, outpainting, and any semantic transformation. A deterministic resize or encoding-only route may inherit locks from the accepted master but must reference them.

## Evaluation expansion

Versioned Visual Evaluation Profiles must evaluate applicable dimensions:

```text
zero-second hook
pattern-match strength
pattern-interrupt strength
viewer-role clarity
activation direction fidelity
prediction gap
payoff
human affinity
anticipation residue
anti-cliché strength
wrong-reading risk
no-text survival / delete-caption test
feature-contract compliance
```

Semantic clarity and aesthetic quality cannot compensate for failed activation.

## Affected base requirements

This release strengthens `FR-009`, `FR-107`, `FR-108`, and `FR-111` while preserving their stable identifiers. It updates the Visual Asset Demand and Visual Quality Evaluation schemas and their Format 02 examples.

## Acceptance tests

1. Schema validation rejects a generated demand without wrong-reading locks.
2. The VAE can trace every selected asset to its Activative Intelligence Pack and, when applicable, Expression Moment.
3. The materializer cannot infer recognition carrier or viewer role solely from free-form semantic intent.
4. Evaluation rejects a technically valid asset when role activation, pattern interrupt, or wrong-reading tests fail.
5. A delete-caption test is enforced for no-text profiles.

## Non-goals

- Giving the VAE ownership of Identity DNA, Context Premise, edge selection, or content archetype routing.
- Replacing the Content Harness as semantic authority.
- Making the VAE execute interviews or compile Activative Calls.
