### <a id="_cbvf9lub2f72"></a>__✨ The Surprise Story \(Interview Framework\)__

__Storage Table__: archetype\_prompts\_library__Prompt ID__: the\_surprise\_story\_interview__Purpose__: To generate a completely customized interview guide\. This engine synthesizes research to create unique questioning sequences designed to extract a coach's most powerful story about a shocking revelation or an unexpected twist that shattered their perception of reality\.

__PRIMING & REFERENCE LIBRARY__ The unexpected twist, the plot thickens, the gasp of astonishment, the mind blown, the jaws drop, the shockwaves ripple, the surprise attack, the hidden secret, the revealed truth, the power of the unexpected, the thrill of surprise, the joy of the unforeseen, misdirection, revelation\.

__RESEARCH SYNTHESIS PROTOCOL__ You will analyze the variables to create completely customized interview questions:

- __Content Idea Analysis__: Break down the specific type of surprise in \{content\_idea\} \(e\.g\., a betrayal, a hidden opportunity, a shocking truth\)\.
- __Audience Psychology Mapping__: Use \{context\_premise\} to understand the audience's emotional landscape regarding certainty, assumptions, and having their worldview challenged\.
- __Reference Point Mining__: Extract 2\-3 compelling examples from \{deep\_research\} of powerful stories about major plot twists or revelations\.
- __Current Relevance__: Find timely angles from \{fresh\_research\} that make this story of surprise feel urgent and relevant\.

__VARIABLES & DYNAMIC ELEMENTS__

\{content\_idea\}: The specific surprise theme to explore

\{context\_premise\}: The audience's psychological profile \(fears, dreams, frustrations\)

\{deep\_research\}: Strategic intelligence and compelling examples

\{fresh\_research\}: Current trends and urgent context

\{coach\_background\}: Coach's expertise and journey

\{stream\_of\_consciousness\}: Your authentic voice and perspective

__DYNAMIC QUESTION GENERATION INSTRUCTIONS__

### <a id="_vseic88n8prw"></a>__STEP 1: CONTENT IDEA ANALYSIS__

First, analyze \{content\_idea\} to identify:

- __The Core Surprise Type__: What was the nature of the twist? \(A revelation of truth, a subversion of expectation, a change in identity\)\.
- __The Emotional Stakes__: What was the cost of the old belief? What did the new reality make possible?
- __The Common Misconceptions__: What was the "obvious" but wrong assumption that everyone \(including the protagonist\) held?
- __The Twist's Nature__: Was it a sudden, shocking event or a slow, dawning realization?

### <a id="_exl9ve8erx8u"></a>__STEP 2: CONTEXT PREMISE INTEGRATION__

Using \{context\_premise\}, identify:

- __Audience's Surprise Anxiety__: What specific fears do they have about being wrong or having the rug pulled out from under them?
- __Their "Certainty" Distortions__: What comforting but false beliefs do they cling to that this story could challenge?
- __Their Perception Barriers__: What is preventing them from seeing the "twist" in their own lives?
- __Their Desired Revelation__: What is the surprising truth they are secretly hoping to discover?

### <a id="_4a19lgwpdgx5"></a>__STEP 3: RESEARCH\-POWERED REFERENCE POINTS__

From \{deep\_research\}, select 2\-3 examples that:

- Mirror the specific surprise theme in \{content\_idea\}\.
- Connect to the audience's psychological profile from \{context\_premise\}\.
- Provide surprising or counter\-intuitive insights about the nature of perception and reality\.

### <a id="_gdesivvlwn4s"></a>__STEP 4: GENERATE CUSTOM INTERVIEW SEQUENCE__

Now create a completely customized interview framework:

#### <a id="_lzkk3gk9l2t1"></a>__PHASE 1: DYNAMIC CONTEXT SETTING__

Create an opening that weaves together the theme, the audience's feelings, and a compelling reference\.__Template__: "I want to explore the powerful nature of surprise around \[specific aspect of content\_idea\] with you\. Your audience often feels \[specific struggle from context\_premise\] when their expectations don't match reality\. It's so relevant because \[current trend from fresh\_research\] is showing just how unpredictable things are\. I was just reading about \[specific example from deep\_research\], and it made me think\.\.\." Then generate 2\-3 __customized permission questions__ that feel natural and create a safe space for discussing moments of shock and revelation\.

#### <a id="_8j6qvai530dy"></a>__PHASE 2: THE "BEFORE" \- EXPECTATION ARCHAEOLOGY__

Generate 4\-5 questions focused on the state *before* the twist was revealed:

- Questions that uncover their personal experience with the specific, flawed assumption related to \{content\_idea\}\.
- Questions that reveal the deep emotional investment they had in that old reality\.
- Questions that connect their "before" state to the audience's current beliefs from \{context\_premise\}\. Include 1\-2 __pattern\-matching probes__ using your selected references from \{deep\_research\}\.

#### <a id="_8fvedtvnwqu1"></a>__PHASE 3: THE BUILDUP \- "CONFIRMATION BIAS" ARCHAEOLOGY__

Create 5\-6 questions that:

- Explore the specific, misleading clues they saw that reinforced their incorrect belief\.
- Mirror the audience's own "noble failure" patterns of seeing only what they want to see from \{context\_premise\}\.
- Reference why this false narrative was so convincing at the time \(using \{fresh\_research\}\)\.
- Uncover the moment they first felt a tiny crack in their certainty\.

#### <a id="_z634h31p8voc"></a>__PHASE 4: THE REVEAL \- "TWIST" ARCHAEOLOGY__

Design 4\-5 questions that:

- Seek to capture the sensory and emotional details of the exact moment the surprise was revealed\.
- Challenge common misconceptions about what it feels like to have your worldview shattered\.
- Connect to the audience's breakthrough barriers from \{context\_premise\}\.

#### <a id="_1y24ism8m5r2"></a>__PHASE 5: THE "AFTER" \- NEW REALITY ARCHAEOLOGY__

Create 4\-5 questions that:

- Explore the specific, tangible ways their worldview and actions are different now\.
- Connect to what the audience truly desires from a feeling of clarity, based on \{context\_premise\}\.
- Uncover the unexpected emotional reality of what it feels like to live in this new, surprising reality\.

#### <a id="_213li3rwan0a"></a>__PHASE 6: WISDOM EXTRACTION \- ACTIONABLE INSIGHTS__

Generate 3\-4 questions that:

- Distill the core lesson learned about the nature of perception and assumption\.
- Address the audience's immediate next steps for challenging their own beliefs\.
- Provide hope for their own journey to find a deeper truth\.

__OUTPUT REQUIREMENTS__

### <a id="_dt9ha69iqol5"></a>__1\. CUSTOMIZED INTERVIEW GUIDE__

- __Content\-Specific Opening Frame__ \(unique to \{content\_idea\}\)
- __Dynamic Permission Questions__ \(2\-3 options tailored to the theme\)
- __Phase\-by\-Phase Question Sets__ \(completely customized to the content and audience\)
- __Strategic Reference Integration__ \(natural weaving of \{deep\_research\} examples\)

### <a id="_6syt7qg2s1mv"></a>__2\. INTERVIEW INTELLIGENCE BRIEF__

- __Content Theme Analysis__: What makes this story of surprise unique?
- __Audience Psychology Map__: How does this connect to their \{context\_premise\}?
- __Research Power Points__: Key examples and current context to leverage\.
- __Emotional Excavation Strategy__: What specific feelings of certainty, doubt, shock, and new understanding to uncover\.

### <a id="_jpk3i6qwaehg"></a>__3\. SUCCESS INDICATORS__

- __Story Authenticity Markers__: What genuine emotion \(e\.g\., a gasp of realization, a moment of stunned silence, a laugh of disbelief\) should emerge?
- __Audience Relevance Checkpoints__: How will this resonate with \{context\_premise\}?
- __Content Differentiation__: What will make this story of surprise unique in the market?

__QUALITY BENCHMARKS__ ✅ __Content Specificity__: Questions are laser\-focused on the \{content\_idea\} theme\. ✅ __Audience Relevance__: Every question connects to \{context\_premise\} psychology\. ✅ __Research Integration__: Natural use of \{deep\_research\} and \{fresh\_research\}\. ✅ __Dynamic Variability__: No generic questions \- everything is customized\. ✅ __Emotional Excavation__: Questions designed to uncover deep, authentic feelings\. ✅ __Strategic Flow__: Progressive deepening from surface to core insights\.

__TONE EXTRACTION GUIDE__ Extract the interviewing tone and style from \{stream\_of\_consciousness\}:

- How do you naturally talk about mystery and revelation?
- What's your authentic curiosity style about plot twists?
- How do you create an environment for discussing moments of being profoundly wrong? Adapt all questions to match this authentic voice while maintaining strategic intent\.
