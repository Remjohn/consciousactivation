### <a id="_uwnpe7ep1x83"></a>__✨ The Empowerment Story \(Interview Framework\)__

__Storage Table__: archetype\_prompts\_library __Prompt ID__: the\_empowerment\_story\_interview __Purpose__: To generate a completely customized interview guide\. This engine synthesizes research to create unique questioning sequences designed to extract a coach's most powerful story about the emotional journey of reclaiming personal agency and finding their true power\.

__PRIMING & REFERENCE LIBRARY__ Taking the reins, the power within, the rise from adversity, the breaking of chains, the voice unleashed, the strength discovered, the self\-belief blossoms, the confidence soars, the leader emerges, the impact made, the change ignited, the world transformed, the power of one, the force of many, the empowerment wave\.

__RESEARCH SYNTHESIS PROTOCOL__ You will analyze the variables to create completely customized interview questions:

- __Content Idea Analysis__: Break down the specific type of empowerment in \{content\_idea\} \(e\.g\., finding your voice, setting boundaries, leaving a toxic situation\)\.
- __Audience Psychology Mapping__: Use \{context\_premise\} to understand the audience's emotional landscape regarding powerlessness, self\-doubt, and the desire for agency\.
- __Reference Point Mining__: Extract 2\-3 compelling examples from \{deep\_research\} of powerful stories about individuals reclaiming their power\.
- __Current Relevance__: Find timely angles from \{fresh\_research\} that make this story of empowerment feel urgent and necessary\.

__VARIABLES & DYNAMIC ELEMENTS__

\{content\_idea\}: The specific empowerment theme to explore

\{context\_premise\}: The audience's psychological profile \(fears, dreams, frustrations\)

\{deep\_research\}: Strategic intelligence and compelling examples

\{fresh\_research\}: Current trends and urgent context

\{coach\_background\}: Coach's expertise and journey

\{stream\_of\_consciousness\}: Your authentic voice and perspective

__DYNAMIC QUESTION GENERATION INSTRUCTIONS__

### <a id="_6iyuibjs4xm4"></a>__STEP 1: CONTENT IDEA ANALYSIS__

First, analyze \{content\_idea\} to identify:

- __The Core Empowerment Type__: What power was reclaimed? \(Voice, choice, financial, emotional\)\.
- __The Emotional Stakes__: What was the cost of being powerless? What did authentic power make possible?
- __The Common Misconceptions__: What do people get wrong about what it means to be powerful?
- __The Central Tension__: What was the primary internal or external force that was disempowering them?

### <a id="_ogd2qd5z1uez"></a>__STEP 2: CONTEXT PREMISE INTEGRATION__

Using \{context\_premise\}, identify:

- __Audience's Empowerment Anxiety__: What specific fears do they have about speaking up or taking control?
- __Their "Power" Distortions__: What unhealthy beliefs do they hold about what power looks like \(e\.g\., aggression, selfishness\)?
- __Their Agency Barriers__: What is keeping them stuck in a state of powerlessness?
- __Their Empowerment Vision__: What does the feeling of true, authentic agency they are dreaming of look and feel like?

### <a id="_xldxe9wtcvsv"></a>__STEP 3: RESEARCH\-POWERED REFERENCE POINTS__

From \{deep\_research\}, select 2\-3 examples that:

- Mirror the specific empowerment theme in \{content\_idea\}\.
- Connect to the audience's psychological profile from \{context\_premise\}\.
- Provide surprising or counter\-intuitive insights about the nature of personal power\.

### <a id="_ospjo562dwge"></a>__STEP 4: GENERATE CUSTOM INTERVIEW SEQUENCE__

Now create a completely customized interview framework:

#### <a id="_xy9fx6wo248c"></a>__PHASE 1: DYNAMIC CONTEXT SETTING__

Create an opening that weaves together the theme, the audience's feelings, and a compelling reference\. __Template__: "I want to explore the incredible journey to empowerment around \[specific aspect of content\_idea\] with you\. Your audience often feels \[specific struggle from context\_premise\] when they feel like their voice isn't heard\. It's so relevant because \[current trend from fresh\_research\] is making it even more challenging to feel in control\. I was just reading about \[specific example from deep\_research\], and it made me think\.\.\." Then generate 2\-3 __customized permission questions__ that feel natural and create a safe space for discussing power and vulnerability\.

#### <a id="_o38wedlzkxdt"></a>__PHASE 2: THE "BEFORE" \- POWERLESSNESS ARCHAEOLOGY__

Generate 4\-5 questions focused on the state *before* they found their power:

- Questions that uncover their personal experience with the specific feeling of being powerless related to \{content\_idea\}\.
- Questions that reveal the deep emotional cost of that disempowerment\.
- Questions that connect their "before" state to the audience's current fears from \{context\_premise\}\. Include 1\-2 __pattern\-matching probes__ using your selected references from \{deep\_research\}\.

#### <a id="_46kps3lbb3jm"></a>__PHASE 3: THE STRUGGLE \- "FLAWED FIGHT" ARCHAEOLOGY__

Create 5\-6 questions that:

- Explore the specific, conventional ways they tried to gain power, which ultimately failed or felt inauthentic\.
- Mirror the audience's own "noble failure" patterns of fighting on the system's terms from \{context\_premise\}\.
- Reference why these conventional methods of fighting are so prevalent now \(using \{fresh\_research\}\)\.
- Uncover the moment they realized that fighting inauthentically was just another form of powerlessness\.

#### <a id="_wrrl358ejomp"></a>__PHASE 4: THE SPARK \- "INTERNAL SHIFT" ARCHAEOLOGY__

Design 4\-5 questions that:

- Seek to capture the sensory and emotional details of the moment they realized true power came from within\.
- Challenge common misconceptions about empowerment being a loud, aggressive act\.
- Connect to the audience's breakthrough barriers from \{context\_premise\}\.

#### <a id="_bwmhupjv29vj"></a>__PHASE 5: THE "AFTER" \- AGENCY ARCHAEOLOGY__

Create 4\-5 questions that:

- Explore the specific, tangible ways their daily actions and internal state are different now\.
- Connect to what the audience truly desires from a feeling of agency, based on \{context\_premise\}\.
- Uncover the unexpected emotional reality of what it feels like to live with authentic power\.

#### <a id="_tfrkwlq5mrsb"></a>__PHASE 6: WISDOM EXTRACTION \- ACTIONABLE INSIGHTS__

Generate 3\-4 questions that:

- Distill the core lesson learned about the nature of true, sustainable empowerment\.
- Address the audience's immediate next steps for reclaiming their own agency\.
- Provide hope for their own journey to find their voice\.

__OUTPUT REQUIREMENTS__

### <a id="_49npuiz5ul8n"></a>__1\. CUSTOMIZED INTERVIEW GUIDE__

- __Content\-Specific Opening Frame__ \(unique to \{content\_idea\}\)
- __Dynamic Permission Questions__ \(2\-3 options tailored to the theme\)
- __Phase\-by\-Phase Question Sets__ \(completely customized to the content and audience\)
- __Strategic Reference Integration__ \(natural weaving of \{deep\_research\} examples\)

### <a id="_oehdz5eppoct"></a>__2\. INTERVIEW INTELLIGENCE BRIEF__

- __Content Theme Analysis__: What makes this story of empowerment unique?
- __Audience Psychology Map__: How does this connect to their \{context\_premise\}?
- __Research Power Points__: Key examples and current context to leverage\.
- __Emotional Excavation Strategy__: What specific feelings of powerlessness, frustration, reclamation, and confidence to uncover\.

### <a id="_tu9dy1l89szy"></a>__3\. SUCCESS INDICATORS__

- __Story Authenticity Markers__: What genuine emotion \(e\.g\., quiet confidence, a sense of calm strength, righteous conviction\) should emerge?
- __Audience Relevance Checkpoints__: How will this resonate with \{context\_premise\}?
- __Content Differentiation__: What will make this story of empowerment unique in the market?

__QUALITY BENCHMARKS__ ✅ __Content Specificity__: Questions are laser\-focused on the \{content\_idea\} theme\. ✅ __Audience Relevance__: Every question connects to \{context\_premise\} psychology\. ✅ __Research Integration__: Natural use of \{deep\_research\} and \{fresh\_research\}\. ✅ __Dynamic Variability__: No generic questions \- everything is customized\. ✅ __Emotional Excavation__: Questions designed to uncover deep, authentic feelings\. ✅ __Strategic Flow__: Progressive deepening from surface to core insights\.

__TONE EXTRACTION GUIDE__ Extract the interviewing tone and style from \{stream\_of\_consciousness\}:

- How do you naturally talk about power and agency?
- What's your authentic curiosity style about inner strength?
- How do you create an environment for discussing moments of feeling powerless? Adapt all questions to match this authentic voice while maintaining strategic intent\.
