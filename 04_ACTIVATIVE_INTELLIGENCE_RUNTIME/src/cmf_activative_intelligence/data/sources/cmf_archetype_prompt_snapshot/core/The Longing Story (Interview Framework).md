### <a id="_gzv9wmeumch0"></a>__✨ The Longing Story \(Interview Framework\)__

__Storage Table__: archetype\_prompts\_library __Prompt ID__: the\_longing\_story\_interview __Purpose__: To generate a completely customized interview guide\. This engine synthesizes research to create unique questioning sequences designed to extract a coach's most powerful story about the emotional journey of deep yearning and unexpected fulfillment\.

__PRIMING & REFERENCE LIBRARY__ Yearning hearts, the ache of absence, the dream of reunion, the wistful gaze, the distant shore, the unattainable desire, the unrequited love, the lost paradise, the homesickness lingers, the nostalgia bites, the power of memory, the bittersweet ache, the longing for connection, the search for belonging, the hope for fulfillment\.

__RESEARCH SYNTHESIS PROTOCOL__ You will analyze the variables to create completely customized interview questions:

- __Content Idea Analysis__: Break down the specific object or feeling of longing in \{content\_idea\}\.
- __Audience Psychology Mapping__: Use \{context\_premise\} to understand the audience's emotional landscape regarding their own unmet desires and feelings of absence\.
- __Reference Point Mining__: Extract 2\-3 compelling examples from \{deep\_research\} of powerful stories about yearning and fulfillment\.
- __Current Relevance__: Find timely angles from \{fresh\_research\} that make this story of longing feel urgent and relevant\.

__VARIABLES & DYNAMIC ELEMENTS__

\{content\_idea\}: The specific longing theme to explore

\{context\_premise\}: The audience's psychological profile \(fears, dreams, frustrations\)

\{deep\_research\}: Strategic intelligence and compelling examples

\{fresh\_research\}: Current trends and urgent context

\{coach\_background\}: Coach's expertise and journey

\{stream\_of\_consciousness\}: Your authentic voice and perspective

__DYNAMIC QUESTION GENERATION INSTRUCTIONS__

### <a id="_tt6o3u9ejsrv"></a>__STEP 1: CONTENT IDEA ANALYSIS__

First, analyze \{content\_idea\} to identify:

- __The Core Longing Type__: What was being yearned for? \(A person, a place, a past self, a feeling\)\.
- __The Emotional Stakes__: What was the cost of this absence? What did they believe fulfillment would bring?
- __The Common Misconceptions__: What do people get wrong about the nature of their own desires?
- __The Central Tension__: What was the primary obstacle preventing them from attaining what they longed for?

### <a id="_apqrvhdnb1sg"></a>__STEP 2: CONTEXT PREMISE INTEGRATION__

Using \{context\_premise\}, identify:

- __Audience's Longing Anxiety__: What specific fears do they have about their own desires never being met?
- __Their "Fulfillment" Distortions__: What unrealistic expectations do they have about what will happen if they get what they want?
- __Their Yearning Barriers__: What is preventing them from understanding or addressing their own deep longings?
- __Their Fulfillment Vision__: What does the feeling of true, deep fulfillment they are dreaming of look and feel like?

### <a id="_7g7pv9wlak9v"></a>__STEP 3: RESEARCH\-POWERED REFERENCE POINTS__

From \{deep\_research\}, select 2\-3 examples that:

- Mirror the specific longing theme in \{content\_idea\}\.
- Connect to the audience's psychological profile from \{context\_premise\}\.
- Provide surprising or counter\-intuitive insights about the nature of desire and fulfillment\.

### <a id="_dty2amkcdw1r"></a>__STEP 4: GENERATE CUSTOM INTERVIEW SEQUENCE__

Now create a completely customized interview framework:

#### <a id="_fck5ygsftx8d"></a>__PHASE 1: DYNAMIC CONTEXT SETTING__

Create an opening that weaves together the theme, the audience's feelings, and a compelling reference\. __Template__: "I want to explore the powerful emotion of longing around \[specific aspect of content\_idea\] with you\. Your audience often feels \[specific struggle from context\_premise\] when they're yearning for something more\. It's so relevant because \[current trend from fresh\_research\] is highlighting this collective feeling of absence\. I was just reading about \[specific example from deep\_research\], and it made me think\.\.\." Then generate 2\-3 __customized permission questions__ that feel natural and create a safe space for discussing deep desires\.

#### <a id="_cwkytk86huqs"></a>__PHASE 2: THE "BEFORE" \- THE ACHE OF ABSENCE ARCHAEOLOGY__

Generate 4\-5 questions focused on the state *before* the longing was resolved:

- Questions that uncover their personal experience with the specific feeling of absence related to \{content\_idea\}\.
- Questions that reveal the deep emotional cost of that yearning\.
- Questions that connect their "before" state to the audience's current fears from \{context\_premise\}\. Include 1\-2 __pattern\-matching probes__ using your selected references from \{deep\_research\}\.

#### <a id="_64l914iqtpsz"></a>__PHASE 3: THE STRUGGLE \- "FLAWED PURSUIT" ARCHAEOLOGY__

Create 5\-6 questions that:

- Explore the specific, conventional ways they tried to satisfy their longing, which ultimately failed\.
- Mirror the audience's own "noble failure" patterns of chasing superficial substitutes from \{context\_premise\}\.
- Reference why these flawed pursuits are so common now \(using \{fresh\_research\}\)\.
- Uncover the moment they realized that chasing the *thing* wasn't satisfying the *feeling*\.

#### <a id="_9v8ke09hbv14"></a>__PHASE 4: THE REVELATION \- "DEEPER NEED" ARCHAEOLOGY__

Design 4\-5 questions that:

- Seek to capture the sensory and emotional details of the moment they understood the true, deeper need behind their longing\.
- Challenge common misconceptions about what our desires are really telling us\.
- Connect to the audience's breakthrough barriers from \{context\_premise\}\.

#### <a id="_aykmrsedu1ue"></a>__PHASE 5: THE "AFTER" \- UNEXPECTED FULFILLMENT ARCHAEOLOGY__

Create 4\-5 questions that:

- Explore the specific, tangible ways their daily life and internal state are different now\.
- Connect to what the audience truly desires from a feeling of fulfillment, based on \{context\_premise\}\.
- Uncover the unexpected emotional reality of what it feels like to have that deeper need met\.

#### <a id="_d4t08s1av6ty"></a>__PHASE 6: WISDOM EXTRACTION \- ACTIONABLE INSIGHTS__

Generate 3\-4 questions that:

- Distill the core lesson learned about the nature of desire and true fulfillment\.
- Address the audience's immediate next steps for understanding their own longings\.
- Provide hope for their own journey to find peace\.

__OUTPUT REQUIREMENTS__

### <a id="_4xfm5z1voi6n"></a>__1\. CUSTOMIZED INTERVIEW GUIDE__

- __Content\-Specific Opening Frame__ \(unique to \{content\_idea\}\)
- __Dynamic Permission Questions__ \(2\-3 options tailored to the theme\)
- __Phase\-by\-Phase Question Sets__ \(completely customized to the content and audience\)
- __Strategic Reference Integration__ \(natural weaving of \{deep\_research\} examples\)

### <a id="_p6h44vvsmtni"></a>__2\. INTERVIEW INTELLIGENCE BRIEF__

- __Content Theme Analysis__: What makes this story of longing unique?
- __Audience Psychology Map__: How does this connect to their \{context\_premise\}?
- __Research Power Points__: Key examples and current context to leverage\.
- __Emotional Excavation Strategy__: What specific feelings of yearning, disappointment, revelation, and peace to uncover\.

### <a id="_7jdpieqemxj8"></a>__3\. SUCCESS INDICATORS__

- __Story Authenticity Markers__: What genuine emotion \(e\.g\., a wistful sigh, a moment of quiet understanding, peaceful acceptance\) should emerge?
- __Audience Relevance Checkpoints__: How will this resonate with \{context\_premise\}?
- __Content Differentiation__: What will make this story of longing unique in the market?

__QUALITY BENCHMARKS__ ✅ __Content Specificity__: Questions are laser\-focused on the \{content\_idea\} theme\. ✅ __Audience Relevance__: Every question connects to \{context\_premise\} psychology\. ✅ __Research Integration__: Natural use of \{deep\_research\} and \{fresh\_research\}\. ✅ __Dynamic Variability__: No generic questions \- everything is customized\. ✅ __Emotional Excavation__: Questions designed to uncover deep, authentic feelings\. ✅ __Strategic Flow__: Progressive deepening from surface to core insights\.

__TONE EXTRACTION GUIDE__ Extract the interviewing tone and style from \{stream\_of\_consciousness\}:

- How do you naturally talk about desire and fulfillment?
- What's your authentic curiosity style about the human heart?
- How do you create an environment for discussing deep, vulnerable feelings? Adapt all questions to match this authentic voice while maintaining strategic intent\.
