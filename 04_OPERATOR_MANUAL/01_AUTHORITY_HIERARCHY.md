# Authority Hierarchy

1. Activative Intelligence & Visual Narrative Constitution V1.1

3. Accepted ADRs and technical specifications aligned to that PRD
4. Shared canonical contracts owned by Delegation
5. Epics, stories and implementation plans

A lower layer cannot silently override a higher layer. A contradiction becomes an explicit blocker or amendment.
