# PRD-CMF-01 - Strategy, Scope, and Release Gates

## Module Purpose

This module defines the product center of CMF STUDIO. CMF STUDIO is an interview-first expression operating system, not a generic content generator, clipping tool, or editor-first media app. Its core product claim is that source expression quality is determined upstream, before editing starts, through research saturation, Context Premise, Matrix of Edging, Narrative State Induction, Interview Asset Contracts, and guided Complete Expression Sessions.

The module also fixes the release philosophy: CMF STUDIO does not operate on MVP framing. The release candidate is the full documented system required to complete one governed brand cycle without developer intervention, manual database edits, direct provider tinkering, hidden prompt surgery, direct legacy runtime imports, or publication without human approval.

## Product Requirements

### PR-CMF-01.01 Interview-First Product Center

The product must begin from research and expression induction rather than post-production extraction. Operators need tools that help them prepare better interviews, surface authentic guest tension, avoid centroid answers, and convert source expression into routeable media assets with lineage.

The system must support both levels of extraction:

- extraction from the guest through live Narrative State Induction, interviewer resonance, question design, contrast pressure, and emotional access;
- extraction from transcript and source artifacts through grounded Expression Moment detection, primitive activation, route selection, and evaluation.

### PR-CMF-01.02 Full-System Release Candidate

The smallest releasable unit is a coherent full system that supports:

- workspace, tenant, role, and commercial governance;
- consent, source, likeness, and voice governance;
- legacy migration and registry conversion;
- Brand Genesis and Brand Context locking;
- research, Context Premise, Emotional DNA, Voice DNA, Matrix of Edging, and Interview Asset Contracts;
- Complete Expression Sessions and post-session extraction;
- archetype and asset derivative routing;
- Complete Editing Sessions, SceneSpecs, composition JSON, asset research, rendering, and assembly;
- evaluation, human review, approval, publishing intent, memory, operations, and recovery.

No public release is allowed until one complete brand cycle passes these gates.

### PR-CMF-01.03 Commercial Scope

Customer-facing content charging must stay limited to:

- `$29/week` trial Guest Asset Packs;
- `$99/month` Monthly Asset Engine.

Internal entitlement, quota, usage, worker, cost, and recovery systems may exist, but the product must not introduce extra customer-facing content tiers, newsletters, or unsupported package promises.

### PR-CMF-01.04 Legacy Continuity Without Runtime Coupling

CMF STUDIO is a greenfield continuity project. Legacy documents, code, prompts, registries, methodology, templates, and tests are sources of intelligence. They are not production runtime dependencies unless migrated through typed contracts, fixtures, worker assets, or evaluation targets.

Legacy value must be transformed into project-owned CMF modules, registries, contracts, and tests. The old CCP PRDs are lineage evidence under `reference/conscious-rivers/`, not canonical CMF product modules.

### PR-CMF-01.05 Human Approval Authority

The Operator and reviewer remain final authority for public release. Models, agents, provider adapters, critics, and Pi orchestration may propose, evaluate, and route work, but they cannot publish, approve their own output, bypass consent, mutate locked Brand Context Versions, or skip evidence obligations.

## Success Criteria

- One Operator completes onboarding, consent, Brand Genesis, interview intelligence, Complete Expression Session, extraction, routing, Complete Editing Session, rendering, review, approval, Publer scheduling, and memory update without developer intervention.
- All high-value legacy assets have migration disposition, target contract, fixture target, evaluation target, reviewer, known defects, and content hash.
- Every approved asset has source lineage, Brand Context version, route receipt, evaluation receipt, and human approval.
- Provider outage, GPU worker failure, workflow interruption, and projection rebuild tests pass without manual database edits.

## Release Blockers

- direct legacy runtime import;
- hidden prompt stack with no registry or compiler record;
- untyped production payload;
- customer-facing pricing outside `$29/week` and `$99/month`;
- newsletter deliverable;
- final publication without explicit human approval;
- Neo4j used as canonical business state;
- TypeScript shadow contracts defining domain semantics.
