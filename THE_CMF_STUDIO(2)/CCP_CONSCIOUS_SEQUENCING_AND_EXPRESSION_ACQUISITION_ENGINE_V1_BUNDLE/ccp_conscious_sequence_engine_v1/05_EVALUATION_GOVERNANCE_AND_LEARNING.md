# Evaluation, Governance, and Learning

## 1. Pre-interview gates

### Research readiness
- Brand Context pinned.
- Guest Dossier sufficient.
- Audience Reality current.
- Context Premises source-supported.
- Interviewer Resonance captured.
- Matrix of Edging reviewed.

### Procurement readiness
- Asset portfolio intent declared.
- Each target asset has a sequence hypothesis or explicit exploratory status.
- Required ingredients identified.
- Each required ingredient has at least one acquisition instrument or approved non-interview source.
- Safety-sensitive territories identified.

---

## 2. Live coverage gates

The system evaluates:

- coverage quantity;
- quality estimate;
- guest comfort;
- state integrity;
- repetition;
- missing mandatory ingredients;
- whether a cue is appropriate now.

Live quality estimates are provisional and may never override human judgment.

---

## 3. Inventory gates

Each ingredient should be scored for:

```text
specificity
emotional density
truth density
audience relevance
clipability
explanatory completeness
evidence strength
visual routeability
source integrity
brand fit
```

Hard failures:

- unclear speaker;
- unverifiable synthetic text presented as guest speech;
- missing timestamps;
- distorted quotation;
- raw unresolved trauma routed without safety approval;
- source-integrity failure.

---

## 4. Sequence gates

### Perceptual entry
- distinctive but brand-consistent;
- not dependent on generic visual noise;
- immediately legible for the format.

### Relevant open question
- clear;
- audience-relevant;
- source-supported;
- payoff promise specific enough to evaluate.

### Active prediction
- details progressively narrow understanding;
- no random information dumping;
- no deceptive head fake;
- confusion remains below threshold.

### Truthful payoff
- loop closure present;
- answer is non-trivial;
- grounded in captured expression or approved evidence;
- useful or emotionally resolving.

### Human affinity
- authentic human cue preserved;
- no manufactured vulnerability;
- Voice DNA and Negative Space pass.

### Expected future value
- assessed at package / series level;
- category promise is coherent;
- value is differentiated and repeated without monotony.

---

## 5. Ethical hard gates

Reject sequences that use:

- false open loops;
- fabricated conflict;
- unclosed promised answers;
- misleading transcript rearrangement;
- trauma sensationalization;
- fabricated urgency;
- synthetic guest claims;
- clickbait that violates the payoff contract.

---

## 6. Approval workflow

```text
Auto-eval
→ sequence preview
→ operator review
→ typed revision commands
→ recompile
→ final approval
→ frozen program
→ render
→ final sequence receipt
```

Operator commands include:

```text
REPLACE_INGREDIENT
REORDER_BEATS
CHANGE_SEQUENCE_PATTERN
CLOSE_LOOP_EARLIER
REMOVE_FALSE_TENSION
REDUCE_EMOTIONAL_AMPLITUDE
CHANGE_AFFINITY_CUE
CHANGE_FUTURE_VALUE_SIGNAL
REQUEST_PICKUP
WAIVE_INGREDIENT_REQUIREMENT
```

Every revision creates a new program version.
