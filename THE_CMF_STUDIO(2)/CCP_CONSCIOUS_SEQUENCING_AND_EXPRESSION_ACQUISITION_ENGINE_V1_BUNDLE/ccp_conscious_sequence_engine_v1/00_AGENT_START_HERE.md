# Agent Start Here

## Mission

Implement a Python-first, schema-driven **Conscious Sequencing & Expression Acquisition Engine** for CCP Studio.

The engine must solve two linked problems:

1. **Before and during an interview:** determine which semantic, emotional, evidentiary, visual, and brand-continuity ingredients must be acquired for the intended asset portfolio, then track whether the interview actually captured them.
2. **After the interview:** compile approved captured ingredients into viewer-state sequences for videos, carousels, single-image posts, polls, reactions, packages, and series.

## Non-negotiable distinctions

### Interview-State Sequence
Controls the guest's journey through safety, memory, vulnerability, authority, teaching, humor, and invitation.

### Viewer-State Sequence
Controls the audience's journey through perceptual entry, relevant open question, active prediction, truthful payoff, human affinity, and expected future value.

### Package / Series Sequence
Controls how multiple assets build recognition, trust, category ownership, participation, and future-value expectation over time.

These sequences interact, but they are not the same and must not share one flattened timeline.

## Core law

A final sequence may only use ingredients that are:

- captured from a source session;
- imported from an approved evidence or visual source;
- retrieved from approved Brand Memory;
- explicitly requested as a pickup;
- or marked as a non-human contextual asset.

The sequence compiler may not fabricate a missing human truth to complete a recipe.

## Runtime authority

- Python + Pydantic: contracts and semantic source of truth.
- DSPy: structured compilation, extraction, ranking, and eval programs.
- Pi Coding Agent: orchestration through typed tools and commands.
- TypeScript: PWA, Telegram Mini App, composition consumers, and renderers only.

## Required boot order

1. Load Brand Context Version and doctrine bundle.
2. Load Guest Dossier, Audience Reality Brief, Interviewer Resonance Context, Context Premises, and Matrix of Edging.
3. Compile Asset Portfolio Intent.
4. Generate provisional Sequence Hypotheses.
5. Derive Expression Acquisition Plan and Interview Asset Contracts.
6. Run state-aware live coverage tracking during the Complete Expression Session.
7. Extract and evaluate an Expression Ingredient Inventory.
8. Compile final Content Sequence Programs.
9. Hand structured programs to composition and CMF render engines.
10. Evaluate, approve, publish, and learn.

## Forbidden shortcuts

- Do not treat the interview as an unstructured footage source.
- Do not force the guest to follow the final viewer sequence.
- Do not turn ingredient coverage into an interrogation checklist.
- Do not use false open loops or fabricate conflict.
- Do not let post-production create a meaning the guest did not express.
- Do not score expected future value from one isolated asset.
- Do not use the term “dopamine” as a measurable runtime variable.
