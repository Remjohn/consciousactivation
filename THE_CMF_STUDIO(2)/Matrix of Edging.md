# Matrix of Edging

## 1. Purpose

This document rewrites the older Matrix of Edging into a proper CCP source of truth.

The old instinct was correct:

- content becomes powerful when it is built around live tension
- flat output comes from centroid behavior
- research matters because it helps locate what actually cuts

But the older framing was still too loose in three ways:

1. it treated edges too close to first-order building blocks
2. it did not distinguish broad pre-trigger signal from sharper post-trigger execution force
3. it did not anchor edging to the actual CCP production sequence

This rewrite fixes that.

The new position is:

**edging is not a gimmick. It is the architecture of controlled tension selection.**

Its job is to help CCP:

- find the strongest broad pressure worth bringing to the coach
- get truth-rich reaction instead of generic answers
- transform authentic material into measurable primitive coalitions
- route the resulting force into the real CCF and CMF workflows

That remains the primary role.

But the current architecture now adds a secondary clarification:

- edging is mainly a `meaning-plane` doctrine
- experience primitives are the main `product-experience` doctrine

Even so, edging still matters for experience design whenever the product needs to decide:

- what deserves attention now
- what social pressure is alive enough to click
- what contradiction makes a score reveal meaningful
- what tension makes a share prompt or debate invitation worth entering

---

## 2. The Core Definition

An edge is not just “something interesting.”

An edge is:

- a meaningful boundary
- under tension
- with enough magnitude to change attention, recognition, or belief

Edges appear where:

- expectation and reality separate
- identity and behavior diverge
- norms crack
- hidden truths surface
- opposing values collide
- public language fails to contain private experience

So the right definition is:

**an edge is a high-magnitude tension site at a meaningful human boundary.**

This is why edges matter more than generic “topics.”
Topics are categories.
Edges are where force lives.

---

## 3. The Most Important Correction

The most important architectural correction is this:

**primitives are not edges.**

Primitives are better understood as:

- meaning spaces
- transformation labs
- perception operators
- candidate generators

Edges are what emerge when:

- real evidence
- primitive activations
- and weighted interactions

combine into a live tension object.

So the correct ontology is:

`research field -> primitive spaces -> candidate survival -> coalition signature -> edge product`

This is the difference between:

- basis
- composition
- emergence

If we mistake products for primitives, we get false coalitions and weak measurement.

---

## 4. The Two Edge Phases

The system should always distinguish two separate edge phases.

### Phase A: Broad Primary Signal

This is the pre-trigger edge phase.

It happens before the coach inbox.

Its job is:

- to find the strongest broad pressure in the current research field
- to provoke authentic coach reaction
- to avoid over-shaping the coach’s response too early

This first signal must be:

- relevant
- timely
- emotionally charged
- broad enough to leave room for surprise

It should be a pressure cue, not a conclusion.

If it is too narrow, the coach performs to the theory.
If it is too weak, the coach answers politely instead of reactively.

### Phase B: Edge Product Formation

This is the post-trigger edge phase.

It happens after the coach response has been captured and authenticated.

Its job is:

- to identify stronger primitive activations inside real material
- to generate candidate transforms
- to form a coalition signature
- to produce an edge product sharp enough for execution

So the governing law is:

**the first edge should be broad enough to elicit truth; the second edge should be sharp enough to organize force.**

---

## 5. Edging, attention, and friction

The older instinct behind edging was mostly content-side.
The newer architecture should widen that slightly.

Edge pressure does not only help scripts cut harder.
It also helps experiences feel more alive.

In practice, the same pressure logic can improve:

- topic brief relevance
- urgency framing
- score reveal significance
- comeback prompts
- share prompts
- debate invitations
- challenge continuation moments

This does not mean edging replaces the experience primitive registry.

The split is cleaner than that:

- edging governs `tension selection`
- experience primitives govern `tension delivery`

So the current doctrine is:

**Matrix of Edging selects the meaningful human pressure; experience primitives decide how that pressure should be timed, framed, softened, intensified, or replayed.**

That is how edging now contributes to behavioral attention and friction management without swallowing the whole product-experience architecture.

---

## 6. Why Research Must Come First

CCP should not start edging only after Trigger-First.

The research phase is already where the earliest edge work begins.

Why:

- the coach inbox must carry something worthy of reaction
- generic research produces generic prompts
- generic prompts produce weak voice notes
- weak voice notes poison the whole downstream chain

So research has to do more than gather relevant facts.
It has to help find:

- active contradictions
- live mechanism pressures
- tribal irritations
- near-native what-is / what-could-be gaps
- latent but evidence-supported tensions

This means the pre-trigger sequence should be:

`CRAL / research field -> broad primary signal extraction -> provocation design -> coach reaction`

Not:

`research -> wait for coach -> then start thinking about edges`

Research is where the first edge pressure is found.

---

## 7. Primitive Spaces

A primitive space is a stable meaning lab.

It reads a research field or authenticated response and asks:

- what kind of live transform is available here?

Examples already grounded in the audit library include:

- `Irony Inversion`
- `Tribal Reference`
- `What-Is / What-Could-Be`
- `Analogy Bridge`
- `Vulnerable Specificity`
- `One-Sentence Lens`
- `Stakes as Personal Why`
- `Throughline`
- `Strong Title as Idea Architecture`
- `Connection Before Content`

Primitive spaces should not be treated as finished content.
They are upstream operators.

Each primitive space should be able to detect:

- native material already present
- latent material almost present
- JIT-completable material that remains faithful
- unsupported invention that must be rejected

This is crucial because many strong pieces are not fully sitting in the evidence as ready-made lines.
They are present as steep semantic gradients.

---

## 8. Candidate Survival

Each primitive space should produce several candidate transforms.

The system should then score which candidates survive.

This survival logic is what protects edging from becoming hallucinated cleverness.

A surviving candidate should be strong in:

- evidence fidelity
- emotional charge
- recognizability
- tribal density
- freshness
- speakability
- execution potential

And it should not be too costly to complete.

This is why the distinction between:

- native
- latent
- JIT-completable
- unsupported

matters so much.

The system should be allowed to complete a strong latent potential.
It should not fabricate a weak unsupported one.

---

## 9. Coalition Signatures

Once candidates survive, they form a coalition signature.

This is the most important measurable object in the edging architecture.

A coalition signature is not merely:

- which primitive fired

It is:

- which candidates survived
- how strongly
- in what family ratios
- with what synergy or redundancy
- and how routeable that combined geometry is

This is why coalition benchmarking is stronger than only tracking:

- which archetype performed best
- which framework performed best
- which single edge looked strongest

Because coalitions tell us the actual geometry of force.

The right future question is not:

- “what primitive wins most?”

It is:

- “what family and candidate geometries survive reality best at this workflow stage?”

That is where the real moat lives.

---

## 10. Edge Products

The edge product is the emergent tension object that the coalition becomes.

It is not the primitive list itself.

It is the resulting force that the audience will later feel.

Examples:

- a recognition edge
- a humor-exposure edge
- a transformation-pressure edge
- a mechanism-reveal edge
- an empathy-rotation edge
- an authority-sharpening edge

Edges should therefore be treated as products, not as the first ontology layer.

That preserves:

- basis clarity
- coalition measurement
- execution flexibility

---

## 11. The Real Matrix

The old version of the file implied that the matrix was a list of angle types.

The new matrix should be understood as a **stage-by-stage force matrix**.

| Stage | Main Object | Main Question |
|---|---|---|
| Research | Broad signal | What pressure is most alive right now? |
| Provocation | Reaction design | What will make the coach speak for real? |
| Authentication | Truth gate | Did we get real source material or performance? |
| Primitive pass | Candidate field | What meaning transforms are truly available here? |
| Coalition pass | Coalition signature | What combination organizes the strongest force? |
| Edge pass | Edge product | What is the actual tension object we can now deliver? |
| Routing pass | Execution route | Which CCF / CMF path can cash this out best? |
| Benchmark pass | Survival memory | Which geometries survive publication and use? |

That is the real Matrix of Edging.

Not static categories.
But the movement of force through the system.

---

## 12. Anti-Centroid Doctrine

The entire point of edging is to defend the system from centroid behavior.

Centroid behavior looks like:

- broad plausibility
- safe coverage
- multiple weak points instead of one live wire
- “nice” content that contains no pressure
- surface coherence with no real cut

RLHF-style flattening is dangerous here because it smooths exactly the boundary tensions that make content worth consuming.

So the edging layer must always optimize for:

- salience
- force concentration
- pressure integrity
- anti-flattening preservation

This does not mean reckless extremity.
It means protecting meaningful tension from polite dilution.

---

## 13. Handoff into experience primitives

The current split-registry architecture means this file should no longer imply that it governs the whole product experience by itself.

The handoff works like this:

- `Matrix of Edging` finds where the meaningful pressure lives
- `Experience_Primitive_Registry_Spec` decides how that pressure should be presented, timed, softened, intensified, scored, and replayed

Examples:

- a hot topic brief uses edging to identify the charged contradiction, then experience primitives to make the brief obvious, premium, and easy to enter
- a debate invitation uses edging to identify the real side-taking pressure, then experience primitives to make voting and sharing feel socially natural
- a redemption prompt uses edging to identify unresolved tension, then experience primitives to make comeback behavior feel safe instead of punishing

So the clean architectural position is:

**edging is the force-selection layer; experience primitives are the force-delivery layer.**

---

## 14. Coalition Fatality

Coalition fatality is what happens when a coalition appears strong in theory but dies in real execution.

Fatality manifests as:

- local charge but poor routeability
- hook without body support
- novelty without truth density
- redundancy masquerading as richness
- tension that cannot survive the chosen format
- flattening during script conversion

Fatality is a core part of the matrix because it tells us:

- which primitive combinations are deceptive
- which family overlaps are unproductive
- which route choices destroy force

A mature edging system should log fatality explicitly and treat it as valuable learning data.

---

## 15. Relationship to Current CCP Flow

For CCF, the current best insertion points are:

- before provocation generation in `ccf-batch`
- inside blueprint distillation

For CMF, the main value appears later:

- quote extraction prioritization
- beat clustering
- premise assembly
- visual and sonic reveal design

For CBCS and Voice systems, the main value is:

- broad signal selection
- cleaner truth extraction
- better relational and performance routing

For `Conscious Reactions` and other product surfaces, the main value is more selective:

- better topic pressure selection
- better debate seeding
- better share-prompt force
- better comeback tension framing

This means edging does not replace the existing CCP spine.

It upgrades:

- what the coach is asked
- what is extracted
- what is selected
- what is routed

---

## 16. The Build Order

The correct build order is:

### Step 1

Implement broad primary signal extraction from research.

### Step 2

Implement primitive candidate generation and survival scoring from authenticated coach response.

### Step 3

Implement coalition signatures as explicit measurable objects.

### Step 4

Implement edge product labeling and route recommendation into the official CCF lattice.

### Step 5

Benchmark coalition survival and fatality across real outputs.

This preserves determinism while still increasing depth.

---

## 17. Governing Laws

### Law 1. Broad-First Law

The first edge must be broad enough to elicit truth.

### Law 2. Basis-First Law

Primitives must be modeled before edge products.

### Law 3. Survival Law

No candidate should route into content unless it survives evidence and execution scoring.

### Law 4. Coalition Law

Force is usually organized by weighted coalitions, not isolated primitives.

### Law 5. Routeability Law

A strong edge that cannot be routed into the official execution stack is not yet useful.

### Law 6. Anti-Centroid Law

The system must defend salience from flattening at every stage.

### Law 7. Benchmark Law

The platform should learn from coalition geometries and fatality patterns, not just from surface performance labels.

---

## 18. Final Position

The Matrix of Edging is no longer a loose list of rhetorical triggers.

It is now the doctrine for how CCP should:

- find broad signal
- provoke truth
- detect transformable meaning
- organize force through coalitions
- allow edge products to emerge
- route them into the real CCF and CMF engines
- and benchmark which geometries actually survive reality

The shortest accurate summary is:

**research finds the pressure field, broad signals make the coach react, primitive spaces extract usable transforms, coalition signatures organize force, edge products emerge from composition, and the official execution lattice turns that force into content.**
