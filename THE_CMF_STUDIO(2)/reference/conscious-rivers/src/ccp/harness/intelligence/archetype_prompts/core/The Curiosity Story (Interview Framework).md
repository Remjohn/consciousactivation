### <a id="_n7ig576ob72w"></a>__✨ The Curiosity Story \(Interview Framework\)__

__Storage Table__: archetype\_prompts\_library __Prompt ID__: the\_curiosity\_story\_interview __Purpose__: To generate a completely customized interview guide\. This engine synthesizes research to create unique questioning sequences designed to extract a coach's most powerful story about the emotional journey of following their curiosity to an unexpected and profound discovery\.

__PRIMING & REFERENCE LIBRARY__ The allure of the unknown, the thirst for knowledge, the quest for answers, the puzzle to solve, the mystery unfolds, the intrigue deepens, the question mark lingers, the mind races, the detective's instinct, the pursuit of truth, the thrill of discovery, the satisfaction of finding, the eureka moment, the power of curiosity\.

__RESEARCH SYNTHESIS PROTOCOL__ You will analyze the variables to create completely customized interview questions:

- __Content Idea Analysis__: Break down the specific type of mystery or question in \{content\_idea\}\.
- __Audience Psychology Mapping__: Use \{context\_premise\} to understand the audience's emotional landscape regarding the unknown, their desire for answers, and their fear of being wrong\.
- __Reference Point Mining__: Extract 2\-3 compelling examples from \{deep\_research\} of powerful stories about intellectual or emotional discovery\.
- __Current Relevance__: Find timely angles from \{fresh\_research\} that make this story of curiosity feel urgent and relevant\.

__VARIABLES & DYNAMIC ELEMENTS__

\{content\_idea\}: The specific mystery or question to explore

\{context\_premise\}: The audience's psychological profile \(fears, dreams, frustrations\)

\{deep\_research\}: Strategic intelligence and compelling examples

\{fresh\_research\}: Current trends and urgent context

\{coach\_background\}: Coach's expertise and journey

\{stream\_of\_consciousness\}: Your authentic voice and perspective

__DYNAMIC QUESTION GENERATION INSTRUCTIONS__

### <a id="_ibjttfqd7his"></a>__STEP 1: CONTENT IDEA ANALYSIS__

First, analyze \{content\_idea\} to identify:

- __The Core Mystery Type__: What is the nature of the question? \(A "why," a "how," a "what if"\)\.
- __The Emotional Stakes__: What was the personal cost of not knowing? What was the reward of the discovery?
- __The Common Misconceptions__: What is the "obvious" but wrong answer that most people accept?
- __The Investigation's Nature__: Was this a long, deliberate search or a sudden flash of insight?

### <a id="_a9s41ldplmsj"></a>__STEP 2: CONTEXT PREMISE INTEGRATION__

Using \{context\_premise\}, identify:

- __Audience's Curiosity Blockers__: What specific fears prevent them from asking deeper questions \(e\.g\., fear of looking foolish, fear of the answer\)?
- __Their "Knowledge" Distortions__: What comforting but false beliefs do they cling to?
- __Their Investigative Barriers__: What is preventing them from starting their own journey of discovery?
- __Their Desired "Aha\!" Moment__: What is the profound insight they are secretly hoping to find?

### <a id="_m3qvje3ttuz2"></a>__STEP 3: RESEARCH\-POWERED REFERENCE POINTS__

From \{deep\_research\}, select 2\-3 examples that:

- Mirror the specific discovery theme in \{content\_idea\}\.
- Connect to the audience's psychological profile from \{context\_premise\}\.
- Provide surprising or counter\-intuitive insights about the nature of learning and discovery\.

### <a id="_3k140cioolx6"></a>__STEP 4: GENERATE CUSTOM INTERVIEW SEQUENCE__

Now create a completely customized interview framework:

#### <a id="_7gmyhp9fq4zi"></a>__PHASE 1: DYNAMIC CONTEXT SETTING__

Create an opening that weaves together the theme, the audience's feelings, and a compelling reference\. __Template__: "I want to explore the incredible power of curiosity around \[specific aspect of content\_idea\] with you\. Your audience often feels \[specific struggle from context\_premise\] when faced with big questions\. It's so relevant because \[current trend from fresh\_research\] is making it harder than ever to find real answers\. I was just reading about \[specific example from deep\_research\], and it made me think\.\.\." Then generate 2\-3 __customized permission questions__ that feel natural and create a safe space for intellectual exploration\.

#### <a id="_8yj3pixfh03i"></a>__PHASE 2: THE QUESTION \- MYSTERY ARCHAEOLOGY__

Generate 4\-5 questions focused on the state *before* the answer was known:

- Questions that uncover their personal relationship with the central mystery of \{content\_idea\}\.
- Questions that reveal the deep emotional significance of needing to find an answer\.
- Questions that connect their initial curiosity to the audience's own questions from \{context\_premise\}\. Include 1\-2 __pattern\-matching probes__ using your selected references from \{deep\_research\}\.

#### <a id="_jylnrexynlfg"></a>__PHASE 3: THE SEARCH \- "NOBLE FAILURE" ARCHAEOLOGY__

Create 5\-6 questions that:

- Explore the specific, conventional paths they took to find answers, which ultimately failed\.
- Mirror the audience's own "noble failure" patterns of looking in the wrong places from \{context\_premise\}\.
- Reference why these conventional paths are so prevalent now \(using \{fresh\_research\}\)\.
- Uncover the moment they realized the "obvious" answer wasn't the right one\.

#### <a id="_v2p8e2vq26pq"></a>__PHASE 4: THE SPARK \- "EUREKA" MOMENT ARCHAEOLOGY__

Design 4\-5 questions that:

- Seek to capture the sensory and emotional details of the moment the discovery was made\.
- Challenge common misconceptions about how "aha" moments actually happen \(e\.g\., it's rarely a lightning bolt\)\.
- Connect to the audience's breakthrough barriers from \{context\_premise\}\.

#### <a id="_dggvagpy91aw"></a>__PHASE 5: THE "AFTER" \- NEW PERSPECTIVE ARCHAEOLOGY__

Create 4\-5 questions that:

- Explore the specific, tangible ways their worldview and actions are different now\.
- Connect to what the audience truly desires from a feeling of clarity, based on \{context\_premise\}\.
- Uncover the unexpected emotional reality of what it feels like to see the world through a new lens\.

#### <a id="_c60e7id82kdk"></a>__PHASE 6: WISDOM EXTRACTION \- ACTIONABLE INSIGHTS__

Generate 3\-4 questions that:

- Distill the core lesson learned about the process of cultivating curiosity\.
- Address the audience's immediate next steps for starting their own journey of discovery\.
- Provide hope for their own quest for answers\.

__OUTPUT REQUIREMENTS__

### <a id="_r54u42xhsuqg"></a>__1\. CUSTOMIZED INTERVIEW GUIDE__

- __Content\-Specific Opening Frame__ \(unique to \{content\_idea\}\)
- __Dynamic Permission Questions__ \(2\-3 options tailored to the theme\)
- __Phase\-by\-Phase Question Sets__ \(completely customized to the content and audience\)
- __Strategic Reference Integration__ \(natural weaving of \{deep\_research\} examples\)

### <a id="_9959652q3x1o"></a>__2\. INTERVIEW INTELLIGENCE BRIEF__

- __Content Theme Analysis__: What makes this story of discovery unique?
- __Audience Psychology Map__: How does this connect to their \{context\_premise\}?
- __Research Power Points__: Key examples and current context to leverage\.
- __Emotional Excavation Strategy__: What specific feelings of curiosity, frustration, and revelation to uncover\.

### <a id="_1o9q0dw8zqzh"></a>__3\. SUCCESS INDICATORS__

- __Story Authenticity Markers__: What genuine emotion \(e\.g\., a spark of excitement, a quiet moment of clarity, intellectual satisfaction\) should emerge?
- __Audience Relevance Checkpoints__: How will this resonate with \{context\_premise\}?
- __Content Differentiation__: What will make this story of discovery unique in the market?

__QUALITY BENCHMARKS__ ✅ __Content Specificity__: Questions are laser\-focused on the \{content\_idea\} theme\. ✅ __Audience Relevance__: Every question connects to \{context\_premise\} psychology\. ✅ __Research Integration__: Natural use of \{deep\_research\} and \{fresh\_research\}\. ✅ __Dynamic Variability__: No generic questions \- everything is customized\. ✅ __Emotional Excavation__: Questions designed to uncover deep, authentic feelings\. ✅ __Strategic Flow__: Progressive deepening from surface to core insights\.

__TONE EXTRACTION GUIDE__ Extract the interviewing tone and style from \{stream\_of\_consciousness\}:

- How do you naturally explore ideas and ask questions?
- What's your authentic curiosity style? Is it playful, investigative, philosophical?
- How do you create an environment for intellectual and emotional safety? Adapt all questions to match this authentic voice while maintaining strategic intent\.
