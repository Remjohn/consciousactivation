### <a id="_d5mg5jmp4pse"></a>__✨ The Discovery Story \(Interview Framework\)__

__Storage Table__: archetype\_prompts\_library __Prompt ID__: the\_discovery\_story\_interview __Purpose__: To generate a completely customized interview guide\. This engine synthesizes research to create unique questioning sequences designed to extract a coach's most powerful story about the emotional journey of uncovering a hidden truth or having a profound "eureka" moment\.

__PRIMING & REFERENCE LIBRARY__ Unveiling the unknown, the thrill of exploration, uncharted territories, the spark of curiosity, the joy of learning, the wonder of the world, the pursuit of knowledge, the satisfaction of finding, the eureka moment, the power of insight, the lightbulb flash, the mind\-expanding journey, the quest for answers, the revelation of truth\.

__RESEARCH SYNTHESIS PROTOCOL__ You will analyze the variables to create completely customized interview questions:

- __Content Idea Analysis__: Break down the specific type of discovery in \{content\_idea\} \(e\.g\., a hidden truth, a new perspective, a forgotten history\)\.
- __Audience Psychology Mapping__: Use \{context\_premise\} to understand the audience's emotional landscape regarding curiosity, confusion, and clarity\.
- __Reference Point Mining__: Extract 2\-3 compelling examples from \{deep\_research\} of powerful stories about investigation and revelation\.
- __Current Relevance__: Find timely angles from \{fresh\_research\} that make this story of discovery feel urgent and relevant\.

__VARIABLES & DYNAMIC ELEMENTS__

\{content\_idea\}: The specific discovery theme to explore

\{context\_premise\}: The audience's psychological profile \(fears, dreams, frustrations\)

\{deep\_research\}: Strategic intelligence and compelling examples

\{fresh\_research\}: Current trends and urgent context

\{coach\_background\}: Coach's expertise and journey

\{stream\_of\_consciousness\}: Your authentic voice and perspective

__DYNAMIC QUESTION GENERATION INSTRUCTIONS__

### <a id="_8tzwf78qkkcl"></a>__STEP 1: CONTENT IDEA ANALYSIS__

First, analyze \{content\_idea\} to identify:

- __The Core Discovery Type__: What was uncovered? \(A secret, a new method, a hidden pattern, a forgotten truth\)\.
- __The Emotional Stakes__: Why did finding this answer matter so much?
- __The Central Mystery__: What was the core question that needed to be answered?
- __The Investigation's Nature__: Was this a long, systematic search or a sudden, serendipitous find?

### <a id="_42dn5487irqw"></a>__STEP 2: CONTEXT PREMISE INTEGRATION__

Using \{context\_premise\}, identify:

- __Audience's Discovery Anxiety__: What specific fears do they have about being wrong or not knowing enough?
- __Their "Truth" Distortions__: What comforting myths do they cling to that this discovery might shatter?
- __Their Clarity Barriers__: What is preventing them from having their own "aha" moments?
- __Their Desired Revelation__: What is the "secret" they are desperately hoping to find?

### <a id="_8z2ueepgb9zs"></a>__STEP 3: RESEARCH\-POWERED REFERENCE POINTS__

From \{deep\_research\}, select 2\-3 examples that:

- Mirror the specific discovery theme in \{content\_idea\}\.
- Connect to the audience's psychological profile from \{context\_premise\}\.
- Provide surprising or counter\-intuitive insights about the process of finding truth\.

### <a id="_xshgonqp96x"></a>__STEP 4: GENERATE CUSTOM INTERVIEW SEQUENCE__

Now create a completely customized interview framework:

#### <a id="_40cikmsp4ed8"></a>__PHASE 1: DYNAMIC CONTEXT SETTING__

Create an opening that weaves together the theme, the audience's feelings, and a compelling reference\. __Template__: "I want to explore the nature of discovery around \[specific aspect of content\_idea\] with you\. Your audience often feels \[specific struggle from context\_premise\] when they're trying to find answers\. It's so relevant because \[current trend from fresh\_research\] is creating so much confusion\. I was just reading about \[specific example from deep\_research\], and it made me think\.\.\." Then generate 2\-3 __customized permission questions__ that feel natural and create a space for intellectual curiosity\.

#### <a id="_iiqe3ko98lr7"></a>__PHASE 2: THE MYSTERY \- QUESTION ARCHAEOLOGY__

Generate 4\-5 questions focused on the initial state of not knowing:

- Questions that uncover their personal relationship with the central question of \{content\_idea\}\.
- Questions that reveal the deep emotional significance of needing to find an answer\.
- Questions that connect their initial confusion to the audience's current state from \{context\_premise\}\. Include 1\-2 __pattern\-matching probes__ using your selected references from \{deep\_research\}\.

#### <a id="_12nc8ae1fxqc"></a>__PHASE 3: THE SEARCH \- INVESTIGATION ARCHAEOLOGY__

Create 5\-6 questions that:

- Explore the specific, often frustrating, journey of looking for answers\.
- Mirror the audience's "flawed investigation" patterns from \{context\_premise\}\.
- Reference why this search is so relevant now \(using \{fresh\_research\}\)\.
- Uncover the "dead ends" and "red herrings" they encountered along the way\.

#### <a id="_9ysp14ir6n88"></a>__PHASE 4: THE SPARK \- "EUREKA" MOMENT ARCHAEOLOGY__

Design 4\-5 questions that:

- Seek to capture the sensory and emotional details of the moment the discovery was made\.
- Challenge common misconceptions about how "aha" moments actually happen\.
- Connect to the audience's breakthrough barriers from \{context\_premise\}\.

#### <a id="_a2xok8brb7yt"></a>__PHASE 5: THE NEW REALITY \- CLARITY ARCHAEOLOGY__

Create 4\-5 questions that:

- Explore the specific transformation the new knowledge created in their life or work\.
- Connect to what the audience truly desires from a feeling of clarity, based on \{context\_premise\}\.
- Uncover the unexpected emotional reality of what it feels like to finally understand something\.

#### <a id="_tvaty59rscv2"></a>__PHASE 6: WISDOM EXTRACTION \- ACTIONABLE INSIGHTS__

Generate 3\-4 questions that:

- Distill the core lesson learned about the process of discovery and learning\.
- Address the audience's immediate next steps for cultivating their own curiosity\.
- Provide hope for their own journey to find clarity\.

__OUTPUT REQUIREMENTS__

### <a id="_jwvly7f4854a"></a>__1\. CUSTOMIZED INTERVIEW GUIDE__

- __Content\-Specific Opening Frame__ \(unique to \{content\_idea\}\)
- __Dynamic Permission Questions__ \(2\-3 options tailored to the theme\)
- __Phase\-by\-Phase Question Sets__ \(completely customized to the content and audience\)
- __Strategic Reference Integration__ \(natural weaving of \{deep\_research\} examples\)

### <a id="_mkjw6hw5820k"></a>__2\. INTERVIEW INTELLIGENCE BRIEF__

- __Content Theme Analysis__: What makes this story of discovery unique?
- __Audience Psychology Map__: How does this connect to their \{context\_premise\}?
- __Research Power Points__: Key examples and current context to leverage\.
- __Emotional Excavation Strategy__: What specific feelings of confusion, curiosity, and clarity to uncover\.

### <a id="_cmomveoihezs"></a>__3\. SUCCESS INDICATORS__

- __Story Authenticity Markers__: What genuine emotion \(e\.g\., excitement, relief, wonder\) should emerge?
- __Audience Relevance Checkpoints__: How will this resonate with \{context\_premise\}?
- __Content Differentiation__: What will make this story of discovery unique in the market?

__QUALITY BENCHMARKS__ ✅ __Content Specificity__: Questions are laser\-focused on the \{content\_idea\} theme\. ✅ __Audience Relevance__: Every question connects to \{context\_premise\} psychology\. ✅ __Research Integration__: Natural use of \{deep\_research\} and \{fresh\_research\}\. ✅ __Dynamic Variability__: No generic questions \- everything is customized\. ✅ __Emotional Excavation__: Questions designed to uncover deep, authentic feelings\. ✅ __Strategic Flow__: Progressive deepening from surface to core insights\.

__TONE EXTRACTION GUIDE__ Extract the interviewing tone and style from \{stream\_of\_consciousness\}:

- How do you naturally express curiosity?
- What's your authentic investigation style?
- How do you create an environment for intellectual exploration? Adapt all questions to match this authentic voice while maintaining strategic intent\.
