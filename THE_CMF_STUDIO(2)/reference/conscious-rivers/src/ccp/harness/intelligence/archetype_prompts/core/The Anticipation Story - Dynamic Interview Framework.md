### <a id="_4m4i08onthsv"></a>__✨ The Anticipation Story \- Dynamic Interview Framework__

__Storage Table__: archetype\_prompts\_library __Prompt ID__: the\_anticipation\_story\_interview __Purpose__: To generate a completely customized interview guide\. This engine synthesizes research to create unique questioning sequences designed to extract a coach's most powerful story about the emotional journey of waiting for a significant future event\.

## <a id="_ij18pa1gkopk"></a>__PRIMING & REFERENCE LIBRARY__

The thrill of the wait, the building excitement, the edge of the seat, the rising tension, the countdown begins, the moments before, the breath held in, the heart races, the eager eyes, the longing gaze, the hopeful heart, the power of dreams, the allure of the future, the promise of what's to come, the magic of anticipation\.

## <a id="_qf8g3q56vzcm"></a>__RESEARCH SYNTHESIS PROTOCOL__

You will analyze the variables to create completely customized interview questions:

- __Content Idea Analysis__: Break down the specific future event or outcome in \{content\_idea\}\.
- __Audience Psychology Mapping__: Use \{context\_premise\} to understand the audience's emotional landscape regarding waiting, hope, and uncertainty\.
- __Reference Point Mining__: Extract 2\-3 compelling examples from \{deep\_research\} of powerful stories of anticipation or suspense\.
- __Current Relevance__: Find timely angles from \{fresh\_research\} that make this story of anticipation feel urgent and relevant\.

## <a id="_aggbe4z1smd"></a>__VARIABLES & DYNAMIC ELEMENTS__

\{content\_idea\}: The specific future event or outcome to explore

\{context\_premise\}: The audience's psychological profile \(fears, dreams, frustrations\)

\{deep\_research\}: Strategic intelligence and compelling examples

\{fresh\_research\}: Current trends and urgent context

\{coach\_background\}: Coach's expertise and journey

\{stream\_of\_consciousness\}: Your authentic voice and perspective

## <a id="_kovcrycvct7n"></a>__DYNAMIC QUESTION GENERATION INSTRUCTIONS__

### <a id="_4bb23ljnpsdf"></a>__STEP 1: CONTENT IDEA ANALYSIS__

First, analyze \{content\_idea\} to identify:

- __The Core Anticipation Type__: What is being awaited? \(A result, a reunion, a decision, a beginning\)
- __The Emotional Stakes__: What are the best and worst possible outcomes?
- __The Central Tension__: What is the primary source of the suspense or excitement?
- __The Time Horizon__: Is this a long, slow wait or a short, intense one?

### <a id="_r5menn9jq83u"></a>__STEP 2: CONTEXT PREMISE INTEGRATION__

Using \{context\_premise\}, identify:

- __Audience's Anticipation Anxiety__: What specific fears do they have about waiting or the future?
- __Their Hope Distortions__: What unrealistic expectations might they have about the outcome?
- __Their Patience Barriers__: What makes the process of waiting difficult for them?
- __Their Desired Outcome__: What does the resolution they are dreaming of look and feel like?

### <a id="_w2wvyceau32t"></a>__STEP 3: RESEARCH\-POWERED REFERENCE POINTS__

From \{deep\_research\}, select 2\-3 examples that:

- Mirror the specific anticipation theme in \{content\_idea\}\.
- Connect to the audience's psychological profile from \{context\_premise\}\.
- Provide surprising or counter\-intuitive insights about the psychology of waiting\.

### <a id="_hcshz3m48usy"></a>__STEP 4: GENERATE CUSTOM INTERVIEW SEQUENCE__

Now create a completely customized interview framework:

#### <a id="_tub2wyw69cbe"></a>__PHASE 1: DYNAMIC CONTEXT SETTING__

Create an opening that weaves together the theme, the audience's feelings, and a compelling reference\.

__Template__: "I want to explore the powerful feeling of anticipation around \[specific aspect of content\_idea\] with you\. Your audience often feels \[specific struggle from context\_premise\] when they're waiting for something important\. It's fascinating because \[current trend from fresh\_research\] is making that feeling even more intense for people\. I was just studying \[specific example from deep\_research\], and it made me think\.\.\."

Then generate 2\-3 __customized permission questions__ that feel natural and specific to the theme of waiting\.

#### <a id="_qdxqgp2qaef0"></a>__PHASE 2: THE STAKES \- FUTURE VISION ARCHAEOLOGY__

Generate 4\-5 questions focused on the awaited event in \{content\_idea\}:

- Questions that uncover their personal vision of the best possible outcome\.
- Questions that reveal the deep emotional significance of this future moment\.
- Questions that connect their hopes to the audience's dreams from \{context\_premise\}\.

Include 1\-2 __pattern\-matching probes__ using your selected references from \{deep\_research\}\.

#### <a id="_sepewr67xgye"></a>__PHASE 3: THE WAIT \- TENSION & HOPE ARCHAEOLOGY__

Create 5\-6 questions that:

- Explore the specific emotional rollercoaster of the waiting period\.
- Mirror the frustrations and anxieties about uncertainty from \{context\_premise\}\.
- Reference why this period of waiting is so relevant now \(using \{fresh\_research\}\)\.
- Uncover the specific coping mechanisms they used during the wait\.

#### <a id="_48bnewrcsbnw"></a>__PHASE 4: THE FINAL MOMENT \- CLIMAX ARCHAEOLOGY__

Design 4\-5 questions that:

- Seek to capture the sensory details of the moment right *before* the outcome was revealed\.
- Challenge common misconceptions about how it feels to be on the verge of a life\-changing moment\.
- Connect to the audience's breakthrough barriers from \{context\_premise\}\.

#### <a id="_mxfbjimccfr9"></a>__PHASE 5: THE OUTCOME \- RESOLUTION ARCHAEOLOGY__

Create 4\-5 questions that:

- Explore the specific emotional flood \(relief, joy, shock\) when the wait was finally over\.
- Connect to what the audience truly desires from a resolution, based on \{context\_premise\}\.
- Uncover the unexpected emotional reality of the outcome \(was it what they imagined?\)\.

#### <a id="_g2myqvemw3s6"></a>__PHASE 6: WISDOM EXTRACTION \- ACTIONABLE INSIGHTS__

Generate 3\-4 questions that:

- Distill the core lesson learned about the process of waiting and anticipation\.
- Address the audience's immediate next steps for managing their own periods of anticipation\.
- Provide hope for their own desired outcomes\.
