### <a id="_p8f4bdy9mb0v"></a>__✨ The Inspiration Story \(Interview Framework\)__

__Storage Table__: archetype\_prompts\_library __Prompt ID__: the\_inspiration\_story\_interview __Purpose__: To generate a completely customized interview guide\. This engine synthesizes research to create unique questioning sequences designed to extract a coach's most powerful story about the emotional journey of finding an authentic spark of motivation in an unlikely place\.

__PRIMING & REFERENCE LIBRARY__ The spark of motivation, the call to action, the awakening of potential, the fuel for dreams, the power of example, the guiding light, the beacon of hope, the surge of determination, the will to succeed, the path to greatness, the heroes' journey, the legacy of inspiration, the ripple effect of change\.

__RESEARCH SYNTHESIS PROTOCOL__ You will analyze the variables to create completely customized interview questions:

- __Content Idea Analysis__: Break down the specific type of inspiration in \{content\_idea\} \(e\.g\., finding purpose, overcoming apathy, starting a movement\)\.
- __Audience Psychology Mapping__: Use \{context\_premise\} to understand the audience's emotional landscape regarding cynicism, motivation, and purpose\.
- __Reference Point Mining__: Extract 2\-3 compelling examples from \{deep\_research\} of powerful stories about finding an "unlikely spark\."
- __Current Relevance__: Find timely angles from \{fresh\_research\} that make this story of inspiration feel urgent and necessary\.

__VARIABLES & DYNAMIC ELEMENTS__

\{content\_idea\}: The specific inspiration theme to explore

\{context\_premise\}: The audience's psychological profile \(fears, dreams, frustrations\)

\{deep\_research\}: Strategic intelligence and compelling examples

\{fresh\_research\}: Current trends and urgent context

\{coach\_background\}: Coach's expertise and journey

\{stream\_of\_consciousness\}: Your authentic voice and perspective

__DYNAMIC QUESTION GENERATION INSTRUCTIONS__

### <a id="_jw5c514705pp"></a>__STEP 1: CONTENT IDEA ANALYSIS__

First, analyze \{content\_idea\} to identify:

- __The Core Inspiration Type__: What was sparked? \(A new project, a change in perspective, a renewed sense of purpose\)\.
- __The Emotional Stakes__: What was the cost of staying uninspired? What did the spark make possible?
- __The Common Misconceptions__: What do people get wrong about where to find motivation?
- __The Central Tension__: What was the primary force causing the initial apathy or cynicism?

### <a id="_elblf5n0ek05"></a>__STEP 2: CONTEXT PREMISE INTEGRATION__

Using \{context\_premise\}, identify:

- __Audience's Inspiration Anxiety__: What specific fears do they have about never finding their "spark"?
- __Their "Motivation" Distortions__: What unhealthy or cliché beliefs do they hold about what it takes to get inspired?
- __Their Apathy Barriers__: What is keeping them stuck in a state of inaction?
- __Their "Spark" Vision__: What does the feeling of true, authentic inspiration they are dreaming of look and feel like?

### <a id="_j4fpjwc1921"></a>__STEP 3: RESEARCH\-POWERED REFERENCE POINTS__

From \{deep\_research\}, select 2\-3 examples that:

- Mirror the specific inspiration theme in \{content\_idea\}\.
- Connect to the audience's psychological profile from \{context\_premise\}\.
- Provide surprising or counter\-intuitive insights about the nature of motivation\.

### <a id="_cmm950ohlped"></a>__STEP 4: GENERATE CUSTOM INTERVIEW SEQUENCE__

Now create a completely customized interview framework:

#### <a id="_kbcmvivrp9eh"></a>__PHASE 1: DYNAMIC CONTEXT SETTING__

Create an opening that weaves together the theme, the audience's feelings, and a compelling reference\. __Template__: "I want to explore the elusive nature of inspiration around \[specific aspect of content\_idea\] with you\. Your audience often feels \[specific struggle from context\_premise\] when they're feeling stuck\. It's so relevant because \[current trend from fresh\_research\] is making it even harder to find authentic motivation\. I was just reading about \[specific example from deep\_research\], and it made me think\.\.\." Then generate 2\-3 __customized permission questions__ that feel natural and create a safe space for discussing moments of apathy and inspiration\.

#### <a id="_rg99mshjp5rz"></a>__PHASE 2: THE "BEFORE" \- APATHY ARCHAEOLOGY__

Generate 4\-5 questions focused on the state *before* the spark was found:

- Questions that uncover their personal experience with feeling unmotivated or cynical in relation to \{content\_idea\}\.
- Questions that reveal the deep emotional cost of that apathy\.
- Questions that connect their "before" state to the audience's current fears from \{context\_premise\}\. Include 1\-2 __pattern\-matching probes__ using your selected references from \{deep\_research\}\.

#### <a id="_5q2e2njp6ldi"></a>__PHASE 3: THE SEARCH \- "NOBLE FAILURE" ARCHAEOLOGY__

Create 5\-6 questions that:

- Explore the specific, conventional, or cliché places they looked for inspiration, which ultimately failed\.
- Mirror the audience's own "noble failure" patterns of chasing inauthentic motivation from \{context\_premise\}\.
- Reference why these cliché sources are so prevalent now \(using \{fresh\_research\}\)\.
- Uncover the moment they realized that "forcing" inspiration wasn't working\.

#### <a id="_dlilxarad1fz"></a>__PHASE 4: THE SPARK \- "UNLIKELY SOURCE" ARCHAEOLOGY__

Design 4\-5 questions that:

- Seek to capture the sensory and emotional details of the moment the authentic spark was found\.
- Challenge common misconceptions about inspiration being a "lightning bolt" moment\.
- Connect to the audience's breakthrough barriers from \{context\_premise\}\.

#### <a id="_rhuw0zhhz12o"></a>__PHASE 5: THE "AFTER" \- PURPOSE ARCHAEOLOGY__

Create 4\-5 questions that:

- Explore the specific, tangible ways their daily actions and internal state are different now\.
- Connect to what the audience truly desires from a feeling of purpose, based on \{context\_premise\}\.
- Uncover the unexpected emotional reality of what it feels like to live with a renewed sense of direction\.

#### <a id="_8hob2pejpjjr"></a>__PHASE 6: WISDOM EXTRACTION \- ACTIONABLE INSIGHTS__

Generate 3\-4 questions that:

- Distill the core lesson learned about the nature of true, sustainable inspiration\.
- Address the audience's immediate next steps for creating an environment where their own spark can ignite\.
- Provide hope for their own journey to find purpose\.

__OUTPUT REQUIREMENTS__

### <a id="_7surzsudtd4s"></a>__1\. CUSTOMIZED INTERVIEW GUIDE__

- __Content\-Specific Opening Frame__ \(unique to \{content\_idea\}\)
- __Dynamic Permission Questions__ \(2\-3 options tailored to the theme\)
- __Phase\-by\-Phase Question Sets__ \(completely customized to the content and audience\)
- __Strategic Reference Integration__ \(natural weaving of \{deep\_research\} examples\)

### <a id="_ob0nia3oxlwa"></a>__2\. INTERVIEW INTELLIGENCE BRIEF__

- __Content Theme Analysis__: What makes this story of inspiration unique?
- __Audience Psychology Map__: How does this connect to their \{context\_premise\}?
- __Research Power Points__: Key examples and current context to leverage\.
- __Emotional Excavation Strategy__: What specific feelings of apathy, frustration, surprise, and purpose to uncover\.

### <a id="_otrzlytdoik5"></a>__3\. SUCCESS INDICATORS__

- __Story Authenticity Markers__: What genuine emotion \(e\.g\., quiet excitement, a sense of clarity, renewed energy\) should emerge?
- __Audience Relevance Checkpoints__: How will this resonate with \{context\_premise\}?
- __Content Differentiation__: What will make this story of inspiration unique in the market?

__QUALITY BENCHMARKS__ ✅ __Content Specificity__: Questions are laser\-focused on the \{content\_idea\} theme\. ✅ __Audience Relevance__: Every question connects to \{context\_premise\} psychology\. ✅ __Research Integration__: Natural use of \{deep\_research\} and \{fresh\_research\}\. ✅ __Dynamic Variability__: No generic questions \- everything is customized\. ✅ __Emotional Excavation__: Questions designed to uncover deep, authentic feelings\. ✅ __Strategic Flow__: Progressive deepening from surface to core insights\.

__TONE EXTRACTION GUIDE__ Extract the interviewing tone and style from \{stream\_of\_consciousness\}:

- How do you naturally talk about motivation and purpose?
- What's your authentic curiosity style about what truly inspires people?
- How do you create an environment for discussing moments of feeling lost? Adapt all questions to match this authentic voice while maintaining strategic intent\.
