# CMF Scene Components Definitions

This document is the canonical definition layer for the 18 CMF scene components. A component is a cinematic vehicle that may fill one or more valid containers.

## Scene 1: HOOK

- Function: direct pattern interrupt with explicit stakes.
- Best fit: `HOOK`

## Scene 2: SETUP

- Function: establish context, identity, and narrative footing.
- Best fit: `SETUP`

## Scene 3: CHALLENGE

- Function: make the problem feel active and costly.
- Best fit: `CHALLENGE`

## Scene 4: JUXTAPOSITION

- Function: create meaning by collision, contrast, or ironic sequence.
- Best fit: `HOOK`, `CHALLENGE`, `TURNING_POINT`

## Scene 5: TURNING POINT

- Function: deliver the explicit revelation beat.
- Best fit: `TURNING_POINT`

## Scene 6: RESOLUTION

- Function: stabilize the emotional landing and show what changed.
- Best fit: `RESOLUTION`

## Scene 7: ENCOURAGING CHANGE

- Function: convert realization into hopeful movement.
- Best fit: `RESOLUTION`, `VISION`

## Scene 8: SYMBOLIC ECHO

- Function: reuse a transformed symbol to trigger closure recognition.
- Best fit: `RESOLUTION`

## Scene 9: FRAME & CONTRAST

- Function: make the binary conflict legible through comparison.
- Best fit: `CHALLENGE`

## Scene 10: THE TEASE

- Function: imply a payoff before fully revealing it.
- Best fit: `HOOK`

## Scene 11: VOICE OF TRUTH

- Function: use external validation or direct testimony to anchor belief.
- Best fit: `SETUP`, `TURNING_POINT`

## Scene 12: THE ARCHETYPAL MOMENT

- Function: activate mythic recognition through symbolic imagery.
- Best fit: `SETUP`, `TURNING_POINT`, `VISION`

## Scene 13: THE DEMONSTRATION

- Function: prove causality through visible mechanism or process.
- Best fit: `CHALLENGE`

## Scene 14: THE EVIDENCE

- Function: prove the claim with clean, low-noise proof.
- Best fit: `CHALLENGE`

## Scene 15: THE COMMUNITY

- Function: show belonging, tribe, and collective future-state.
- Best fit: `VISION`

## Scene 16: THE PAUSE

- Function: provide the silence container required after a peak.
- Best fit: `RESOLUTION`

## Scene 17: THE VIBE

- Function: carry tone, atmosphere, and aspirational texture.
- Best fit: `HOOK`, `SETUP`, `VISION`

## Scene 18: THE VISION

- Function: render the final transformed future and fuse it with action.
- Best fit: `VISION`
