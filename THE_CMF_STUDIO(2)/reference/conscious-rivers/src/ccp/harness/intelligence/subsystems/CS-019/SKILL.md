# CS-019 Skill

## Purpose

Apply Directional Semantics so movement direction reinforces the intended narrative meaning instead of encoding a contradictory one.

## Use This Skill When

- selecting push, pan, tilt, or drift directions
- designing empowerment or challenge reveals
- checking whether motion path matches scene semantics

## Required Inputs

- motion vector
- narrative function
- cultural profile
- scene role

## Decision Procedure

1. Determine the intended scene meaning.
2. Compare the planned motion direction against that meaning.
3. Reject vectors that subtly encode the opposite story.

## Runtime Guidance

- Direction is not neutral.
- Up and left-to-right often support lift and progress.
- Down and right-to-left often support resistance, weight, or challenge.
