# CS-017 - Temperature Arc

CS-017 exists because color temperature is not only a local scene property. It can also function as a longitudinal signal of movement through intimacy, confrontation, authority, release, and resolve.

The subsystem reads scene order, current and prior temperatures, scene role, and brand constraints. It succeeds when the thermal journey quietly reinforces the narrative progression. It fails when adjacent scenes are thermally flat or when temperature contradicts the intended scene meaning.

CS-017 works best alongside CS-016: PAD handles dimensional affect, while Temperature Arc handles thermal storytelling over time.

## Current CMF Thresholds

- minimum meaningful adjacent thermal delta: 600 K
- vulnerability target band: 3200 K to 4200 K
- challenge target band: 5000 K to 6000 K

## Good Example

The confession starts warm, the evidence sequence cools, and the resolution restores measured warmth.

## Failure Example

Every scene stays at the same thermal feel, so emotional progression has no temperature support.
