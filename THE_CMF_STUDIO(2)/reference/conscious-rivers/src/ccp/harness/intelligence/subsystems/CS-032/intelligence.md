# CS-032 - Silence Container

CS-032 exists because excitation transfer needs a physical vessel. A peak cannot convert into deeper feeling if the composition never becomes quiet enough for the residual arousal to settle into a new label. In CMF, silence or near-silence after a major peak is not an artistic indulgence. It is a technical requirement for emotional processing.

The subsystem reads the prior beat's CLS, the time since the peak, soundtrack density, dialogue density, and the visual intensity of the candidate pause. It succeeds when a clean low-CLS container exists soon after the peak. It fails when the composition continues to talk, score, or move through the recovery zone and leaves no place for emotion to land.

CS-032 should usually be consulted together with CS-008. CS-008 tells you whether the timing window exists. CS-032 tells you whether the window has a usable vessel inside it.

## Current CMF Thresholds

- trigger condition: preceding beat at CLS 4
- preferred receiving container: CLS 1 silence or near-silence beat
- insertion target: inside the 2 to 5 second post-peak window
- dense dialogue during the pause: not allowed

## Good Example

After a revelation, the timeline cuts into a held quiet beat with minimal score and no explanatory speech.

## Failure Example

The video moves directly from the peak into more narration, music lift, and overlay explanation, leaving no recovery vessel.
