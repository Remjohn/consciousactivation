# CS-026 Skill

## Purpose

Apply Identity Tracking so extra faces do not impose an unnecessary individuation burden on the viewer.

## Use This Skill When

- reviewing multi-face montages
- deciding whether a secondary face is functional or decorative
- protecting coach-face continuity across a short-form arc

## Required Inputs

- face count over time
- role tags
- shot prominence
- narrative necessity

## Decision Procedure

1. Count dominant faces across the sequence.
2. Separate necessary secondary faces from decorative additions.
3. Reduce identity load unless contrast or testimony genuinely requires it.

## Runtime Guidance

- One recognisable human anchor is the default.
- Extra faces need justification.
- Decoration does not justify individuation cost.
