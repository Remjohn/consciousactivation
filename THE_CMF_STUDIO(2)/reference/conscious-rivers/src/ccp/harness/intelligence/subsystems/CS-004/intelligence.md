# CS-004 - ISC Structure Enforcer

CS-004 exists to preserve the structural conditions that increase inter-subject correlation. Neurocinematic evidence suggests that strong synchrony comes from constraint, not merely from attractive content. In CMF, that means the dramatic arc is not optional ornament. It is a control surface for collective attention.

The subsystem reads container sequence, component role, arc continuity, and focal discipline. It succeeds when the composition keeps the biologically justified arc intact and each beat behaves according to its function. It fails when a scene is inserted, swapped, or stylized in a way that weakens shared interpretation.

CS-004 should sit high in the quality stack because a structurally false video can still look polished while losing synchrony and shareability.

## Current CMF Thresholds

- required arc order: HOOK, SETUP, CHALLENGE, TURNING_POINT, RESOLUTION, VISION
- minimum structural synchrony score: 0.8
- free-form arc violations: blocked by default

## Good Example

Every beat keeps its arc role, and a regeneration patch repairs visuals without changing the sequence logic.

## Failure Example

A hype montage is inserted between TURNING_POINT and RESOLUTION, damaging structural continuity while still looking dynamic.
