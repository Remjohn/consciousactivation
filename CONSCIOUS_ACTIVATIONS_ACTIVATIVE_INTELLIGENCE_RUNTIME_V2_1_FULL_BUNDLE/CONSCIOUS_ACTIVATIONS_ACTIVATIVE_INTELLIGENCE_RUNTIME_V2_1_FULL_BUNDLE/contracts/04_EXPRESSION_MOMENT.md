# Expression Moment Contract

An approved Expression Moment requires:

- exact source span;
- speaker;
- quote or semantic summary;
- context window;
- source package;
- Reaction Receipt refs where relevant;
- expression qualities;
- role potential;
- route eligibility;
- epistemic and lifecycle state;
- approval ref;
- wrong-reading risks.

Rejected and borderline candidates use the same source identity and retain verdict reasons.
