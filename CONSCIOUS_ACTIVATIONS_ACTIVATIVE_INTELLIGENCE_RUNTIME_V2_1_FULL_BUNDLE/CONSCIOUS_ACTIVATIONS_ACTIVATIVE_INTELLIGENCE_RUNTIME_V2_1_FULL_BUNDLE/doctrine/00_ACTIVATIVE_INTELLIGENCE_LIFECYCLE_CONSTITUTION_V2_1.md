## 1. The V2 correction in one sentence

Activative Intelligence is the versioned, evidence-bearing lifecycle that forms activation hypotheses, induces and observes human state transitions, resolves source expression, transfers the original charge into format-specific derivatives, observes audience roles and reactions, and learns only within explicit scope.

The shortest equation remains:

```text
AI compiles the activation field.
The human reacts.
The reaction becomes source expression.
```

V2 adds what happens around that sentence:

```text
Identity and audience understanding
→ diverse activation hypotheses
→ planned source / relationship / audience program
→ live observation
→ reaction evidence
→ expression resolution
→ transfer contract
→ derivative program
→ final asset
→ audience observation
→ human resolution
→ scoped learning
```

A static Activative Intelligence Pack is still useful, but it is one snapshot inside this lifecycle rather than the entire system.

---

## 2. Why V2.1 exists

V1 solved a foundational problem: AI-generated content becomes centroid when the system starts with a topic, prompt, transcript, or template and asks the model to invent the missing human pressure. V1 therefore placed identity pressure, Context Premise, Coach Identity DNA, roles, stances, participation traps, Visual Semantics, and format harnesses upstream.

That was necessary but insufficient.

A static pack cannot fully represent:

- the difference between what an interview intended and what the guest actually expressed;
- the changing state of a guest during a conversation;
- the pressure dose appropriate to the current moment;
- the evidence contained in silence, hesitation, breath, gaze, contradiction, and repair;
- the difference between a good answer and an approved Expression Moment;
- how activation survives or decays during editing and format translation;
- how a campaign manages role diversity and audience fatigue;
- how a human correction becomes durable programming material;
- where a learning applies and where it must not be generalized;
- whether a failure came from semantics, narrative, composition, generation, evaluation, or operator interpretation.

V2 therefore turns Activative Intelligence into a temporal control system.

## 5. The three activation domains

### 5.1 Source activation

Source activation is directed at the coach, guest, founder, expert, or participant whose reaction may become original expression.

Its purpose is to move the person from a current expression state into a state likely to produce useful, truthful, complete material.

Typical roles include:

- witness;
- storyteller;
- teacher;
- challenger;
- confessor;
- authority;
- inviter.

Source activation is evaluated by state transition, anchor hit, answer depth, complete premise, reaction tail, landing quality, and expression eligibility.

### 5.2 Relationship activation

Relationship activation is directed at a prospect or collaborator across a progressive-disclosure journey.

Its purpose is to create recognition, trust, micro-commitments, and an earned next step.

Typical stages include:

```text
observed
→ publicly recognized
→ replied
→ idea elevated
→ micro-committed
→ ReelCast proposed
→ scheduled
→ recorded
→ asset delivered
→ broader system revealed
```

Relationship activation is not identical to source activation. A public comment may activate a reply without yet inducing a deep expression state.

### 5.3 Audience activation

Audience activation is directed at viewers, listeners, readers, or community members consuming a derivative asset.

Its purpose is role entry and participation:

```text
“That is me.”
“That is them.”
“That is us.”
“That violates what matters.”
“That is what I lost.”
“That is who I want to become.”
“I need to complete this.”
```

Audience activation is evaluated through role evidence, reaction classes, transfer fidelity, wrong readings, retention, qualitative comments, shares, saves, and market behavior.

### 5.4 Domain law

The three domains share Identity DNA, Context Premise, Edge Pressure, roles, and stances. They do not share identical state machines, receipts, or success criteria.

Every activation program must declare its domain.

## 6. Activation epistemology

Every important activation claim declares its epistemic state.

The canonical states are:

- **planned** — intended before the event;
- **observed** — directly supported by recorded source evidence;
- **inferred** — model or analyst interpretation awaiting resolution;
- **operator_confirmed** — explicitly resolved by an authorized human;
- **rejected** — considered and denied for a recorded reason;
- **superseded** — replaced by a later additive decision while preserving history.

Examples:

- “Truth versus approval” can be a planned edge.
- A visible hesitation before answering can be an observed reaction.
- “Fear of rejection caused the hesitation” is inferred unless the guest says or confirms it.
- “The safer sentence is central to my method” becomes operator-confirmed when the coach resolves it.
- A catchy quote with reversed context is rejected.
- A revised audience segment supersedes an older one without deleting it.

Epistemic states must not collapse silently.

Inference cannot outrank observed or operator-confirmed evidence.

Rejected evidence stays available for negative retrieval and future re-evaluation within its operator-provided source authority and applicability scope.

## 7. Canonical source and dual admission

Every interview-derived campaign must have one Canonical Interview Source Package.

The source package binds:

- accepted source media;
- audio;
- transcript;
- speakers;
- word and phrase timestamps;
- source shot map;
- keyframes;
- planned tags;
- observed tags;
- Reaction Receipts;
- Anchor Hits;
- approved and rejected Expression Moments;
- derivative-use authority and route scope;
- provenance;
- versions and supersession history.

Two admission paths are first-class.

### 7.1 Brief-led admission

A planned Activative Interview Brief, Matrix of Edging, anchors, Interview Asset Contracts, intended states, and recording session enter together.

The package preserves planned versus observed fields separately.

An unmet planned edge remains unmet. It is never fabricated into the source.

### 7.2 Imported-source admission

An existing interview, talking-head video, transcript, or recording may enter without pretending that a Brief, planned anchors, or predeclared asset contracts existed.

Retrospective tags remain inferred until resolved.

Missing history is represented as absent, not synthesized.

### 7.3 Source-root law

Every derivative must resolve exact source objects through the source package. A model response, transcript summary, local edit, or prompt cannot become a substitute lineage root.

## 8. Identity DNA, Context Premise, Resonance, and Relationship State

### 8.1 Coach / Guest Identity DNA

Identity DNA maps what the person can credibly embody and activate:

- identity roles;
- lived proof;
- origin patterns;
- recurring premises;
- signature stances;
- enemy beliefs;
- emotional range;
- public fire;
- private fire;
- implied-only material;
- negative space;
- visual world;
- emerging identities;
- contradictions;
- source-backed amplifiers.

Canonical Identity DNA is human-owned.

Models may produce **Identity DNA Candidate Observations**. They may not silently mutate the canonical profile.

### 8.2 Context Premise

Context Premise is a versioned hypothesis about what the audience:

- wants;
- fears;
- hides;
- repeats;
- resents;
- believes;
- envies;
- avoids;
- recognizes;
- uses to cope;
- treats as success;
- treats as threat.

It is not a persona summary and not a fact.

It includes evidence, confidence, validity window, audience implication, question implication, visual implication, and friction-if-wrong.

### 8.3 Coach–Audience Resonance

Resonance maps the collision between what the coach can credibly carry and what the audience is ready to feel.

A high-resonance edge is not merely popular. It is both:

```text
audience-active
and
coach-owned
```

### 8.4 Interviewer Resonance

The interviewer is not a neutral transport layer.

Interviewer Resonance records:

- genuine curiosity;
- lived overlap;
- disbelief;
- recognition;
- personal stake;
- questions the interviewer cannot ask credibly;
- topics where their reaction may deepen the guest;
- topics where their reaction may distort the guest.

### 8.5 Relationship State

Comments, DMs, and invitations depend on what has already happened between people.

A first public comment cannot assume the same intimacy as a fifth exchange or a completed ReelCast.

The current relationship state must be part of relationship activation.

## 9. Activation Hypothesis Portfolio

V2 prohibits premature convergence on one “best” activation.

Before source, relationship, audience, or visual execution, the system should form a bounded portfolio of meaningfully different hypotheses.

Diversity axes include:

- activation direction;
- target role;
- identity urge;
- stance;
- edge;
- pressure dose;
- narrative state;
- source question route;
- recognition carrier;
- visual operator;
- format;
- smallest commitment;
- likely counteractivation.

Example portfolio:

```text
A — mirror recognition through regret
B — target mapping through moral violation
C — aspiration through dignity
D — contradiction through identity gap
E — curiosity through an incomplete consequence
```

Mechanical gates remove unsupported, duplicate, stale, overexposed, or wrong-scope candidates.

Independent comparative evaluation selects or escalates.

Rejected and repaired candidates remain in the portfolio history.

## 12. Narrative State Induction

The primary source-expression states are:

- **Cinematic** — scene, place, sequence, sensory detail, stakes;
- **Vulnerability** — internal cost, uncertainty, contradiction, exposed truth;
- **Authority** — clear stance, judgment, standard, belief;
- **Teaching** — mechanism, distinction, method, causal explanation;
- **Invitation** — next move, possibility, participation, decision.

States are not archetypes.

A transformation story may require Cinematic, Vulnerability, Authority, and Teaching states in sequence.

Narrative State Induction is a policy:

```text
observe current state
→ compare with target state
→ select bounded activative action
→ watch reaction
→ update state
→ continue, repair, reset, or land
```

The system must not repeatedly ask deeper questions when the person is closing, overperforming, confused, or counteractivated.

## 13. First-Line Anchor, Depth Anchor, and Landing

### 13.1 First-Line Anchor

The First-Line Anchor shapes the direction and energy of the answer before generic language takes over.

It may ask for:

- a scene;
- a sentence;
- a moment;
- a person;
- a choice;
- a specific consequence.

### 13.2 Depth Anchor

The Depth Anchor prevents a useful beginning from ending in centroid language.

It directs the answer toward:

- cost;
- contradiction;
- mechanism;
- bodily signal;
- exact example;
- identity stake;
- before/after difference;
- the part usually left unsaid.

### 13.3 Landing Evaluation

Landing is evaluated, not forced.

Possible outcomes:

- complete landing;
- partial landing;
- unexpected but valuable landing;
- flat answer;
- counteractivation;
- topic escape;
- unresolved contradiction;
- no activation;
- source-worthy but not derivative-ready;
- derivative-ready Expression Moment candidate.

## 19. Activation Transfer Fidelity

The purpose of derivatives is not merely to preserve information. It is to preserve enough of the original activation generator to induce the intended audience role.

An Activation Transfer Contract declares:

- source Expression Moments;
- original state and reaction generator;
- source role and audience role;
- invariants that must remain true;
- required changes;
- creative degrees of freedom;
- permitted compression;
- forbidden collapses;
- wrong-reading locks;
- target format;
- target audience segment;
- evaluation method.

Activation Transfer Fidelity asks:

> Did the derivative preserve the pressure, role, tension, and recognition structure that made the source expression alive?

A faithful quote can still be activatively dead if it removes the scene, contradiction, or reaction tail that carried the force.

## 22. Activation freshness and fatigue

Activation weakens when the audience recognizes the formula before feeling the emotion.

The system tracks:

- recently used linguistic structures;
- repeated inversion patterns;
- repeated visual operators;
- role saturation;
- edge saturation;
- audience exposure;
- format repetition;
- emotional direction repetition;
- novelty;
- surprise;
- prediction-error decay.

Freshness does not require novelty for its own sake.

It requires a new entrance into a still-relevant human pressure.

## 23. Counteractivation and dose

Strong emotion is not always correct activation.

Counteractivation includes:

- denial;
- reactance;
- shame shutdown;
- tribal defense;
- projection;
- moral outrage;
- misrecognition;
- performative agreement;
- target fixation;
- premature vulnerability;

Each program declares:

- desired role;
- probable defense role;
- pressure dose;

- affinity reset;

```text
recognition
→ specificity
→ contradiction
```

## 24. Relationship activation and progressive disclosure

Relationship activation moves through earned stages.

An activative comment should:

- recognize an actual idea;
- add a stance or edge;
- invite a meaningful answer;
- preserve the prospect’s authorship.

A reply may become new evidence.

The next move may elevate the message:

```text
“This is the real idea.”
“This deserves a fuller conversation.”
“This belongs in front of a specific community.”
```

The system then seeks a small commitment, closes into ReelCast or an interview, delivers value, and only later reveals the broader product.

Every relationship move must fit the current stage.

## 26. HumanResolutionEpisodes

Every meaningful human decision becomes a HumanResolutionEpisode.

An episode records:

- before candidate;
- exact human decision;
- reason;
- wrong reading;
- implicated layer;
- corrected direction;
- accepted replacement;
- applicability scope;
- operator-provided source authority and route scope;
- downstream invalidations;
- whether it is a learning candidate.

Examples from the development history include:

- rejecting a symbolic footprint loop because it communicates metaphor without lived activation;
- rejecting a triptych that collapses the premise into phone addiction;
- identifying gaze as a missing contract;
- rejecting repeated “You are not X” language because the formula became visible;
- lowering an inflated visual score from 7/10 to 3.5/10.

These are not conversational leftovers. They are programming material.

- admitted source-corpus evidence;
- empirical model evidence;
- external-product evidence;

# Part II — V2.1 Primitive, Archetype, Brand, Script, and Composition Integration

This Part is the V2.1 amendment. When it conflicts with an earlier V2 statement, this Part controls. It does not erase the V2 lifecycle. It completes the missing production-semantic layer.

## 35. The governing activation sentence

> **Content activates when it gives the viewer a psychological role inside a tension.**

Information, sentiment, polish, novelty, persuasion, and reaction intensity may support activation, but none is equivalent to activation. A derivative is not activative merely because it communicates a true point or produces a strong emotion. The asset must position the viewer as someone inside a meaningful tension: witness, judge, confessor, challenger, protector, aspirer, participant, member, dissenter, learner, or another explicitly declared role. The role must be built from source-backed pressure, not pasted onto the content after composition.

Every audience-facing activation program therefore declares:

| Required field | Constitutional question |
|---|---|
| psychological role | Who does the viewer become while encountering the asset? |
| tension | What unresolved pressure makes that role meaningful now? |
| recognition path | What lets the viewer recognize themselves, another person, or a system inside the tension? |
| stance | From what credible position does the source expression address the tension? |
| participation threshold | What is the smallest useful act available to the viewer? |
| counteractivation | Which defensive role may replace the intended one? |
| transfer invariant | What must survive every rewrite, crop, layout, edit, animation, and platform adaptation? |

A program that cannot answer these questions is descriptive, expressive, promotional, or decorative, but it is not yet an approved Activative program.

## 36. Interview-First and Expression-First laws

Conscious Activations begins from real expression whenever an interview source is available or intentionally created. The Brief-led interview and imported interview are equally legitimate source-admission paths, but their epistemic histories remain different. A Brief-led source can reference Planned Activative Intelligence, Interview Asset Contracts, live calls, and Reaction Receipts. An imported source declares those absent where they did not occur and begins from observed media, transcript, speakers, timestamps, keyframes, and operator-confirmed context.

Expression precedes derivative writing. The source package is not raw material to be rewritten freely until it resembles a content formula. The system first resolves what the human actually expressed, what created the expression, how complete the premise is, which reaction tail belongs to it, and which identity or audience pressure is evidenced. Only then may derivative programs be compiled.

## 37. Primitive-First doctrine

Primitive Families are the operative alphabet of Activative Intelligence. A Skill or Steering Recipe may coordinate work, but it cannot substitute for exact Primitive semantics. Every Primitive Binding resolves to one exact versioned Primitive YAML and carries:

- Primitive ID, plane, family, canonical name, and exact source hash;
- the core move that the Primitive performs;
- activation conditions and suppression conditions;
- misuse modes and incompatible uses;
- required inputs and observable effects;
- the semantic or experiential role assigned in the current program;
- the evidence that makes the binding applicable now.

Meaning Plane and Experience Plane remain distinct. Meaning Primitives shape recognition, tension, narrative, identity, language, visual semantics, and persuasion. Experience Primitives shape participation, progression, feedback, trust, commitment, and interaction. They may cooperate in one coalition, but their functions cannot be flattened into one undifferentiated list.

A model, agent, or retrieval result may propose a Primitive. It may not create a Primitive definition from memory, substitute a similar name, or ignore the exact YAML because the prose appears familiar.

## 38. Primitive Coalition Contract

The true Recipe builder is the Primitive coalition.

A Primitive Coalition Contract is required before an archetype coalition, Final Script, or composition becomes approvable. It identifies:

| Coalition element | Required meaning |
|---|---|
| bindings | exact Primitive versions and their local jobs |
| ordering | when one Primitive must precede, condition, or suppress another |
| compatibility | why the selected Primitives can cooperate in this source, audience, format, and relationship state |
| conflicts | which otherwise useful moves would undermine the coalition |
| Coalition Signature | the compact structural identity of the coalition |
| Edge Product | the specific pressure-and-position product that survives the coalition |
| misuse risks | predictable ways the coalition could become manipulative, generic, contradictory, sentimental, over-resolved, or visually flattened |
| evaluation | non-compensable Primitive checks and expected receipts |

A list of Primitive IDs is not a coalition. The coalition becomes real only when the bindings, functions, order, conflicts, signature, edge, and evaluation contract are explicit.

Steering Recipes are evidence-backed operationalizations of successful coalitions. They do not own the Primitive definitions, the source truth, the archetype, or the Final Script. A Recipe remains scoped to the applicability envelope proved by its episodes and evaluations.

## 39. Coalition Signature and Edge Product

The Coalition Signature records the recurring structure of a successful coalition without reducing it to surface wording. It may include the dominant pressure path, recognition move, tension-release pattern, role transition, participation threshold, visual attention logic, and experiential progression.

The Edge Product is the specific content pressure produced after Identity DNA, Context Premise, Matrix of Edging, evidence, counteractivation, and Primitive compatibility are reconciled. It is not a controversial sentence. It is the surviving relationship between truth, pressure, role, stance, and consequence that makes an archetype useful.

Edge Products are versioned and evidence-bearing. A derivative may compress or re-express the Edge Product, but it may not replace it with a safer, smoother, or more familiar centroid merely because that alternative is easier to generate or more likely to receive average approval.

## 40. Archetype coalition and psychological-role geometry

An archetype is a category-native meaning and sequence geometry. Historical archetype prompts are evidence, not current authority. An archetype becomes eligible when it can embody the approved Edge Product and Primitive coalition while giving the audience the intended psychological role inside the tension.

Archetype selection therefore occurs after Primitive coalition compilation, not before. The Archetype Coalition Program declares:

- the primary archetype and any supporting archetypes;
- the psychological role and tension geometry;
- the sequence or static reading logic;
- compatibility with the current Primitive bindings;
- source spans, Expression Moments, and evidence available to fill the archetype;
- Guest Voice DNA and Visual DNA requirements;
- category-specific composition obligations;
- anti-centroid constraints and wrong-reading locks;
- candidate alternatives and rejection reasons.

A familiar hook, listicle, reaction, myth, case study, or story template does not become valid merely because a historical prompt exists. The coalition must prove why the archetype carries the present source and audience relationship.

## 41. Brand Genesis, Brand Context Version, Voice DNA, and Visual DNA

Brand context is versioned source material, not a style adjective. The Runtime resolves the active Brand Genesis Session and Brand Context Version before script or visual-world compilation.

Guest Voice DNA governs the way derivative language preserves the guest’s credible vocabulary, rhythm, sentence pressure, stance, specificity, metaphor range, and emotional distance. It permits verbatim quotation, condensation, adaptation, and authored rewriting, provided each transformation remains traceable and does not claim invented wording as a verbatim source quote.

Visual DNA governs the recurring visual world: real-life references, subject treatment, visual temperature, materiality, composition tendencies, edge behavior, negative space, typographic posture, annotation logic, motion character, and prohibited centroid defaults. Visual DNA does not replace category-native composition; it constrains and enriches it.

The Brand Context Version, Voice DNA, and Visual DNA used in one Final Script or composition are immutable references. Later refinements create successor versions and invalidate only dependent descendants.

## 42. RSCS Distillation Layers

RSCS is required to preserve signal while reducing accidental sameness. The five Distillation Layers are:

1. **Saturation:** generate and gather enough source-backed signal, candidate language, imagery, Primitive moves, and archetype possibilities to avoid premature convergence.
2. **Collision:** place meaningful signals in contact so contradiction, tension, resonance, and non-obvious relationships become visible.
3. **Compression:** remove explanatory excess while preserving the Edge Product, psychological role, tension, and source fidelity.
4. **Evaluation:** compare candidates against Primitive contracts, CBAR tensions, Voice DNA, Visual DNA, source evidence, counteractivation, and category function.
5. **Recursion:** use the evaluated result to refine the previous layer when the signal collapsed, became generic, or lost its role inside the tension.

Distillation is not summarization. Compression that removes the reason an expression matters is failure. Expansion that buries the tension in explanation is also failure.

## 43. CCV, SDA, and SFL

CCV governs controlled variation. Variants must change declared dimensions while preserving the source, coalition, Edge Product, archetype function, and required composition laws. Random wording or random seeds are not sufficient strategic diversity.

SDA governs representation geometry: how the source and coalition become a coherent species of composition rather than a pile of decorative elements. SFL governs the functional language of the surface: what each visible or temporal element does for recognition, role, pressure, release, proof, transition, and participation.

The Runtime uses the exact supplied SDA and SFL registry snapshots as historical and implementation evidence. Current production profiles require explicit ratification and cannot be inferred from filename similarity.

## 44. Guest Voice DNA Final Script authority

Every content-bearing derivative requires an approved archetype-coalition Final Script before composition.

The Final Script Package includes:

- the exact source package and source spans;
- the approved Expression Moments and evidence;
- the viewer psychological role and tension;
- Primitive Coalition Contract, Coalition Signature, and Edge Product;
- archetype coalition and category target;
- Guest Voice DNA version and rewrite lineage;
- verbatim, condensed, adapted, and newly authored text labels;
- Visual DNA and real-life reference requirements;
- RSCS layer receipts;
- CCV variation permissions;
- wrong-reading and anti-centroid locks;
- operator approval and supersession state.

Composition may not begin from loose quotes, a generic brief, or a model-generated outline when the Final Script gate applies. Editing may not become the place where the semantic sequence is invented accidentally.

## 45. Composition-Before-Editing doctrine

Composition determines how approved meaning is distributed through space, time, hierarchy, relationship, negative space, source media, and visual interruption. Editing executes and refines that composition.

The order is:

```text
approved Final Script
→ category-native composition program
→ asset and source bindings
→ timeline or static layout
→ editing operations
→ render
→ reparse and evaluation
→ selective repair
```

For source-led short video, the original interview footage and audio remain the A-roll spine. Overlays, evidence, B-roll, animation scenes, captions, and visual interruptions support the source expression. They do not silently replace it while the output is represented as source-led.

Negative Space is functional structure. Edge Integrity / Anti-Centroid doctrine rejects default centering, equalized hierarchy, symmetrical smoothing, and generic polish when those choices erase tension, gaze, separation, or the source’s felt truth.

## 46. Visual Research and Real-Life Reference Before Generation

Generation begins only after the composition identifies what is missing and what real-life reference is required. Visual research gathers source keyframes, environments, objects, gestures, materials, cultural references, proof surfaces, and real-world composition precedents before a generative provider is asked to invent.

This law protects source specificity. It prevents the model from filling unresolved visual questions with statistically familiar imagery that weakens the Edge Product or pulls the work toward a generic visual centroid.

## 47. Mandatory reusable 2D Animation Scene Package

Every eligible Final Script compiles a reusable 2D Animation Scene Package, even when the current derivative does not render a complete animation short.

The package may contain character or avatar identity references, pose and expression intents, keyframes, source audio or Voice DNA script, scene beats, props, environments, diagrams, quote visualizations, concept demonstrations, B-roll opportunities, transparency requirements, and runtime bindings.

The package is composition-ready by default. Full rendering occurs when the active archetype, template, scene contract, or downstream composition requires it. Individual scenes may be reused as B-roll in short videos, embedded in Carousel slides, used in SuperVisual components, or assembled into a separately authorized animation derivative.

This law does not activate Format 02. Format 02 remains deferred until its current Atomic Harness is built and independently validated. The reusable scene package is a broader source-expression production obligation.

## 48. Primitive and Activative evaluation

Evaluation is layered and non-compensable.

A Primitive Evaluation Receipt verifies that each binding performed its declared function, avoided its misuse modes, respected conflicts and suppression, and contributed to the Coalition Signature and Edge Product.

Activative evaluation verifies the psychological role, tension, recognition path, participation threshold, counteractivation, freshness, and Activation Transfer Fidelity.

Script evaluation verifies source lineage, Voice DNA, archetype geometry, RSCS compression, and anti-centroid integrity.

Composition evaluation verifies hierarchy, negative space, gaze, edge relationships, source media role, visual reference fidelity, temporal function, and wrong-reading locks.

A high aesthetic or engagement score cannot compensate for a failed source, Primitive, role, tension, transfer, or composition gate.

## 49. V2.1 product sovereignty

The Activative Intelligence Runtime owns the lifecycle and semantic programs defined here. It does not conduct the live interview by itself, compile Atomic Harness structures, execute renderers, generate visual assets, or become the Studio’s state store.

Interview Expression owns live source activation and canonical source resolution. The Builder declares exact semantic dependencies. The Pipeline executes approved derivative programs. The VAE realizes typed visual demands. Delegation transports cross-product objects. The Studio shows state and captures attributable human resolutions. Program Control owns cross-product ratification, current status, and release claims.
