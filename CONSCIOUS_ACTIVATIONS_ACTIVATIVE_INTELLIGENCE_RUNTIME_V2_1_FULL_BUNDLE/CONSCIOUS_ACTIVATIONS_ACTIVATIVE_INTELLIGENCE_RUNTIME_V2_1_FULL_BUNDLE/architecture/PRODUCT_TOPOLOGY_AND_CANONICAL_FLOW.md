## Sovereignty rule

Every arrow is a typed handoff. The consumer may validate and reject the producer object; it may not silently reinterpret or mutate it. Semantic repair returns to the owning Activative or Interview product. Production repair remains inside the Pipeline or VAE when the upstream semantic program remains valid.
