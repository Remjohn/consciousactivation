# Studio V2.2 — Activative Lifecycle Projection and Human Resolution Amendment

The Studio shall project, compare, and resolve the Activative lifecycle without becoming the semantic state store.

Required surfaces include:

- source package, admission mode, tags, reaction, Expression Moment, and source-time lineage inspection;
- Activation Hypothesis Portfolio comparison;
- Primitive bindings, coalition signature, Edge Product, misuse risks, and evaluation receipts;
- psychological role, tension, archetype coalition, Brand Context, Voice DNA, Visual DNA, and RSCS layer inspection;
- Final Script candidate comparison and operator approval;
- campaign freshness, role diversity, counteractivation, and audience-observation views;
- reusable animation-scene package browser and downstream usage map;
- failure attribution, invalidation graph, repair plan, and replay;
- Programmed Model claim, harness, benchmark, fallback, and promotion views.

Every approval, rejection, comparison, revision, direct manipulation, tool override, taste explanation, and publication decision emits a HumanResolutionEpisode. Natural-language and direct-manipulation commands compile through the same typed change architecture.
