# Integrated Activative Intelligence V2 — Product Requirements
**PRD ID:** CCP-AI-V2

**Status:** Reference product-authority candidate

## Product claim

The product compiles, operates, observes, and learns from source, relationship, and audience activation without collapsing planned intent into observed truth or structural contracts into semantic evidence.

## Primary user outcomes

- Operators can turn public interactions into source-activation opportunities.
- Interviewers receive adaptive, state-aware Interview Asset Contracts.
- Source packages preserve exact human expression and planned–observed deltas.
- Composers receive transfer contracts rather than generic prompts.
- Campaigns manage role diversity, freshness, and fatigue.
- Human corrections become scoped programming material.
- Failures are attributed and repaired locally.

## Product boundaries

- Builder compiles governed harness structures.
- Activative Intelligence Runtime owns semantic activation programs.
- Interview Expression owns live source activation and source resolution.
- Format harnesses own category execution.
- VAE and renderers own production.
- Evaluators score independently.
- Program Control owns release and claim authority.

## Non-functional requirements


- All canonical objects use stable serialization and immutable version identities.
- Every lifecycle transition is receipt-bearing.
- JIT contexts remain minimum-complete and role-bounded.
- Production generators never receive authority to invent upstream source meaning.
- Replay, cancellation, supersession, and selective invalidation are deterministic.
- Evaluation contracts expose non-compensable gates.
- Human-resolution and learning objects remain versioned and reversible.
- No production or human-reaction claim is promoted from synthetic evidence.
