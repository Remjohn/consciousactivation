### <a id="_hhsffiepqk4y"></a>__✨ The Nostalgia Story \(Interview Framework\)__

__Storage Table__: archetype\_prompts\_library __Prompt ID__: the\_nostalgia\_story\_interview __Purpose__: To generate a completely customized interview guide\. This engine synthesizes research to create unique questioning sequences designed to extract a coach's most powerful story about the emotional journey of reconnecting with a cherished past\.

__PRIMING & REFERENCE LIBRARY__ Warm memories, golden haze, childhood freedom, safe spaces, lost innocence, time capsule, analog warmth, simpler days, shared experiences, emotional echoes, sun\-faded photographs, bittersweet, reminiscent, throwback, vintage, retro, the ache of memory, looking back\.

__RESEARCH SYNTHESIS PROTOCOL__ You will analyze the variables to create completely customized interview questions:

- __Content Idea Analysis__: Break down the specific type of nostalgia in \{content\_idea\} \(e\.g\., a time period, a specific memory, a lost feeling\)\.
- __Audience Psychology Mapping__: Use \{context\_premise\} to understand the audience's emotional landscape regarding their own past, memory, and the feeling of things being "lost\."
- __Reference Point Mining__: Extract 2\-3 compelling examples from \{deep\_research\} of powerful stories about memory and the past\.
- __Current Relevance__: Find timely angles from \{fresh\_research\} that make this reflection on the past feel urgent and relevant today\.

__VARIABLES & DYNAMIC ELEMENTS__

\{content\_idea\}: The specific nostalgia theme to explore

\{context\_premise\}: The audience's psychological profile \(fears, dreams, frustrations\)

\{deep\_research\}: Strategic intelligence and compelling examples

\{fresh\_research\}: Current trends and urgent context

\{coach\_background\}: Coach's expertise and journey

\{stream\_of\_consciousness\}: Your authentic voice and perspective

__DYNAMIC QUESTION GENERATION INSTRUCTIONS__

### <a id="_gv0irc8w292b"></a>__STEP 1: CONTENT IDEA ANALYSIS__

First, analyze \{content\_idea\} to identify:

- __The Core Nostalgia Type__: What is being remembered? \(A place, a person, a feeling, an object, an era\)\.
- __The Emotional Stakes__: What does this memory represent? \(Simplicity, safety, lost potential, joy\)\.
- __The Common Misconceptions__: What do people get wrong about idealizing the past?
- __The Central Tension__: What is the primary conflict between the memory and the present reality?

### <a id="_f6cdjxa0ppy6"></a>__STEP 2: CONTEXT PREMISE INTEGRATION__

Using \{context\_premise\}, identify:

- __Audience's Nostalgia Anxiety__: What specific fears do they have about the past being "gone forever"?
- __Their "Memory" Distortions__: What unrealistic expectations do they have about recapturing past feelings?
- __Their "Present" Barriers__: What is preventing them from appreciating how the past has shaped their present?
- __Their Longing Vision__: What is the feeling from the past they are desperately trying to reconnect with?

### <a id="_euiko76exxao"></a>__STEP 3: RESEARCH\-POWERED REFERENCE POINTS__

From \{deep\_research\}, select 2\-3 examples that:

- Mirror the specific nostalgia theme in \{content\_idea\}\.
- Connect to the audience's psychological profile from \{context\_premise\}\.
- Provide surprising or counter\-intuitive insights about the function of memory\.

### <a id="_vycdmro4ujoc"></a>__STEP 4: GENERATE CUSTOM INTERVIEW SEQUENCE__

Now create a completely customized interview framework:

#### <a id="_542axk8m4wch"></a>__PHASE 1: DYNAMIC CONTEXT SETTING__

Create an opening that weaves together the theme, the audience's feelings, and a compelling reference\. __Template__: "I want to explore the powerful role of nostalgia around \[specific aspect of content\_idea\] with you\. Your audience often feels \[specific struggle from context\_premise\] when they look back on their past\. It's so relevant because \[current trend from fresh\_research\] is making everyone feel a deep sense of longing for simpler times\. I was just reading about \[specific example from deep\_research\], and it made me think\.\.\." Then generate 2\-3 __customized permission questions__ that feel natural and create a safe space for reminiscing\.

#### <a id="_i695wvq1i3jl"></a>__PHASE 2: THE MEMORY \- "GOLDEN HAZE" ARCHAEOLOGY__

Generate 4\-5 questions focused on the cherished memory itself:

- Questions that uncover the rich, sensory details of the past moment related to \{content\_idea\}\.
- Questions that reveal the deep emotional significance of why this memory is so powerful\.
- Questions that connect their cherished memory to the audience's own longings from \{context\_premise\}\. Include 1\-2 __pattern\-matching probes__ using your selected references from \{deep\_research\}\.

#### <a id="_bmtro2wsl6bu"></a>__PHASE 3: THE ATTEMPT \- "NOBLE FAILURE" ARCHAEOLOGY__

Create 5\-6 questions that:

- Explore a specific time they tried to "go back" or perfectly recapture that nostalgic feeling, which ultimately failed\.
- Mirror the audience's own "noble failure" patterns of trying to relive the past from \{context\_premise\}\.
- Reference why this attempt to recapture the past is so common now \(using \{fresh\_research\}\)\.
- Uncover the moment they realized that the past could not be perfectly replicated\.

#### <a id="_rk387qa30wfx"></a>__PHASE 4: THE REALIZATION \- "BITTERSWEET" ARCHAEOLOGY__

Design 4\-5 questions that:

- Seek to capture the sensory and emotional details of the moment they accepted the past is gone\.
- Challenge common misconceptions about nostalgia being purely positive or negative\.
- Connect to the audience's breakthrough barriers from \{context\_premise\}\.

#### <a id="_z9llphoylsx9"></a>__PHASE 5: THE NEW MEANING \- WISDOM ARCHAEOLOGY__

Create 4\-5 questions that:

- Explore the specific transformation in their understanding of how that memory serves them *today*\.
- Connect to what the audience truly desires from a healthy relationship with their past, based on \{context\_premise\}\.
- Uncover the unexpected emotional reality of finding peace with the passage of time\.

#### <a id="_avo6mz96kql8"></a>__PHASE 6: THE LESSON \- ACTIONABLE INSIGHTS__

Generate 3\-4 questions that:

- Distill the core lesson learned about how to honor the past without being trapped by it\.
- Address the audience's immediate next steps for finding the wisdom in their own memories\.
- Provide hope for their own journey to integrate their past and present\.

__OUTPUT REQUIREMENTS__

### <a id="_a7ay1yt6uibz"></a>__1\. CUSTOMIZED INTERVIEW GUIDE__

- __Content\-Specific Opening Frame__ \(unique to \{content\_idea\}\)
- __Dynamic Permission Questions__ \(2\-3 options tailored to the theme\)
- __Phase\-by\-Phase Question Sets__ \(completely customized to the content and audience\)
- __Strategic Reference Integration__ \(natural weaving of \{deep\_research\} examples\)

### <a id="_8634hlwnccj"></a>__2\. INTERVIEW INTELLIGENCE BRIEF__

- __Content Theme Analysis__: What makes this story of nostalgia unique?
- __Audience Psychology Map__: How does this connect to their \{context\_premise\}?
- __Research Power Points__: Key examples and current context to leverage\.
- __Emotional Excavation Strategy__: What specific feelings of warmth, longing, disappointment, and wisdom to uncover\.

### <a id="_c4js3auz8l1s"></a>__3\. SUCCESS INDICATORS__

- __Story Authenticity Markers__: What genuine emotion \(e\.g\., a wistful smile, a moment of quiet reflection, bittersweet gratitude\) should emerge?
- __Audience Relevance Checkpoints__: How will this resonate with \{context\_premise\}?
- __Content Differentiation__: What will make this story of nostalgia unique in the market?

__QUALITY BENCHMARKS__ ✅ __Content Specificity__: Questions are laser\-focused on the \{content\_idea\} theme\. ✅ __Audience Relevance__: Every question connects to \{context\_premise\} psychology\. ✅ __Research Integration__: Natural use of \{deep\_research\} and \{fresh\_research\}\. ✅ __Dynamic Variability__: No generic questions \- everything is customized\. ✅ __Emotional Excavation__: Questions designed to uncover deep, authentic feelings\. ✅ __Strategic Flow__: Progressive deepening from surface to core insights\.

__TONE EXTRACTION GUIDE__ Extract the interviewing tone and style from \{stream\_of\_consciousness\}:

- How do you naturally talk about the past and memories?
- What's your authentic curiosity style about reminiscence?
- How do you create an environment for discussing both the joy and the pain of looking back? Adapt all questions to match this authentic voice while maintaining strategic intent\.
