### <a id="_mihinzk7ahy"></a>__✨ The Connection Story \(Interview Framework\)__

__Storage Table__: archetype\_prompts\_library __Prompt ID__: the\_connection\_story\_interview __Purpose__: To generate a completely customized interview guide\. This engine synthesizes research to create unique questioning sequences designed to extract a coach's most powerful story about the emotional journey of finding their tribe and overcoming loneliness\.

__PRIMING & REFERENCE LIBRARY__ Bonds of friendship, the power of love, shared moments, finding your tribe, the strength of community, the warmth of belonging, overcoming loneliness, the joy of togetherness, the comfort of companionship, the magic of empathy, the beauty of relationships, the tapestry of human connection, the threads that bind us\.

__RESEARCH SYNTHESIS PROTOCOL__ You will analyze the variables to create completely customized interview questions:

- __Content Idea Analysis__: Break down the specific type of connection in \{content\_idea\} \(e\.g\., friendship, community, partnership\)\.
- __Audience Psychology Mapping__: Use \{context\_premise\} to understand the audience's emotional landscape regarding loneliness and belonging\.
- __Reference Point Mining__: Extract 2\-3 compelling examples from \{deep\_research\} of powerful stories about finding connection\.
- __Current Relevance__: Find timely angles from \{fresh\_research\} that make this story of connection feel urgent and relevant\.

__VARIABLES & DYNAMIC ELEMENTS__

\{content\_idea\}: The specific connection theme to explore

\{context\_premise\}: The audience's psychological profile \(fears, dreams, frustrations\)

\{deep\_research\}: Strategic intelligence and compelling examples

\{fresh\_research\}: Current trends and urgent context

\{coach\_background\}: Coach's expertise and journey

\{stream\_of\_consciousness\}: Your authentic voice and perspective

__DYNAMIC QUESTION GENERATION INSTRUCTIONS__

### <a id="_yuq3spq72175"></a>__STEP 1: CONTENT IDEA ANALYSIS__

First, analyze \{content\_idea\} to identify:

- __The Core Connection Type__: What is the nature of the bond? \(Friendship, mentorship, romantic, community\)\.
- __The Emotional Stakes__: What does this connection save the person from? What does it allow them to become?
- __The Common Misconceptions__: What do people get wrong about finding "their people"?
- __The Central Tension__: What is the primary obstacle to forming this connection? \(e\.g\., fear, past trauma, societal pressure\)\.

### <a id="_20qdp84hbxsz"></a>__STEP 2: CONTEXT PREMISE INTEGRATION__

Using \{context\_premise\}, identify:

- __Audience's Connection Anxiety__: What specific fears do they have about vulnerability or rejection?
- __Their Belonging Distortions__: What unhealthy beliefs do they hold about what it takes to fit in?
- __Their Connection Barriers__: What is preventing them from forming the deep bonds they desire?
- __Their Connection Vision__: What would their ideal "tribe" or relationship look and feel like?

### <a id="_5eupbk91i1he"></a>__STEP 3: RESEARCH\-POWERED REFERENCE POINTS__

From \{deep\_research\}, select 2\-3 examples that:

- Mirror the specific connection theme in \{content\_idea\}\.
- Connect to the audience's psychological profile from \{context\_premise\}\.
- Provide surprising or counter\-intuitive insights about the nature of human bonding\.

### <a id="_8c2wr588gned"></a>__STEP 4: GENERATE CUSTOM INTERVIEW SEQUENCE__

Now create a completely customized interview framework:

#### <a id="_g6qoxwudykmq"></a>__PHASE 1: DYNAMIC CONTEXT SETTING__

Create an opening that weaves together the theme, the audience's feelings, and a compelling reference\. __Template__: "I want to explore the idea of true connection around \[specific aspect of content\_idea\] with you\. Your audience often feels \[specific struggle from context\_premise\] in their search for belonging\. It's so relevant because \[current trend from fresh\_research\] shows how challenging this is today\. I was just reading about \[specific example from deep\_research\], and it made me think\.\.\." Then generate 2\-3 __customized permission questions__ that feel natural and create a safe space for discussing relationships\.

#### <a id="_p2rwz06ykaid"></a>__PHASE 2: ISOLATION ARCHAEOLOGY \- THE "BEFORE" STATE__

Generate 4\-5 questions focused on the feeling of disconnection before the key relationship formed:

- Questions that uncover their personal experience with the specific type of loneliness related to \{content\_idea\}\.
- Questions that reveal the deep emotional significance of feeling misunderstood or alone\.
- Questions that connect their past struggles to the audience's current fears from \{context\_premise\}\. Include 1\-2 __pattern\-matching probes__ using your selected references from \{deep\_research\}\.

#### <a id="_eupojoxk8mwn"></a>__PHASE 3: THE BRIDGE \- CATALYST ARCHAEOLOGY__

Create 5\-6 questions that:

- Explore the specific event, person, or realization that created the opportunity for connection\.
- Mirror the audience's desire for a "turning point" from \{context\_premise\}\.
- Reference why this type of catalyst is so relevant now \(using \{fresh\_research\}\)\.
- Uncover the specific moment they chose vulnerability over safety\.

#### <a id="_gxmpkkbpxdpq"></a>__PHASE 4: THE BOND \- CONNECTION ARCHAEOLOGY__

Design 4\-5 questions that:

- Seek to capture the sensory and emotional details of the moment a true bond was formed\.
- Challenge common misconceptions about what a "spark" of connection actually feels like\.
- Connect to the audience's breakthrough barriers from \{context\_premise\}\.

#### <a id="_wns2gp62eljn"></a>__PHASE 5: THE "AFTER" \- BELONGING ARCHAEOLOGY__

Create 4\-5 questions that:

- Explore the specific transformation the new connection created in their life\.
- Connect to what the audience truly desires from a sense of belonging, based on \{context\_premise\}\.
- Uncover the unexpected emotional reality of what it feels like to be truly seen and accepted\.

#### <a id="_5pq455vh3r9h"></a>__PHASE 6: WISDOM EXTRACTION \- ACTIONABLE INSIGHTS__

Generate 3\-4 questions that:

- Distill the core lesson learned about building, not just finding, connection\.
- Address the audience's immediate next steps for overcoming their own connection barriers\.
- Provide hope for their own journey to find their "tribe\."

__OUTPUT REQUIREMENTS__

### <a id="_wuhxe2fxtodx"></a>__1\. CUSTOMIZED INTERVIEW GUIDE__

- __Content\-Specific Opening Frame__ \(unique to \{content\_idea\}\)
- __Dynamic Permission Questions__ \(2\-3 options tailored to the theme\)
- __Phase\-by\-Phase Question Sets__ \(completely customized to the content and audience\)
- __Strategic Reference Integration__ \(natural weaving of \{deep\_research\} examples\)

### <a id="_grsfqj5exay7"></a>__2\. INTERVIEW INTELLIGENCE BRIEF__

- __Content Theme Analysis__: What makes this story of connection unique?
- __Audience Psychology Map__: How does this connect to their \{context\_premise\}?
- __Research Power Points__: Key examples and current context to leverage\.
- __Emotional Excavation Strategy__: What specific feelings of loneliness, vulnerability, and belonging to uncover\.

### <a id="_adwikyrxcc8h"></a>__3\. SUCCESS INDICATORS__

- __Story Authenticity Markers__: What genuine emotion \(e\.g\., warmth, relief, gratitude\) should emerge?
- __Audience Relevance Checkpoints__: How will this resonate with \{context\_premise\}?
- __Content Differentiation__: What will make this story of connection unique in the market?

__QUALITY BENCHMARKS__ ✅ __Content Specificity__: Questions are laser\-focused on the \{content\_idea\} theme\. ✅ __Audience Relevance__: Every question connects to \{context\_premise\} psychology\. ✅ __Research Integration__: Natural use of \{deep\_research\} and \{fresh\_research\}\. ✅ __Dynamic Variability__: No generic questions \- everything is customized\. ✅ __Emotional Excavation__: Questions designed to uncover deep, authentic feelings\. ✅ __Strategic Flow__: Progressive deepening from surface to core insights\.

__TONE EXTRACTION GUIDE__ Extract the interviewing tone and style from \{stream\_of\_consciousness\}:

- How do you naturally create intimacy and trust?
- What's your authentic curiosity style about relationships?
- How do you create emotional safety for vulnerability? Adapt all questions to match this authentic voice while maintaining strategic intent\.
