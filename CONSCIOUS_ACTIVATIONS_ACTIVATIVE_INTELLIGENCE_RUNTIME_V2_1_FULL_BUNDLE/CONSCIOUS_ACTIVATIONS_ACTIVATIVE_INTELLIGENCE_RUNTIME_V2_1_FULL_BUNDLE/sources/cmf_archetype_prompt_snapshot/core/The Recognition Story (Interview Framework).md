### <a id="_l859k8odt8r6"></a>__✨ The Recognition Story \(Interview Framework\)__

__Storage Table__: archetype\_prompts\_library __Prompt ID__: the\_recognition\_story\_interview __Purpose__: To generate a completely customized interview guide\. This engine synthesizes research to create unique questioning sequences designed to extract a coach's most powerful story about the emotional journey of finding authentic validation\.

__PRIMING & REFERENCE LIBRARY__ The spotlight shines, the moment of validation, the applause echoes, the achievement acknowledged, the hard work pays off, the feeling of pride, the sense of worth, the power of recognition, being seen, feeling heard, authentic validation, hollow victory, quiet confidence\.

__RESEARCH SYNTHESIS PROTOCOL__ You will analyze the variables to create completely customized interview questions:

- __Content Idea Analysis__: Break down the specific type of recognition in \{content\_idea\} \(e\.g\., professional, personal, creative\)\.
- __Audience Psychology Mapping__: Use \{context\_premise\} to understand the audience's emotional landscape regarding feeling unseen, the desire for validation, and the fear of being a fraud\.
- __Reference Point Mining__: Extract 2\-3 compelling examples from \{deep\_research\} of powerful stories about the difference between external applause and internal validation\.
- __Current Relevance__: Find timely angles from \{fresh\_research\} that make this story of recognition feel urgent and relevant in a world of vanity metrics\.

__VARIABLES & DYNAMIC ELEMENTS__

\{content\_idea\}: The specific recognition theme to explore

\{context\_premise\}: The audience's psychological profile \(fears, dreams, frustrations\)

\{deep\_research\}: Strategic intelligence and compelling examples

\{fresh\_research\}: Current trends and urgent context

\{coach\_background\}: Coach's expertise and journey

\{stream\_of\_consciousness\}: Your authentic voice and perspective

__DYNAMIC QUESTION GENERATION INSTRUCTIONS__

### <a id="_b7jvjhu3dgbb"></a>__STEP 1: CONTENT IDEA ANALYSIS__

First, analyze \{content\_idea\} to identify:

- __The Core Recognition Type__: What kind of validation was sought? \(Public praise, an award, a single person's respect\)\.
- __The Emotional Stakes__: What was the cost of being invisible? What did being truly seen make possible?
- __The Common Misconceptions__: What do people get wrong about what external validation will give them?
- __The Central Tension__: What was the primary conflict between the desire for applause and the need for authentic acknowledgment?

### <a id="_mha7svd0wnod"></a>__STEP 2: CONTEXT PREMISE INTEGRATION__

Using \{context\_premise\}, identify:

- __Audience's Recognition Anxiety__: What specific fears do they have about their work going unnoticed?
- __Their "Validation" Distortions__: What unhealthy beliefs do they hold about likes, followers, or awards equaling self\-worth?
- __Their Visibility Barriers__: What is preventing them from feeling seen and valued?
- __Their "True Seen" Vision__: What does the feeling of authentic recognition they are dreaming of look and feel like?

### <a id="_pfa2acmhcxz1"></a>__STEP 3: RESEARCH\-POWERED REFERENCE POINTS__

From \{deep\_research\}, select 2\-3 examples that:

- Mirror the specific recognition theme in \{content\_idea\}\.
- Connect to the audience's psychological profile from \{context\_premise\}\.
- Provide surprising or counter\-intuitive insights about the nature of validation\.

### <a id="_jswzb2gr1hvl"></a>__STEP 4: GENERATE CUSTOM INTERVIEW SEQUENCE__

Now create a completely customized interview framework:

#### <a id="_3xjk9p7k9tua"></a>__PHASE 1: DYNAMIC CONTEXT SETTING__

Create an opening that weaves together the theme, the audience's feelings, and a compelling reference\. __Template__: "I want to explore the complex idea of recognition around \[specific aspect of content\_idea\] with you\. Your audience often feels \[specific struggle from context\_premise\] when their hard work goes unnoticed\. It's so relevant because \[current trend from fresh\_research\] has turned validation into a numbers game\. I was just reading about \[specific example from deep\_research\], and it made me think\.\.\." Then generate 2\-3 __customized permission questions__ that feel natural and create a safe space for discussing the vulnerable need to be seen\.

#### <a id="_brnt1lita4fb"></a>__PHASE 2: THE "BEFORE" \- UNSEEN EFFORT ARCHAEOLOGY__

Generate 4\-5 questions focused on the state *before* any recognition was achieved:

- Questions that uncover their personal experience with the feeling of working "in the dark" in relation to \{content\_idea\}\.
- Questions that reveal the deep emotional cost of feeling invisible or unappreciated\.
- Questions that connect their "before" state to the audience's current fears from \{context\_premise\}\. Include 1\-2 __pattern\-matching probes__ using your selected references from \{deep\_research\}\.

#### <a id="_emvdazqkgoj5"></a>__PHASE 3: THE STRUGGLE \- "CHASING APPLAUSE" ARCHAEOLOGY__

Create 5\-6 questions that:

- Explore the specific, conventional ways they tried to get external validation \(chasing likes, awards, etc\.\), which ultimately felt hollow\.
- Mirror the audience's own "noble failure" patterns of seeking empty validation from \{context\_premise\}\.
- Reference why this form of validation is so prevalent now \(using \{fresh\_research\}\)\.
- Uncover the moment they realized that the applause wasn't fulfilling\.

#### <a id="_5kmeo3y72cea"></a>__PHASE 4: THE REVELATION \- "HOLLOW VICTORY" ARCHAEOLOGY__

Design 4\-5 questions that:

- Seek to capture the sensory and emotional details of the moment they received external validation and felt empty\.
- Challenge common misconceptions about what fame or public recognition actually feels like\.
- Connect to the audience's breakthrough barriers from \{context\_premise\}\.

#### <a id="_ontt89d3pngq"></a>__PHASE 5: THE "AFTER" \- AUTHENTIC VALIDATION ARCHAEOLOGY__

Create 4\-5 questions that:

- Explore the specific, often small or quiet, moment when they felt *truly* seen and recognized\.
- Connect to what the audience truly desires from a feeling of validation, based on \{context\_premise\}\.
- Uncover the unexpected emotional reality of what it feels like to be authentically acknowledged by the right person\.

#### <a id="_zcr0qq6x0z8e"></a>__PHASE 6: WISDOM EXTRACTION \- ACTIONABLE INSIGHTS__

Generate 3\-4 questions that:

- Distill the core lesson learned about the nature of true, sustainable recognition\.
- Address the audience's immediate next steps for finding authentic validation in their own lives\.
- Provide hope for their own journey to feel seen and valued\.
