### <a id="_85r8rkpplca"></a>__✨ The Joy Story \(Interview Framework\)__

__Storage Table__: archetype\_prompts\_library __Prompt ID__: the\_joy\_story\_interview __Purpose__: To generate a completely customized interview guide\. This engine synthesizes research to create unique questioning sequences designed to extract a coach's most powerful story about the emotional journey of discovering spontaneous, authentic joy\.

__PRIMING & REFERENCE LIBRARY__ Laughter echoes, smiles beam, hearts soar, spirits dance, pure delight, unadulterated happiness, the joy of living, the celebration of life, the infectious energy, the warmth of connection, the shared moments of bliss, the power of positivity, the light within, the joy of being, the happiness found\.

__RESEARCH SYNTHESIS PROTOCOL__ You will analyze the variables to create completely customized interview questions:

- __Content Idea Analysis__: Break down the specific type of joy in \{content\_idea\} \(e\.g\., simple pleasures, connection, creative flow\)\.
- __Audience Psychology Mapping__: Use \{context\_premise\} to understand the audience's emotional landscape regarding their own struggles with happiness and the pressure to be joyful\.
- __Reference Point Mining__: Extract 2\-3 compelling examples from \{deep\_research\} of powerful stories about finding joy in unexpected places\.
- __Current Relevance__: Find timely angles from \{fresh\_research\} that make this story of joy feel urgent and necessary in today's world\.

__VARIABLES & DYNAMIC ELEMENTS__

\{content\_idea\}: The specific joy theme to explore

\{context\_premise\}: The audience's psychological profile \(fears, dreams, frustrations\)

\{deep\_research\}: Strategic intelligence and compelling examples

\{fresh\_research\}: Current trends and urgent context

\{coach\_background\}: Coach's expertise and journey

\{stream\_of\_consciousness\}: Your authentic voice and perspective

__DYNAMIC QUESTION GENERATION INSTRUCTIONS__

### <a id="_lr78v3xydp11"></a>__STEP 1: CONTENT IDEA ANALYSIS__

First, analyze \{content\_idea\} to identify:

- __The Core Joy Type__: What was the source of the joy? \(A moment, a person, an activity, a realization\)\.
- __The Emotional Stakes__: What was the cost of the previous joyless state? What did this moment of joy make possible?
- __The Common Misconceptions__: What do people get wrong about how to find happiness?
- __The Central Tension__: What was the primary internal or external force that was blocking their joy?

### <a id="_scd62muxbu8h"></a>__STEP 2: CONTEXT PREMISE INTEGRATION__

Using \{context\_premise\}, identify:

- __Audience's Joy Anxiety__: What specific fears do they have about not being "happy enough" or the fleeting nature of joy?
- __Their "Happiness" Distortions__: What unhealthy beliefs do they hold about what they need to *do* or *have* to feel joy?
- __Their Joy Barriers__: What is preventing them from experiencing spontaneous delight in their own lives?
- __Their Joy Vision__: What does the feeling of authentic, unforced happiness they are dreaming of look and feel like?

### <a id="_9qjziugbvm9g"></a>__STEP 3: RESEARCH\-POWERED REFERENCE POINTS__

From \{deep\_research\}, select 2\-3 examples that:

- Mirror the specific joy theme in \{content\_idea\}\.
- Connect to the audience's psychological profile from \{context\_premise\}\.
- Provide surprising or counter\-intuitive insights about the nature of happiness\.

### <a id="_t5f665f5qrt6"></a>__STEP 4: GENERATE CUSTOM INTERVIEW SEQUENCE__

Now create a completely customized interview framework:

#### <a id="_olzbge4a99w"></a>__PHASE 1: DYNAMIC CONTEXT SETTING__

Create an opening that weaves together the theme, the audience's feelings, and a compelling reference\. __Template__: "I want to explore the nature of authentic joy around \[specific aspect of content\_idea\] with you\. Your audience often feels \[specific struggle from context\_premise\] and the pressure to be happy\. It's so relevant because \[current trend from fresh\_research\] talks about how we've forgotten how to find simple pleasures\. I was just reading about \[specific example from deep\_research\], and it made me think\.\.\." Then generate 2\-3 __customized permission questions__ that feel natural and create a safe space for discussing happiness without pressure\.

#### <a id="_7dm1jxr1p0u"></a>__PHASE 2: THE "BEFORE" \- JOYLESSNESS ARCHAEOLOGY__

Generate 4\-5 questions focused on the state *before* the moment of joy:

- Questions that uncover their personal experience with feeling a lack of joy in relation to \{content\_idea\}\.
- Questions that reveal the deep emotional cost of that joyless state \(e\.g\., numbness, going through the motions\)\.
- Questions that connect their "before" state to the audience's current fears from \{context\_premise\}\. Include 1\-2 __pattern\-matching probes__ using your selected references from \{deep\_research\}\.

#### <a id="_yoimxre6iuos"></a>__PHASE 3: THE STRUGGLE \- "FORCED FUN" ARCHAEOLOGY__

Create 5\-6 questions that:

- Explore the specific, conventional ways they tried to "manufacture" happiness, which ultimately failed\.
- Mirror the audience's own "noble failure" patterns of scheduling or forcing fun from \{context\_premise\}\.
- Reference why these conventional methods of seeking happiness are so prevalent now \(using \{fresh\_research\}\)\.
- Uncover the moment they realized that "trying" to be happy was the problem\.

#### <a id="_lvzn85cswjl9"></a>__PHASE 4: THE SPARK \- "SPONTANEOUS DELIGHT" ARCHAEOLOGY__

Design 4\-5 questions that:

- Seek to capture the sensory and emotional details of the unexpected moment joy appeared\.
- Challenge common misconceptions about joy needing to be big or loud\.
- Connect to the audience's breakthrough barriers from \{context\_premise\}\.

#### <a id="_mwd1alubh11e"></a>__PHASE 5: THE "AFTER" \- RESONANCE ARCHAEOLOGY__

Create 4\-5 questions that:

- Explore the specific, tangible ways their daily life and internal state are different now\.
- Connect to what the audience truly desires from a feeling of joy, based on \{context\_premise\}\.
- Uncover the unexpected emotional reality of what it feels like to carry that spark of joy forward\.

#### <a id="_qe4xpkulex2d"></a>__PHASE 6: WISDOM EXTRACTION \- ACTIONABLE INSIGHTS__

Generate 3\-4 questions that:

- Distill the core lesson learned about the nature of true, spontaneous joy\.
- Address the audience's immediate next steps for creating space for more joy in their own lives\.
- Provide hope for their own journey to find happiness\.

__OUTPUT REQUIREMENTS__

### <a id="_ay0p3at4kh3d"></a>__1\. CUSTOMIZED INTERVIEW GUIDE__

- __Content\-Specific Opening Frame__ \(unique to \{content\_idea\}\)
- __Dynamic Permission Questions__ \(2\-3 options tailored to the theme\)
- __Phase\-by\-Phase Question Sets__ \(completely customized to the content and audience\)
- __Strategic Reference Integration__ \(natural weaving of \{deep\_research\} examples\)

### <a id="_onqtyp9f4t7s"></a>__2\. INTERVIEW INTELLIGENCE BRIEF__

- __Content Theme Analysis__: What makes this story of joy unique?
- __Audience Psychology Map__: How does this connect to their \{context\_premise\}?
- __Research Power Points__: Key examples and current context to leverage\.
- __Emotional Excavation Strategy__: What specific feelings of emptiness, frustration, surprise, and delight to uncover\.

### <a id="_dvvxt9fr5itk"></a>__3\. SUCCESS INDICATORS__

- __Story Authenticity Markers__: What genuine emotion \(e\.g\., a light laugh, a warm smile, a sense of peace\) should emerge?
- __Audience Relevance Checkpoints__: How will this resonate with \{context\_premise\}?
- __Content Differentiation__: What will make this story of joy unique in the market?

__QUALITY BENCHMARKS__ ✅ __Content Specificity__: Questions are laser\-focused on the \{content\_idea\} theme\. ✅ __Audience Relevance__: Every question connects to \{context\_premise\} psychology\. ✅ __Research Integration__: Natural use of \{deep\_research\} and \{fresh\_research\}\. ✅ __Dynamic Variability__: No generic questions \- everything is customized\. ✅ __Emotional Excavation__: Questions designed to uncover deep, authentic feelings\. ✅ __Strategic Flow__: Progressive deepening from surface to core insights\.

__TONE EXTRACTION GUIDE__ Extract the interviewing tone and style from \{stream\_of\_consciousness\}:

- How do you naturally talk about happiness and simple pleasures?
- What's your authentic curiosity style about what truly brings people joy?
- How do you create an environment for discussing moments of authentic delight? Adapt all questions to match this authentic voice while maintaining strategic intent\.
